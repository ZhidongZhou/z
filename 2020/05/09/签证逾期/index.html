签证逾期

zdz
09-05-2020
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL
ID Original Translated
E1 Mr. Li, I understand that you have overstayed a 3-month tourist visa and you’ve been working illegally. Do you have anything to say about this? 李先生，我知道您已逾期3个月的旅游签证，并且一直在非法工作。您对此有话要说吗？
M1 我不知道谁给你的这些信息， 这些都不是真的。 I don’t know who gave you this information. It’s not true
E2 It is in your best interest to cooperate with us. We have evidence to support these claims. You’ve broken the law and it is very likely you will be deported. 与我们合作是对你自己最有利的选择。我们有证据支持这些说法。你违反了法律，很可能会被驱逐出境。
M2 不行， 你不可以把我送回到那里，我已经什么都没有了， 我必须呆在这， 我想在这个国家安家。 No, you can’t send me back, I have nothing left there, I have to stay here, I want to settle down in this country.
E3 The conditions were clearly explained to you when you were granted the tourist visa. You signed a document stating that you are fully aware of your obligations. 当你获得旅游签证时， 已经清楚地向你说明了这些条件。 你签署了一份文件，声明你完全了解自己的义务。
M3 我记得我签了一个什么文件。 但从那以后我已经忘了这件事和文件的内容了。 我以为那只是一个简单的仪式， 在我来到这里之后就不会再被约束了。 I remember I signed a document and since then I’ve forgotten about it, and the contents of the document. I thought it was just a simple formality, and after I came here I would not be restrained anymore.
E4 There seems to be some degree of ignorance on your part, deliberate or not, what explanation do you have for overstaying your tourist visa? 您似乎有某种程度的不知情，有意或无意，您对逾期持有旅游签证有何解释？
M4 我不是故意要违法的。 我现在认识到我已经逾期滞留了。 但我这样做是有正当理由的。 我有一个小女儿， 她天生得了兔唇。 我想在澳大利亚和她呆在一起, 这样我的女儿会得到比较好的治疗。 I did not intentionally violate (违反) the law. I now realize that I have been overstayed. But I have legitimate reasons for doing so. I have a little daughter who was born with rabbit lips (兔唇) / Harelip (/heə’lɪp/) . I want to stay with her in Australia. This way my daughter will get better treatment.
E5 I see. I can sympathise with your situation, but you must understand you went about solving your problem in the wrong way. 我明白了。我能理解你的处境，但你必须明白你是用了错误的方法去解决问题。
M5 但我不得不工作否则我没有办法在这里生存。 我也不会有钱给我女儿做手术。 求求你了， 就让我呆在这里吧。 But I have to work or I won’t be able to survive here and I won’t have the money to support my daughter’s surgery, I begging you, just let me stay here
E6 There’s little I can do. Your case may be given special consideration but you have to set out the relevant details on this form first and then wait for a decision to be made. 我无能为力。你的个案子可能会得到特别考虑，但你必须先在此表格上列出有关的详情，然后等待作出决定
M6 那我的家人和我的工作怎么办呢？ 他们都指着我呢！ What about my family and my job? They are all count on me ( 指望我 )!
