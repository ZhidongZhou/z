出手伤人

zdz
09-05-2020
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL
ID Original Translated
E1 Mr. Wang, you are not obliged to say or do anything unless you wish to do so, but whatever you say or do may be used against you in court. Do you understand? 王先生， 你不需要说或者做任何事， 除非你自愿， 但是你所说和所做的一切都有可能会成为呈堂证供。 你明白么？
M1 明白了。 我已经说过了， 我完全是处于自卫， 我怎么可能主动打人呢？ I understand. I already told you that I did it out of self-defence. Why would I assault someone? (How can I take the initiative to hit someone?)
E2 According to Mr Smith, you asked them to end their party and they refused. After which you assaulted his friend, causing grievous bodily harm (严重人体伤害; 重伤;). 根据史密斯先生的笔录， 你让他和他的朋友们们结束聚会， 但他们拒绝了。 然后你殴打了他的朋友， 造成严重人身伤害。
M2 Missing Missing
E3 According to Mr Smith, you started the fight. They claim they tried to explain, but you wouldn’t listen and were quite aggressive. 嗯， 根据史密斯先生说， 是你先动的手。 他们当时想和你解释， 但是你听不进去， 而且态度很恶劣。
M3 我承认我当时挺激动， 但是是因为我的宝宝在睡觉， 而且我绝对没有先动手。 他们那时候都喝醉了。 我敢打赌他们还抽了大麻。 I admit I was quite upset, but that’s because my baby was sleeping. I absolutely did not start the fight. They were all drunk. Bet they had even been taking Marijuana (大麻 /ˌmærɪ’hwɑːnə/)
E4 The police officer states that one of Mr Smith’s friends was scratched by the ring on your hand. The victim also claimed to have abdominal pain (腹痛) 根据警官的笔录， 史密斯先生的一位朋友被你的戒指划伤了。 而且受害人还声称腹部疼痛。
M4 那我呢？ 我也受伤了啊！ 你看， 这是我手臂上的淤青。 警官你可千万不要听信他的一面之词啊， 我是正当防卫。 What aobut me? I am injured too! Look at the bruises on my arm. Officer, don’t just listen to his side of the story. I was acting in self-defence.
E5 You should’ve told the police officer last night. You’ve been charged with common assault通袭击罪 resulting in grievous bodily harm严重身体伤害. You are scheduled to appear before Hurstville Local Court on the 2 of August, 2010. 嗯昨晚你应该向现场的警官反应这个情况。 你被控犯有普通袭击罪， 并造成严重身体伤害。 你将于2010年8月2日在Hurstville 赫斯特维尔 地方法院出庭。
M5 Missing Missing
E6 You can be released on bail otherwise you’ll be held in police custody until the trial. You may have your solicitor represent you in court. 你可以获得保释， 不然你会一直被警察收押， 直到开庭。 你可以让你的律师代表你上庭。
M6 好的， 我要打电话给我的律师， 让他们来帮我办理保释手续， 以后有什么事情直接找我律师谈。 Ok. I need to call my solicitor and ask him to bail me out / Apply for bail formalities. Please talk to him directly if anything else comes up.
