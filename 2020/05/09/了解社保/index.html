了解社保

zdz
09-05-2020
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL
ID Original Translated
E1 Good morning Mrs. Wang. Please have a seat. I understand that you are here to make an enquiry about government support. How can I help you? 早上好，王女士，请坐。我知道你来这里是想要咨 询一下政府补助，我能怎么帮你呢?
M1 您好，我对这里的社会保障制度 (Social Security System)不是很清楚。我是听我邻居说我可以在这里得到一些帮助的。 Hello, I don’t know much about the social security system here. My neighbour told me that I could get some help here.
E2 That’s right, Mrs. Wang. In Australia, the government provides support in many ways to make sure residents can get the help they need. Now, could you please tell me about your situation? 没错，王女士。在澳大利亚，政府提供多种补助来保证居民能得到需要得帮助。现在，能请你告诉我你的情况吗?
M2 我英语不好，不好找工作，所以我丈夫是家里的顶梁柱。他原先在一家家居用品店做销售。 It’s difficult for me to find a job because of my poor English, so my husband is the main income earner. He used to be a salesman in a home ware shop.
E3 You said he used to work there. What happened? 你说他以前在那里工作，（后来）发生了什么事呢?
M3 一年前，我丈夫和店长发生了一点冲突，他一冲动就辞职了。那之后，因为没有之前雇主的推荐，他很难找到理想的工作。 My husband had a conflict with the manager a year ago and quit in the heat of the moment. Since then, he has had difficulty finding an ideal job without a reference letter from his previous employer.
E4 What do you mean by an ideal job? Do you have specific requirements about the pay, position or other matters? 你指的理想的工作是什么意思呢？你对工资、职位或是其他事情有特定的要求吗?
M4 我丈夫身体不好，没法干重体力活。销售这类工作比较适合他，但是薪水很重要，毕竟要养活全家呀。 He is not physically fit so he can’t perform physical labour. Sales suits him well, but since he has a family to feed, the salary is a very important factor.
E5 Mrs. Wang, I understand your situation. However, we do encourage people to rely on their own means to get Reemployed. 王女士，我了解你的处境了。然而，我们鼓励人们通过自己的方法来重新就业。
M5 我丈夫也想尽快找到工作的，他现在偶尔做些零散工作，勉强养家。 My husband also wants to find a job AS soon AS possible. At the moment, he does some casual work occasionally; however the income is barely enough to support the family.
E6 Mrs. Wang, Centrelink does offer help to people who experience financial hardship. However, the decision will be made after we conduct an assessment. At the same time, we can also provide help to people who are looking for work. 王女士，社会福利联络中心 (Centrelink)给有财务困难的人提供帮助。但是要经过评估以后才能够做出决定。同时，我们也为那些找工作的人提供帮助。
M6 那太好了。下次我一定要让他来咨询一下如何找工的事情。 Great! Next time I will definitely ask him to enquire about how to find a job.
