题号 101003


题目:

Some people think human behaviors can be limited by laws, other think law have little effect. Waht is you opinion.

作答:

The controversial topic with respect to the relationship between law and human behaviors never failed to attract public attention. There has been a heated debate on whether the law can effectively limit human behaviors. As far as I am concerned, I tend to be an opponent regarding this issue. My explanation will be illustrated in the following part of this essay.

It is reasonable for me to believe that law is of great importance due to the fact that its law states how people may and may not behave in a society. It can be seen that without laws there will have no rules to follow when judging a criminal offense. Therefore, the law acts an essential role in regulating human behavior.

In, contrast, there are quite a few to hold the opinion that there is little that law can change in how human behaves. This argument does not hold water at all since people with no rule to obey can lead to chaos. For example, people can assault each other whenever they want without being charged. And there is a finding conducted by the researcher at the University of Melbourne has figured out that about 50% decrease in criminal offense after government conducted free popularization course on law.

In a nutshell, I may safely reach the conclusion that law is the essential rules that guiding people to behave in the right way based on the examples and results of the research.
