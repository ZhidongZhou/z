CCL

This is a dialogue between an immigration officer and Mr.Peng.

ID Original Translated
E1 Your friend told me briefly about your situation and I’m glad that he managed to persuade you to come and talk to us. 您的朋友向我简要介绍了您的处境，我很高兴他设法说服您来与我们交谈。
M1 你⼀定要帮我， 我现在头脑⼀⽚混乱，你不能把我遣返回国， 回国我⼜是⼀⽆所有。 You have to help me, I am totally confused, you can’t force me to leave this country, I have nothing back there if I go back.
E2 Don’t worry. From what I heard from your friend, you appear to fall into the GROUP of illegal immigrants who qualify for the special concessions (特殊条款) recently announced BY the Federal Government. Tell me, when did you come to Australia. 不要担⼼， 据你朋友所说， 你属于⾮法移⺠，你有资格获取联邦政府最新发布的特殊条款， 告 诉我你是什么时候来到澳洲的。
M2 我在2003年5⽉持学⽣签证来到这⾥， 在⼀所私⽴学院学了5周英语后， 我就在⼀家英语⼯⼚做全职⼯作。 I arriving Australia on May-2003, I was on a student visa, after I attends 5 week English class at private school, I go work as full time at an English factory.
E3 Won’t you aware you are not allowed to work full time on student visa? 你知不知道学生签证不允许你做全职工作?
M3 我知道的， 但是我需要钱来交付我的学费， 否则我就⽆法延续我的签证，是否真的要对⾮法移⺠进⾏⼤赦 呢？ Yes, I knew that, but I need some money to pay for my tuition fee, otherwise I could not extend my Visa. Is that True there will be amnesty for illegal immigrant.
E4 I wouldn’t see that there will be an amnesty, nevertheless, people in different circumstances may have different concessions. 我能承诺会有大赦，然而，根据个人人情况的不同将我们也许有会做出不同的让步。
M4 What kind of Concessions? Does it mean that I can apply to change my status and remain in Australia as a legal resident? 什么样的特殊待遇呢？这是否意味着我可以申请更改身份， 并以合法居民身份留在澳大利亚呢？
E5 No, there is no guarantee. Under these concessions, some illegal immigrants who have over-stayed will be given an opportunity to apply for permanent residency. 不，我没有办法保证。不过在这些特殊条款下，一些逾期居留的非法移民将有机会申请永久居留权。
M5 然⽽, 你们审批我申请的这段时间， 我是否会被遣返回中国? 我学历⼜不⾼, 找不到像样的 (decent) ⼯作的。 During the processing period, is it possible that I will be sent back to China? I don’t have a good education and won’t be able to find a decent job.
E6 No action will be taken for enforce your departure, you will be granted with bridging visa, during the time your application is being processed. 在你申请的审理期间，我们不会采取任何措施来强制您离开，你将会获得过渡签证。
M6 那我就放⼼了， 像我这样的⼈就是廉价劳动力 Cheap Labour， 到处被剥削被侮辱， 不过在这⾥被剥削相⽐中国要好很多。 Well, I’m relieved. Cheap labourers like myself are humiliated and exploited everywhere. But bing exploited here is better than being exploited in China.

重点:

拓展:

如果是被遣返Remove: 遣返 或 Deport: 驱逐出境, 限制十年不得入境； 十年限制期间不影响当事人以亲属或伴侣的身份入境。
