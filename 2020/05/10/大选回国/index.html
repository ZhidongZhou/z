大选回国

zdz
10-05-2020
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL
ID Original Translated
E1 Good Afternoon, NSW Electoral Commission, how can I help you? 下午好，这里是新南威尔士州选举委员会, 请问有什么可以帮助您的？
M1 午好， 我想请问一下: 这次的州大选我能不能提前投票? Good afternoon. May I ask if I can vote early for the upcoming State Election
E2 That’s possible, but you need to fulfil certain Criteria before we allow you to do this. Can I ask why you are considering voting early? 这是可行的， 但是你需要满足一定的条件我们才能允许你这么做， 我可以问问你想要提前投票的原因么？
M2 我的哥哥昨晚打电话给我， 说我的老母亲突然生病住院了， 我想尽快回去看她， 不过我不知道我会在中国待多久。 所以我想在离开澳洲前提前投票。 My brother called me last night to say that my mother has been suddenly hospitalized. I want to go back to see her as soon as possible, but I don’t know how long I will stay in China. So I want to vote early before leaving Asutralia.
E3 Okay, your situation would allow you to vote before election day via postal vote. But are you aware that there are other options available, such as internet voting or voting at overseas polling stations? 好的， 根据您的情况您可以通过邮寄的方式提前投票。 不过您是否知道您还有其他的选择， 比如说网上投票或者在海外投票站投票呢？
M3 那我到不太清楚。 能不能给我解释一下呢？ 我并不是一定要通过邮寄方式投票, 只是不想再收到选举委员会开的罚单。 Then I don’t know much about this. Can you explain it to me? I don’t have to vote by mail, I just don’t want to receive the fine from the Election Commission again.
E4 Certainly. Internet voting is pretty much self-explanatory. You need to go to the NSW Electoral Commission website, enter your details, answer a few simple questions, and then you’ll be granted permission to vote online. 当然， 网上投票是不需要加以说明的。 你需要登陆新南威尔士选举委员会的网站， 输入你的信息， 回答一些简单的问题， 然后你就便可以网上投票了。
M4 可是我的英语很差， 也不会用电脑上网。 您刚才说的另一种办法是什么呢？ But my English is poor and I don’t know how to use the internet. What’s the other option you just mentioned?
E5 The oter option is to vote at an overseas polling station. These are usually situated in the Australian embassy and consulates in major cities, and on election day all you have to do is visit one of them. 另一个选择是在海外投票站投票。 这些投票站通常设立在主要城市的澳大利亚领事馆和大使馆， 在选举日， 你所需要做的就是去拜访其中一处。
M5 听上去好像还不错。 那么今年在南京有没有这种海外投票站呢？ 我记得南京好像没有澳大利亚领事馆。 That sounds good. Is there a polling station in Nanjing this year? I don’t think there is an Australian consulate there.
E6 That’s true, but this year we are setting up a few overseas polling station in some of China’s major cities even if they don’t have pre-existing consulate. I can give you the address now. 是没有， 不过今年我们在中国的一些主要城市设置了海外投票站， 尽管这些城市预先没有存在的领事馆， 我可以现在把地址给你。
M6 多谢了， 我这就去拿纸和笔， 请你稍等一下。 Thanks a lot. I’ll get a pen and paper, please hold on a moment.
