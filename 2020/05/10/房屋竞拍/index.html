Mrs. Liu is consulting with a solicitor about buying a property

ID Original Translated
E1 Hello Miss Liu， Please Come in and have a seat. 你好，刘小姐，请进请坐。
M1 你好， 谢谢您抽时间⻅我，我今天来是想咨询⼀下在澳⼤利亚买房的事情 Hello, thank you to taking sometime to see me today, I am here today to seek you advise about consulting with solicitor about buying a property in Australia.
E2 Ok, have you decided on the type of property you are look after？ A house or a unit? 好的，您确定了要关注的房屋类型么？ 是独栋房屋还是单元房？
M2 是house, 是在南边的⼀个区， 前天和中介⼀起去看的房⼦， 整体还不错不错， 不过我不懂澳⼤利亚的过户⼿续。 Is house, at southern suburb, today before yesterday I went with my agent to inspect the house, overall is quite good, am very like it, but I don’t know Australia Conveyancing (过户;/kən’veɪənsɪŋ/) procedure.
E3 You don’t need to worry about this, I will be able to handle this for you. As your legal representative I need to know whether you are are financially prepared (钱准备好了么). 您无需担心，我将为您处理。作为您的法定代表人，我需要知道您的财务状况是否良好
M3 missing someting about financial status here… 我问过中介了， 他告诉我说， 拍卖会会在下周六举⾏， 将有3到4个⼈参加， 中介要我去参加拍卖会， 但是我并不知道拍卖会的流程。 I already asked with my agent, they told me there will be a auction next Saturday, and my agent ask me to go, but I am not familiar with the auction process.
E4 First, you should be eligible (有资格参与) to be admitted, during the auction, your final bid should be higher than the reserve price, and your bid must be the highest among all the bidders to get the house. 首先，您应该有有资格参与，在拍卖过程中，您的最终出价应高于底价，并且您的出价必须在所有要价的竞标者中最高。
M4 哦， 我明⽩了， 中介给我建议了⼀个价格， 是⼀百⼀⼗万澳元$110W 。 ⾸付是$30W 澳元， 我觉得 这个价格还可以承担。 Ok, understood, my agent suggested a price for me, which is 1.1M, and the First Payment (首付) is $300K, I think the price is affordable.
E5 I see, I suggest you to go online and research about this property, and get some detailed information about it. Such as: DA Approval, district planning, zoning, as well as feature planing which you neibour will planing for their property. 知道了，建议您上网研究该房产，并获取有关该房产的一些详细信息。例如：DA(Development Approval) 开发申请批准，区域规划，分区和功能规划，以及您的邻居将为其财产计划的功能规划。
M5 多亏你提醒我， 这些因素都和这个房产的价格密切相关，同时也在很⼤程度上对以后房屋拓建有影响。 Thank you for reminding me that these factors are closely related to the price of this property, and also have a great impact on future house expansion.
E6 Yes, one of my friends had to sold his property at low price, AS one of his neighbour added a second storey (阁楼；/‘stɔːrɪ/) which blocked the sunlight and affected his privacy. 是的，我的一个朋友不得不低价卖掉他的房子，因为他的一个邻居加盖了一层阁楼，挡住了阳光，影响了他的隐私。
M6 看来对这个地区做⼀个深⼊调查是有必要的， 如果竞标成功, 我会再来请你帮忙。 It Appears that thorough research is required on this area, I will come back for your help if I win the auction.
