建筑工伤

zdz
17-05-2020
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL
ID Original Translated
E1 Good Morning, Mr. Zhang. According to our records, you’ve booked an appointment today to talk about workers compensation Am I right? 早上好，张先生， 根据我们的记录，您之前预订过一个会面，谈论关于职工补偿的问题，对吗？
M1 是的， 我之前来过一次， 但是由于家里有急事， 就急忙赶回去了。 Yes, I came once before but had a rush home due to a family emergency.
E2 I see. I need to know as many details about the accident as possible. Could you start by telling me what you do for a living and how you got injured? 我知道了， 我需要知道这个意外的细节
M2 我是一个建筑工人， 非常熟悉操作规程(Protocols) 和安全规则， 至今没有出过一次事故。 可是两个月前， 我在搬运铁板的时候弄伤了胳膊。 I’m a construction worker. I’m very familiar with operation and safety protocols and I had never got caught in an accident until two months ago when my arm got injured while I was moving iron planels.
E3 Is the injury to your arm severe ? What treatment have you received so far? Also, what did your boss do after the accident? 手臂受伤严重吗？到目前为止，您接受了什么治疗？另外，事故发生后您的老板做了什么？
M3 老板把我送进医院， 他不让我说是怎么受的伤， 并且他保证会照顾我。 我怕不照他说的做我会工作不保， 所以说在打篮球的时候受伤的。 My boss took me to the hospital. He told me not to say a word about how I got injured and promised he would take care of me. Fearing I’d lose my job if I didn’t do as he said I told the doctor that I injured myself while playing basketball.
E4 It is apparent that your employer was worried that he might get into trouble because of your injury. So did your employer actually keep his promise to take care of you? 显然，您的雇主担心他可能因您的受伤而惹上麻烦。那么，您的雇主是否真的信守了照顾您的诺言？
M4 他根本没有。 他根本不给我付医药费， 也没有给我其他形式的金钱补助。 所以我今天才来寻求您的意见， 看看我能不能获得些工伤赔偿来维持生活。 Not at all. He didn’t pay any of my medical bills or offer other forms of financial assistance. That’s whay I’m here today hoping to get your advice on claiming workers’ compenstation to ensure my livelihood.
E5 Well, proving your case can be quit challanging, because your medical records all indicate a sporting accident as opposed to a workplace injury. We need to find witnesses, such as your co-workers who were at the scene at the time of the accident. 好吧，证明您的情况可能很难，因为您的病历都表明是运动事故，而不是工作场所受伤。我们需要找到证人，例如在事故发生时在现场的同事。
M5 证人的话我到是可以找得到。 当时在场有不少工友。 这之后会如何能？ I should be able to find some (很多) witnesses. Many co-workers were there at the time. So what happens after that?
E6 well, after collecting any relevant evidence, what you need to do is to lodge an application with the Workers Compensation Commission. 在收集到所有相关证据之后， 您需要做的是向 *工人赔偿委员会* 提出申请。
M6 希望希望最后我能报销我所有的医药费， 获得美洲的工伤赔偿及以及残疾福利。 Hopefuly, I will be reimbursed (报销) for all my medical expenses and be granted weekly Workers Compensation, Disability Support Pension.
