九元时薪

zdz
2020-05-17
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL
CCL Original Translated
A1 Mr. Wang, I understan that you have lodged a complaint with us at Fair Work Australia and that you wish to speak with an advisor here. Can you tell me that exactly you’d like to work out with your former employer? 王先生，我想知道您已在澳大利亚公平公正委员会向我们投诉，并希望在这里与顾问交谈。您能告诉我，您确认想与前雇主算账么？
Z1 我打从去年五月就在这家咖啡厅工作， 老板是华人， 处处欺负我， 少算我工资不提， 竟然无缘无故在我最需要收入的时候把我解雇了！ I started working at this café in May last year. The owner is Chinese, but bullied me in every possible way. Besides miscalculation of my wages, he fired me without any explanation when I really needed the income.
A2 It sounds like there are a few issues other than unfair dismissal. Firstly, can you tell me how exactly your employer miscalculated your wages? 听起来，除了无故解雇你之外，还有其他一些问题。 首先，您能告诉我您的雇主究竟是如何误算了您的工资吗？
Z2 好的。 我的是心只不过是九元， 可是他竟然还不算我观点前清理厨房的半小时， 说什么那段时间咖啡厅没有客人， 没有生意也就没有理由负我额外的钱 OK. My hourly rate was only 9 dollars. However, he didn’t count the half an hour of cleaning the kitchen before closing, saying that since there are no customers in the café during the period, no business means no reason to pay me.
A3 What he did was actually illegal. Nine dollars an hour is well under the minimum wage. He’s obviously evaded income tax and short-changed you by expecting you to work extra hours which were not paid. 他所做的实际上是非法的。每小时九美元远低于最低工资。他显然是在逃避所得税，并通过期望您加班而没有支付工资，从而使您变短了。
Z3 是啊， 但我初来乍到对这些权益都不了解， 加上看在他给我第一份工， 就这么忍受了他的不公待遇一年多了。 可是上周他突然叫我不用去上班了， 我一下子没了收入。 Yes, but I put up with his unfair treatment because I didn’t understand there rights when I first arrived here and he gave me my first job. However, last weekend he just suddenly asked me not to work anymore.
A4 Did you see any reason why he could possibly consider terminating your employment? You have worked for him for over 14 months so he must have been satisfied with you performance in general. 您是否看到他为什么可以考虑终止您的工作的任何原因？您已经为他工作了14个月以上， 所以他一定满意你一直以来的的表现。
Z4 是的， 我工作勤奋又有效率， 我想很可能是因为他的外甥放暑假， 在找临时工， 他为了省钱才把我解雇。 Yes, I’m a diligent and efficient worker. I think he fired me to save money because his nephew is on summer vacation and is looking for a temporary job.
A5 We will certainly look into this. You mentioned he bullied you at work. Can you explain this in further detail? 我们一定会对此进行调查。你提到他在工作中欺负你。您可以进一步详细解释吗？
Z5 没错， 他总是挑剔我的腔调， 说我和杨客人的对话听起来很滑稽， 笑我笨并常常骂我 That’s right. He always picked on my accent and said that I sounded funny when talking with western customers. He mocked me, called me stupid and often called me names.
A6 That does constitute bullying his repeted verbal taunts toward you were unreasonable. I believe we now have a clearer picture of your situation, I’ll make some notes in the complaint you’ve made and we shall get back to you this week. 这确实构成了欺凌, 他重复的口头嘲讽对你是不合理的。我相信我们现在可以更清楚地了解您的情况，我会在您的投诉中记下一些记号，我们将在本周与您联系。
Z6 那太好了， 我会等你们给我电邮答复， 谢谢你了。 That’s great, I’ll wait for your reply. Thank you.
