腰疼开药

CCL Original Translated
A1 Good morning. How are you today? 早上好今天感觉如何？
Z1 我今天感觉不错， 不过大夫。 上次我腰疼你开给我的大哥消炎药处方用完了， 能不能再给我开一张？ I am good today. But doctor, I’ve used up the anti-inflammatory medicine you prescribed to me for my lower back pains. Could you please give me another prescription?
A2 Hmm. That prescription should have lasted you three month. Do you mean to say that you’ve used it all? 嗯， 这张处方药应够你吃三个月的， 你是说药全吃完了么？
Z2 是的， 那个药不太猛， 有时我一天吃三四片晚上才不会疼的睡不着。 但是吃了这高药之后胃里就老泛酸。 Yes. That medicine is not that effective and sometimes I have to take three or four pills so I won’t be kept awake by the pain at night. But I was often belching a lot after taking it. (Got acid Reflux 反酸)
A3 That’s one of the common side effects of that mediction. I would recommend for you not to take it more than once a day from now on, because an excessive dosage (用量) can damge the kidneys. 这是这种药的正常副作用之一。 我建议你现在开始一天只吃一次药， 因为过量服药会损害肾脏。
Z3 那可不行。 我这个月全靠(Rely on it/count on it)它了， 老骨头痛起(The Pain of my old Bone)来什么可真不是开玩笑的。 根本睡不着觉， 什么都不能做。 No way. I have to count on it this month. I’m not kidding about how painful it is when it aches. I can’t sleep and can’t perform daily activities.
A4 when you came last time, you side you injured your back when you tried to hold grandson. Any soft tissue injuries you might have sustained whould have improved by now. 上个月你来的时候， 你说你抱孙子的时候伤了腰。 你造成的软组织尊上现在应该改善了
Z4 刚开始的时候到不怎么痛， 不过现在连咳嗽、 打喷嚏也会痛， 更别 (Let alone / Not to mention) 说弯腰(bending over)了。 大夫， 我这是怎么了？ It wasn’t that bad at the beginning but now it hurts every time I cough or sneeze, let alone bending over. What’s happend to me doctor?
A5 the technical name of your condition is lumbago (腰痛病), it can be caused by lots of differen things. I’m going to examine you now and book in some tests to determine the cause of your pain. 你的病症的学名叫腰痛， 也就是说背的下部疼。 很多情况都会引起你腰疼。 我现在要给你做个检查， 还要让你做几项化验， 来弄清引你腰疼痛的原因。
Z5 好的， 不过我还是想要一张消炎药的厨房 I see but I would still like to have an anti-inflammatory prescription.
A6 I will give you another prescritpion, but I will only prescribe one-month’s supply, because when the test results come out we may need to switch you over to a more appropriate analgesic (止痛剂) 我会再给你开张消炎药的厨房， 但这次我只会开一个月的量， 因为等化验结果出来， 我们可能要换一个更合适的止痛药。
Z6 好的， 医生， 谢谢， 我会按你说的做的， 结果出来后， 我再来。 Ok Doctor, Thank you. I will follow your instructions and will make an appointment with you once the results come out.

(OTC: over the counter drug)

酒驾争端

* Original Translated
A1 Ah here is the interpreter. Now we’ll be able to understand each other. 翻译员来了， 现在我们可以明白彼此在说什么了。
Z1 太好了， 你总与来了！ 我要让他们给我解释为什么要这么对待我。他们有什么权利拘捕我？ 我有做错什么么？ Greate, you are finally here! I want them to explain why they’ve treated me this way. What right do they have to arrest me? Have I done someting wrong?
A2 You have been brought here to the police station becase you refused to take a breath test, and we have reason to believe you were driving under the influence of alcohol 你被带到警局是因为你拒绝接受呼吸测试， 而且我们有理由相信你刚才的驾驶收到了酒精的影响。
Z2 放屁！ 我没喝醉。 我只是在酒吧和朋友喝了几杯， 根本没喝多。 我完全清醒， 也可以开车。 有个小伙子拦下我， 说我喝醉了， 让我朝那个什么。。。玩意吹气。 我可不愿意 Bullshit! I was not drunk. I just drank a bit with my friends at the pub and it was not much at all. I’m completely sober (冷静) and able to drive. Then a young man stopped me, saying I was drunk and asked me to blow the … thing. I didn’t want to do that.
A3 That was police constable (警员——低级别) Johnson, who saw you driving down Parramatta Rd in a dangerous manner, when he stopped you, you are abusive and refused to do the breath test. That means you are have already committed an offence, even you are under the limit. 那是我们的警员约翰逊， 他说你以一种危险的方式(driving down)沿着’帕拉马塔’路驾驶， 当他拦住你时， ( Abusive )你态度恶劣并拒绝进行呼气测醉， 这意味着即使你没有超过酒精标准， 你还是犯了罪
A3 违法？ 什么叫违法？ 从什么时候开始和朋友看球赛转播也算是违法了？ 你们都是些种族主义者。 你们专挑无辜的人， 就是因为我们不会说英文。 Offence? What offence? Since when has watcing sports with your friends been regarded as an offence? You are all racists. You always pick on innocent people just because we don’t speak English.
A4 You’re not doing yourself any favours carrying on like that. I suggest you calm down and take the breath test. Otherwise it will be assumed that you are over the prescribed limit and you will be charged with PCA. 你像现在这样做下去对你没有任何的好处， 我建议你冷静下拉并进行呼吸测醉。 否则你会被假定醉酒超标， 并将受到PCA指控
Z4 哼， 好吧。 这该死的国家他妈的根本就没有正义可言。 把这该死的东西给我， 我来吹口气。 快点结束拉倒。 Fine, okay. There is no justice in this damn / （Funcking） country. Give me this damn thing, and let me blow. I want an end to it.
A5 Hmm, it shows a reading of 0.15, which is well over the limit. We’ll be charging you what a high-range PCA offence. If you could have over your licence, sir? 嗯，它显示的读数为0.15，远远超出限制标准。我们将向您收取高额PCA违法行为。先生可以将您的驾照给我么？
Z5 我才不给你驾照呢， 如果没了驾照， 我怎么去工作啊？ 你知道， 我要养家的。 I’m not giving you my driver’s license. How am I supposed to work without it? You know, I have to feed my family!
A6 You should have thought of that before you got into your car under the influence of alcohol. You’ll be appearing before a magistrate at 10 a.m. tomorrow morning. 您喝醉后到车里之前就该考虑过这一点。您明天早上10点会出庭。
Z6 上法庭就上法庭， 想整我？ 没那么容易。 Fine by me! You want to give me a hard time? It won’t be that easy!
