理科天才

相·遇
27-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



The following is a dialogue between a teacher and the mother of a 19-year-old girl who is doing her Higher School Certificate.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good to see you again, Mrs. Tian. This time I have good news for you regarding your daughter’s results. She is doing extremely well.
24




M1
哦！我太高兴了。过去两年一直非常担心。对于她还有整个家庭，过去两年很困难。
Oh, I’m so happy. I’d been very worried over the past two years. It has been a difficult time for her and the whole family.




E2
Trust me, she is an excellent achiever. Her marks are in the top 40% and that really means something, considering her background.
22




M2
的确如此。她来到一个新的国家，说着不同的语言，还必须同时学习这里的语言和学校的功课，要是人们知道这有多困难就好了。
ndeed. She came to a new country where people speak a different language, and she has had to study the language and the school subjects at the same time. I wish people could understand how hard it is.




E3
I know, it must have been extremely difficult for all of you but Tracy is such a smart girl! Plus, she has been working very hard. All her teachers are so impressed by her incredible success.





M3
他对于数学和物理非常感兴趣，所以她这两门的成绩非常好。她因此还选了另外四门工科的课。哦，我真希望能够有个好成绩，因为她特别想学工程。
She is very interested in maths and physics, so she has got very good results in them. And that’s why she also chose four other science subjects. Oh, I hope she can do well because she is so eager to study engineering.




E4
Well, we’ll have to wait until she sits her exams before we can consider what she can do. I’m confident she’ll get into university, if not at her preferred institution, but at least in her preferred subject area.





M4
你为什么能这么肯定？你说过她在模拟考试中考得很好，能够考上大学，不过我觉得她跟当地孩子比起来，没有那么聪明。
Why are you so sure? You said she did great in the mock exam and was able to make her way to university. But as I understand it, she is not that smart, compared to local kids.




E5
I think what you are saying is not quite right because based on her current performance, she could easily be admitted for an arts degree. I understand that may not be what she wants currently, but if it is the only choice, she’ll have to take it, I’m afraid.





M5
但如果她学习不喜欢的东西，她是不会学好的。如果不是她感兴趣的专业，她是不会接受的。而且因为她语言背景的问题，文科比理科更难学。
If she studied something she doesn’t like, she wouldn’t do well at it. She wouldn’t take a major, if she wasn’t interested in it. Plus because of her language background, arts are more difficult than science.




E6
Yes, that’s the irony of it. We all know that she can do a lot better in a science course rather than an arts one, and yet being admitted to a science course requires higher grades.





M6
我知道你已经帮了 Tracy 很多了，但请你再尽力帮帮她。如果她没有考到一个好成绩，并没能在大学学到理科，她的未来就完了！
I know you have helped Tracy a lot. But please try to help her more! If she doesn’t get a good score and fails to get into science at university, she won’t have a future.
