A yong women is consultanting her GP about Air-Travel

Original
Translated





E1
Good Afternoon Miss Xu, I understand you about to go to Europ for a month long holiday.
下午好，徐小姐，我了解到你要去欧洲度假一个月。




M1
是的医生这也是我今天来见你的原因，最近在电视和报纸上报道了许多关于经济舱综合症的事儿，我这次到欧洲旅游要乘20多个小时的飞机， 买的就是经济舱的票， 您说我会得经济舱综合症么？
Yes, doctor, that’s why I came to see you today. Recentely, there have been a lot of reports about Economic-Class-Syndrome on TV and Nespapers. It will take me over 20 hours to fly to Europe, I bought an economic ticket. Do you think I will get Economic-Class-Syndrome?




E2
There are many factors that can predispose someone to developing economy class syndrome. These factors can be genetic (基因； /dʒɪ’netɪk/) or life style realted, seating still for long period of time is unfortunately one of them, which is why its ofen occurs among travellers on long distance air-flight.
有很多因素会使人容易患上经济舱综合征。这些因素可能是基因或生活方式的关系，不幸的是，长时间坐着不动是其中之一，这就是为什么它经常发生在长途飞行的旅客中。




M2
对， 我就是看了九号电视台的新闻时事节目， 才知道远程飞行可能会导致经济舱综合症的， 以前我从没听说过这种病也不了解所以非常担心。
Yes, I just watched A current Affair on Channel Nine and realized that long-distance flight may cause Economy Class Syndrome. I have never heard of this disease before and I don’t understand it.




E3
Ok, how about people who suffer from it, almost always have additional risk factors. Such as Coronary Heart Disease, smoking and family history of thrombosis or a high doses of Combined Oral Contraceptives. Do any of this apply to you?
好吧，那么患有这种病的人呢， 他们几乎总是有另外的风险因素， 如冠心病，吸烟和家族遗传的血栓或高剂量的复方口服避孕药。




M3
我正在服用复方口服避孕药是这个医疗中心的另一位医生给我的，不过我没有家族史， 从不吸烟， 也没有心脏病。
I’m on a Combined Oral Contraceptives pill that was given to me by another doctor at this medical center, but I have no family history, never smoked, and I don’t have a heart disease.




E4
The risk of you getting DVT during your flight is very low. and that risk can be further reduced if you heed a number of recommendations
你在飞行中患上深度静脉血栓(Deep Vein Thrombosis)的风险非常低。如果你听从一些建议，这种风险可以进一步降低




M4
要不要停止服用我的避孕药呢？ 我的朋友建议我吃阿司匹林， 说这样能减少我得经济舱综合症的风险， 这是真的吗？
Should I stop taking my Combined Oral Contraceptives? My friend suggested me to take aspirin, saying it would reduce the risk of getting Economy Class Syndrome. Is this true?




E5
You don’t need to cease to taking your pills as the risks a lot, and for aspirn its may cause excessive bleeding in some cases. So I would not recommend that you take it as a preventative measure.
你不必停药，因为风险太大，而且阿司匹林有时会引起大出血。 所以我不建议你把它作为一种预防措施。




M5
听你解释后我就放心了心了， 我平时很少坐长途飞机， 所以担心会得这种综合症， 现在我不担心了， 谢谢你医生。
After hearing your explanation, I feel relieved. I don’t take long-distance flights often, so I am worried about developing this syndrome. Now I am not worried anymore. Thank you doctor.




E6
You are welcome, have a safe and happy trip.
不用谢，一路平安，旅途愉快。







背景
Economy class syndrome 经济舱并发症: the formation of blood clots (血凝块) in veins (静脉) deep within the legs deep vein thrombosis (深度静脉血栓), occuring during (or just after) a long airplane flight, especially in economy class (tourist class) where there is the least space allotted per passenger and  legs tend especially to be immobilized for lack of leg room.


The economy class syndrome is directly related to immobility for long periods during which blood pools in the legs, raising the risk of clot formation. The tendency to immobility is often compounded by the fasten-seat belt sign, the presence of carts in the aisles, etc.
Other risk factors (风险因素) contributing to the syndrome include lower oxygen pressure and dehydration. Changes in oxygen pressure in the cabin tend to decrease the oxygen level in the blood. The  air in the cabin lacks the normal degree of humidity which contributes to dehydration. The serving of coffee, tea and alcoholic beverages (all of which are diuretics) further causes passengers to become dehydrated.
The economy class syndrome is not confined(受限制) to that class on a plane. It is recommended that all persons traveling on air flights, irrespective of which clss they are in, drink lots of water and move their legs by salking whenever possible and by periodically flexing and extending their ankles, knees, and hips(臀部) to minimize risk of economy class syndrome. Deep Vein Thrombosis is potentially serious and can even have fatal consequences.
