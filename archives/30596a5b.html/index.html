涨租社福

相遇
11-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue is between Mrs. Lu and Henry, a Centrelink officer. Mrs. Lu is enquiring about rent assistance.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Hello, Mrs. Lu. My name is Henry. How have things been recently?
12




M1
说实话，糟透了。我之前找到一份清洁的兼职，所以你们减少了我的失业金。现在他们不需要我了，你们是否能将失业金调高？
To be honest, things couldn’t be worse. I found a part-time cleaning job, so you lowered my payment. Now they don’t want me anymore, so can you now increase the payment again?




E2
After we have re-assessed your income we’ll decide whether your payment should be increased. Just fill out this form and it should be done within three working days.
28




M2
还有一件事, 我想看看你们是否还能帮我什么。上周房东通知我要涨房租了。要是你们不帮我，我就要无家可归了。
One more thing, I want to see what else you can help me with. Last week the landlord said he would raise the rent. I will be homeless if you don’t help me.




E3
I think our rent assistance may offer you a lot of help in your current dilemma. You are getting more than the base rate of Family Tax Benefit and you’re over 25 years old. Is your landlord a private one?
40




M3
是的，是我通过一家房产中介找到他的。我已经在那儿住了两年。我也试过申请公租房，但没申请到。你为什么问这个？
Yes, I found him through a property agent. I have been living there for two years. I tried to apply for public housing, but in the end I didn’t get it. Why do you ask?




E4
Rent Assistance is generally not payable if you have a lease with a state or territory housing authority or if you own or are buying the home in which you live, except for mobile and relocatable homes.
39




M4
我要是有自己的房子就好了。你们会付给我多少租房补助呢？希望不是杯水车薪。
I wish I had my own property. How much will you pay me in rent assistance? I hope it’s not an insignificant amount.




E5
You are single, aren’t you? If your fortnightly rent is more than $265.40, you can receive the maximum payment of rent assistance which is $119.40 per fortnight.
27




M5
我只是租了整栋房其中的一间，所以我没有正式的租约，在这种情况下我应该如何证明我交的房租是多少呢？
I am only renting one room in that house, so I didn’t sign any formal lease. Under such circumstances, how can I prove how much rent I am paying?




E6
You need to complete a Rent Certificate and the person you pay rent to will need to sign it. When we’ve got the certificate we’ll calculate how much you can get.
31




M6
那好，我明白了，非常感谢。
Ok, I see. Thank you very much.
