母亲移民

相遇
05-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue is between Mr. Lu, a new migrant, and Henry, an immigration officer. Mr. Lu is seeking advice regarding bringing his mother to live in Australia.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Sorry for keeping you waiting for so long. How can I help you today?





M1
我希望你可以帮到我。我想要一些资讯关于我的母亲是不是可以在澳洲和我的家人生活在一起。
I do hope you can help me. I’d like to have some information regarding whether my mother can come and live with my family in Australia.




E2
Well first of all, are you an Australian citizen or permanent resident? Also, if you don’t mind me asking, how old is your mother?
我从 1999 年就已经是澳洲公民了。我的父亲不久前刚去世, 留下我




M2
母亲一个人生活。到三月份我母亲就 63 岁了。
I have been an Australian citizen since 1999. My father passed away not long ago, leaving my mother alone. My mother will turn 63 in March.




E3
In that case, she may be eligible to apply for a parent visa to come to Australia as an aged parent. But she also needs to pass the family balance test.





M3
你能不能给我解释一下什么是家庭平衡测试？我听说过，但是并不是真正的明白。
Could you explain to me what the family balance test is? I have heard of it but I don’t really understand.




E4
Basically to pass the family balance test, at least half of your mother’s children must be living in Australia or more of them live in Australia than any other single country.





M4
我明白了。我有一个妹妹她生活在墨尔本，和一个哥哥住在中国。我母亲没有其他的子女或继子女了。
I see. I have a younger sister living in Melbourne and an older brother living in China. My mother doesn’t have any other children or step-children.




E5
Well if that is the case, it makes your mother eligible to apply to come here as an aged parent. She needs to complete and sign an application form. I would also suggest that you buy a copy of our parent migration booklet.





M5
行，我会先把表格里该填都帮她填好，然后再寄回中国给她签字。 可是，为什么我需要买一本小册子呢？
Ok. I will fill out everything on the application form for her first, and then send it to China so she can sign it. But why do I need to buy a booklet?




E6
It contains the information you need regarding applying for a parent visa. It lists the basic requirements that must be met and sponsorship information and contains the application form and a checklist of supporting documents.





M6
我明白了。好吧。我买一份。告诉我，一旦你们收到她的申请表，申请的处理过程需要多长时间？申请费要多少钱？
I see. Ok, I’ll take one. Could you tell me, once you receive her application, how long will it take to process? How much is the application fee?




E7
We can’t begin processing her application unless the application form is signed and every question is answered. Also, the supporting documents must be attached and the visa application fee must be paid. The current fee is 1,470 Australian dollars and there’s no refund given for unsuccessful applications.





M7
我明白了。那么，如果我们递交了所有的文件和表格，并且付了申请费。申请受理要多长时间？
I understand. So how long is the processing time once we have submitted all the documents and forms and paid the fee?




E8
I’m afraid that the average timeframe for processing applications in the aged parent category is currently 24 to 48 months.





M8
我明白了。好的，我要一份小册子。再回家和我的家人商量一下。谢谢。
All right. I see. I’ll take a booklet. Then I’ll go home and discuss it with my family. Thank you.
