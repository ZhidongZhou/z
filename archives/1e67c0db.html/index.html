儿子入学

This is a dialogue between Mr. Gao and a school principal about enrolling Mr. Gao’s son in school.

ID Original Translated
E1 Good morning Mr. Gao. Thank you for coming to this enrolment appointment for Jeremy. 14
M1 早上好。我已经带来了注册入学所需要的文件。这是市政税的账单，上面有我们的住址。这是 Jeremy 的出生证明。 Good morning. I have brought here all the required documents for enrolment. This is my council tax bill with our residential address. And this is Jeremy’s birth certificate.
E2 Thank you. Well, on top of that, we still need his immunisation history statement from the Australian Childhood Immunisation Register. 20
M2 哦，这我也带来，给你。我还有个担心。虽然 Jeremy 在国内也学习过英语，但这毕竟不是他的母语。我怕他上课跟不上。 Yes, I have it here too. Here you are. Now I still have one concern. Jeremy has studied English in China, but English is not his mother tongue. I’m worrying that he may have difficulty keeping up in class
E3 I understand your concern. Fortunately, we have ‘English as a Second Language’ programs, which can support students who have recently arrived in Australia and Australian born students from a non-English speaking background. 32
M3 那他是否能在学校学习中文呢？一方面，我希望他能够继承祖先的文化，另一方面，能说双语也是他未来发展的优势。
E4 Although there are no Chinese language classes available at our school, Jeremy can attend the Saturday School of Community Languages. We also have resources to promote different traditions in language and culture. Can he study Chinese at school? Not only do I hope he can carry on the Chinese culture, but being bilingual can be an advantage for his career development.
M4 那太好了。Jeremy 在中国的时候已经读完了 2 年级，他在这里应该从几年级开始读呢？他会学习哪些科目呢？
E5 I think he should start from Year 2 since the curriculum here is quite different. He will be learning English, Mathematics, Science and Technology, Human Society and its Environments, Personal Development, Health and Physical Education and Creative Arts. That’s great. Jeremy had finished Grade 2 in China. What year should he start from here? What subjects will he study?
M5 这些课程听上去很高级啊，比如科技和人类社会什么的，中国的小学生都不学这个的。如果 Jeremy 觉得有困难，你们是否能够提供辅导来帮助他进步呢？
E6 Yes. Students who experience learning difficulties in grasping the basics are supported through the Learning Assistance Program at their local school. Students in primary and secondary school may receive additional assistance in literacy, numeracy and language. These courses all sound quite advanced. Chinese students do not study technology and human society in primary schools. If Jeremy has difficulty, can you provide tutoring to help him progress?
M6 那我就放心了。校长先生，希望我儿子入校后你能够关注他的成长，有什么问题随时联系我，谢谢。
