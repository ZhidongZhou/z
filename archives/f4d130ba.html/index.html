养老咨询

ZDZ
06-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mrs Hong, a mandarin speaking woman is consulting with Thomas, the
manager of an aged care centre. * * *











ID
Original
Translated






E1
你好！谢谢你抽出时间来跟我面谈。我今天来是想要了解一下你们这个养老院的一些大致情况。
Hello, thank you for taking the time to see me. I’m here today to
get some general information about your aged care centre.




M1
Good morning! Thank you very much for showing your interest in our
centre. Please have a seat. My name is Thomas, I’m more than happy to
help you.
28




E2
是这样的。我已经 75
岁了。我打算从家里搬出来，因为我的儿子和儿媳妇不再愿意和我住在一起了，说是没有隐私了。我想了解一下你们这里不同房间的价格。
Well, I’m 75 and I am planning to move out of my home, because my
son and his wife don’t like living with me anymore. They said they need
more privacy. I’d like to know the rates for different types of rooms
here?




M2
Sure. Actually it is a very wise decision to choose us as we have
various types of rooms which come with a variety of rates so you can
choose the one that best suits your needs. The most popular one is a
studio.
43




E3
哦，我了解了。那么你们这里通常都是住着什么样的人呢？我希望我的左邻右舍都是很好心又乐于助人的人。人老了，就图个心情好，要是遇到一些不好的邻居，那就麻烦了。
Oh, I see. So generally speaking, what kind of people are living
here? I ‘d like to have some kind and warm-hearted neighbours. When
you’re old, being happy is all one cares about. If my neighbours happen
to be unfriendly, that would cause trouble.




M3
Don’t worry. Our centre has been among the top ten aged care centres
for five years in a row, and we are as careful in choosing our members
as they are in choosing us. If there is any dispute, you can always
refer it to our dispute mediation committee to have it solved
fairly.
54




E4
听起来还不错，你们还有专门的调解委员会。那么我想问问你们对客户在健康方面的要求有什么特别设计的一些设施吗？
Sounds great! So you have this particular mediation committee here.
Also I’d like to know, do you have any specially designed facilities and
support for members with special health needs?




M4
Oh yes, we take our members’ health issues into consideration. We
have a carer team to help people who have Alzheimer’s and dementia, and
all the rooms are specifically designed for seniors, and include things
such as automatic lighting systems and heating systems. Our members’
specific health requirements are seen as very important to us.
55




E5
我有健忘症，还有高血压，而且你也看到了，我是用轮椅的。你们这里有针对我这样情况的设施和护理人员吗？
I have amnesia and hypertension. And as you can tell, I am a wheel
chair user.Do you have any facilities or carers to help people in my
kind of situation?




M5
Amnesia and hypertension are common among seniors, and when we first
designed this centre, we took this into consideration. We have a team of
16 experienced nurses to provide professional care. Also, our rooms are
all purpose built, which means they are all wheel-chair accessible.
45




E6
这很好。那么关于心理方面，你们有相关的专业人员吗？你知道，老年人很容易感到孤单，被社会隔离等，这些负面的心理倾向往往会引发一些生理上的问题。
That’s good. What about the mental health services? Do you have any
professionals? You know seniors often feel lonely and socially isolated.
These negative psychological tendencies often lead to mental
problems




M6
I understand your concern. We have our own counselling service
centre and activity centre. Our professional counsellors are more than
happy to help each of our members. Also, social activities such as
shopping and seeing movies are organised on a monthly basis.
42
