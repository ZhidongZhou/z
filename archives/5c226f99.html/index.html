蛀牙手术

相·遇

Hits

This is a dialogue between a general dentist Mr Bryant and a patient, Ms Gao, regarding her toothache caused by horizontal tooth impaction.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Ms Gao. What brings you here today?
9




M1
医生，早上好，我牙齿的 X 光的结果出来了吗? 上次检查，您说我是长智齿 了。我每天都牙疼得吃不下、睡不香，生活一团糟。
Good morning, doctor. Have you received my dental X-ray results? Last time, you told me that I have a wisdom tooth coming out. I cannot eat or sleep well due to the pain. My life is just a mess!




E2
I have examined your X-ray report, I can see that you do have one wisdom tooth at the back left corner of your mouth. It seems that it is causing the gum around the tooth to be infected, and adjacent teeth to decay also. We have to treat this matter seriously, otherwise you might end up with severe complications.
59




M2
您指的是哪些并发症？您认为我现在的牙周炎是由长智齿引起的么 ? 我 近来已经格外注意保持口腔卫生了，但似乎也没什么帮助。
What kind of complications are you referring to? Do you think my current periodontitis was caused by this? I have been paying particular attention to keeping my mouth clean lately but it seems it hasn’t been much help.




E3
The most common complications include erosion cavities, infections, crowding and cysts. Your wisdom tooth has stalled halfway, which makes brushing and flossing of it extremely difficult. Food and bacteria have built up around the neighbouring molars, causing decay and gum infection. I can imagine the enormous pain you are experiencing.
50




M3
是这样， 那麻烦您再给我开点抗生素和止痛药吧！上次的药， 我都已经用完 了。我每天都疼得受不了，而且越来越严重。怎么才能彻底让它不痛呢？
That’s true. I have finished all the antibiotics and pain killers that you prescribed for me last time. Could you give me some more? This pain is unbearable and worsening each day. How can I completely get rid of this pain?




E4
Unfortunately, although mouth infections such as periodontitis caused by wisdom teeth can be treated with antibiotics, the infection will re-occur unless the wisdom teeth are removed.
26




M4
一定要拔吗？虽然我身边的很多朋友都拔了，但我听说拔牙也可能引发许多后 期的问题，比如说牙齿渗血、出血，牙槽干燥，甚至神经损伤。如果真的是这 样，那听起来可太吓人了。
Do I have to? Many of my friends have had their wisdom teeth extracted, but I was told that it could cause many post-extraction problems. For example, bleeding and oozing, dry sockets or even nerve injury. It sounds so terrible if that is true.




E5
But I’m afraid it is the best option. As you can see from the X-ray, the emerging tooth is angled 90 degrees sideways, and growing into the roots of the second molar, which is generally known as horizontal tooth impaction. We will have to remove it.
46




M5
看来是别无选择了。你看我的下牙是不是都被挤歪了？我是不是还要戴牙套？ 我小的时候戴过好几年，但是总被人嘲笑，我可不想再经历一次了。那手术时 会不会很疼？
It seems I have no other choice. Have you noticed that my lower teeth are crooked? Do you think I need to get braces? I wore them for years when I was little, and I was often laughed at because of that. I don’t want to go there again. Will I suffer a lot of pain in the surgery?




E6
You will be under local anaesthesia during the surgery so you won’t feel any pain during the procedure. We can talk about orthodontic treatment after the surgery.
27




M6
那我就照您说的做吧。请您给我安排个手术时间。谢谢。
Ok, then I will follow your instructions. Please schedule a time for my surgery. Thank you.
