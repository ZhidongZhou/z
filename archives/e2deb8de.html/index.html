性别歧视

相遇
07-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Ms. Yang is speaking to a Legal Aid officer about gender discrimination and unfair dismissal.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
你好，我想来咨询一下关于性别歧视和不公平解雇方面的问题。
Hello, I am here to make an enquiry about gender discrimination and unfair dismissal.




M1
Certainly. Do you feel you have been the victim of this in your work place? Would you like to tell me more about it?
24




E2
在被解雇前, 我在一家俱乐部做代理经理，每星期工作 20 小时。前 不久俱乐部的董事们决定将这个职位提升为全职职位。 // 可是一星 期前董事们给了我两周通告，把我辞退了， 理由是他们想找个男的 来做这份工作。我觉得这太不公平了！
I was the acting manager of a club before I got dismissed. I worked there 20 hours a week. Not long ago, the club directors decided to make this role a full-time position. However, a week ago, they gave me a two- week dismissal notice, because they preferred to get a male staff member to do the job. I think it’s so unfair!




M2
Based on what you’ve told me, the actions of the club directors are indeed against the law. Have you tried talking to them? If the club has a set of policies or a procedure to deal with this kind of thing, it would be advisable to try to get this problem addressed through these channels first.
56




E3
我已经试过和董事们沟通了，但他们不听，我也没有办法。俱乐部也没有专门应付此类事件的策略或程序 – 我是代理经理，这一点我最清楚了。
I tried to talk to the directors but they wouldn’t listen to me. There’s nothing I could do about it. And the club doesn’t have any policies or procedures to deal with that kind of issue. I know that better than anyone else as a previous acting manager there.




M3
What I suggest you do then is to write to the Anti-Discrimination Board, explain what happened and ask them to help you. It won’t cost you anything and you don’t need a lawyer to do it, but the complaint must be in written form, and you must sign it.
49




E4
可是，我的英语水平很差， 肯定看都看不懂那些表格和文件。万一 写错了什么地方，可怎么办呢？所以，您能不能替我起草一份投诉书呢？
But my English is very poor. I can’t even understand those documents and forms. What if I make a mistake? Can you help me draft a complaint application?




M4
You can write to the board in any language, and there are discrimination complaint forms you can obtain from their website, so you don’t have to worry about your English skills. The board staff can also help you complete the form.
41




E5
那太好了。那您能否现在帮我打开那个反歧视委员会的网站，然后帮我把表格打印出来呢？口译员在这里，更好可以帮我翻译。那么我投诉之后会怎样呢？
That’s great. Could you now go to the website of the AntiDiscrimination Board and print out the forms for me? The interpreter here can help me translate. So what will happen after the complaint is lodged?




M5
Sure. Here you are. The board will investigate your complaint, and if they find that the actions of your employer were against the law, they will try to facilitate conciliation. The settlement will be a private agreement between you and your employer, and what it entails depends on what both parties are willing to offer and accept.
57




E6
听起来有些复杂而且很耗时间吧？我想继续在俱乐部里做经理，您觉得这有可能吗？
It sounds a bit complicated and time-consuming. I’d like to continue being a manager at the club. Do you think this would be possible?




M6
It will depend on what your employers are willing to offer. They may choose to offer you an apology, financial compensation, or perhaps your old position. You will have to talk to them about it when the board organises a conciliation session.
42
