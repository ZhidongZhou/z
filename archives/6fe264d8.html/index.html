入室行窃

相·遇
31-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mr Liu, a mandarin speaking man, is reporting a home burglary to the police.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Hi. Mr. Liu. Now the interpreter is here. We are able to understand you now You have reported that you’ve been robbed; please tell me when and what did they take.
31




M1
谢天谢地，你终于来了。今天晚上我回家的时候发现门是开着的，我进去一 看，有个人正在往我新买的宝马上装我那心爱的 60 寸电视。
Thank goodness you are finally here. When I came home tonight I found my door was unlocked. When I got in closer, I saw a guy was loading my treasured 60’’ TV into my newly bought BMW.




E2
Did you get a good look at the guy who was trying to take your stuff， and did you confront him?
22




M2
没看清楚，因为我下班回来已经天黑了，而且那小偷也每开灯。我没敢惹那 小偷，他看到我以后就马上开走了。
No, as it was already dark then and the thief did not turn on the light. I didn’t dare confront him. He drove away quickly after he saw me.




E3
How many guys did you see at that particular moment? And can you give me a detailed list of things that are missing?
23




M3
我隐隐约约看到车里有 2 个人，加上房间里的。一共 3 个。丢的东西太多 了，你让我好好看看房间里少了什么，因为我刚刚一直在等你们，没敢碰里 面的东西。
I vaguely saw there were two guys in the car, plus the one inside my house, so in total there were three. I lost so many things. Please let me have a look at what has been stolen because I have been waiting for you and did not touch anything.




E4
You are right to keep the crime scene uncontaminated. Ok, Mr. Liu, now you can go in and see what’s missing. Just be careful, try not to touch anything on the floor or on the walls and doors.
38




M4
恩。刚买的车被开走了，丢的还有电视，电脑，游戏机，iPad。还有一些 钱。
Hmm, the car I just bought was driven away. My TV, computer, video game console, iPad and some cash were also stolen.




E5
I think the cash is something you won’t be able to claim. Can you give me an estimated total value of the stuff missing, and have you got home and contents insurance?
32




M5
所有丢的东西加起来一共$5 万多块钱吧，车买了保险，其他的没有保险，因 为是租的房子怎么可能有保险。
That would be around $50,000 in total. The car is insured but the others are not. It’s impossible for me to get insurance for this is only a rented place.




E6
Ok, I will give you a police note with which you can make a claim for the car from your insurer, but as for the other things, you didn’t get them insured so there is nothing I can do.
39




M6
好吧，那也只能干认倒霉吧。希望你们能尽快把这些窃贼捉拿归案。谢谢你 了警官。
All right, it seems I have to take this bad luck. I hope you can catch those burglars as soon as possible. Thank you officer.
