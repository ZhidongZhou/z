谈性骚扰

相·遇
14-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This dialogue is an interview between a mandarin-speaking journalist and a guest professor who is an expert in the study of sexual harassment.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
您的到场让我倍感荣幸，教授。非常感谢您能以专业的视角探讨性骚扰这 个话题，并和我们分享一些您的看法。
I am very honoured by your presence, professor. I will be highly grateful if you could offer your professional perspectives on the topic of sexual harassment and share with me some of your points of view on this issue.




M1
And I am very glad to be here and thanks for having me. I will try and address your issues to the very best of my ability.
27




E2
太好了。谢谢。现在人们的共识是，性骚扰就是指在性的方面骚扰某人。 这个定义准确吗？
Great. Thank you. Now it is common knowledge that sexual harassment indicates bothering someone in a sexual way. Is this an accurate definition?




M2
No, I am afraid not. It is in fact a violation of the Civil Rights Act. The confusing aspect is that perpetrators are not arrested. This is because sexual harassment is not a violation of state criminal codes. Rather, remedy is through civil litigation.
44




E3
谢谢您为我们解释清楚。那么，就定义而言，如果我说，工作场所的性骚 扰可以发生在任何时间、任何地点，发生在任何两个人之间，这种说法对吗？
Thanks for your clarification. Speaking of the definition, am I right in saying that workplace sexual harassment can occur anytime, anywhere, between any two people?




M3
Not really. As in the discussion above, workplace sexual harassment can occur outside the office, but it cannot occur between any two people. The harassment must take place between people who work together.
33




E4
我明白了。那骚扰者一定必须是老板或者上级吗？如果是客户或顾客呢？ 如果他们做出不当性关注，会怎么样呢？这算是性骚扰吗？
I see. So the harasser has to be a boss or supervisor? What about a client or a customer? What if they make unwanted sexual advances? Can it be regarded as sexual harassment?




M4
The same definition applies. If the harasser is a co-worker, not a boss, it can also be regarded as sexual harassment. The workplace is supposed to be a safe place where employees are protected from any inappropriate or promiscuous actions by customers, vendors, or clients.
45




E5
如果不当触碰只发生了一次会怎样？那就不算性骚扰，是吗？我听说性骚 扰的受害者并不一定是异性，对吗？
What if the unwanted touching occurs only once, that is not a sexual harassment, is it? I’ve also heard that the victim of sexual harassment does not necessarily have to be of the opposite gender, do they?




M5
That is correct; people can also be capable of committing sexual harassment against individuals of the same sex under current laws. However, many people are not aware of this yet. And as to your other question, even if it only occurs once it is still considered sexual harassment.
48




E6
是啊。很多人对于同性之间的性骚扰仍然存在一些误解。下面我问个棘手 的问题，根据刑法，旁观者可能成为性骚扰的受害者吗？
True. Still many people have some misunderstandings when it comes to sexual harassment occurring among people of the same sex. Now this is a tricky issue, according to criminal law, can a bystander be the victim of sexual harassment?




6.1
The truth is, criminal law stipulates that the victim can be anyone who is affected by offensive conduct. Let me break it down for you, a more likely example would be one in which person A (a supervisor) is having a sexual relationship with person B (a subordinate).
48




6.2
Person C (a subordinate) is not getting promotions due to the fact that he or she is not in a sexual relationship with person A. This type of favouritism creates a discriminatory situation for person C as well as the potential for coercion due to the implication that C must have sex with A in order to get ahead.
59




M6
非常感谢您为我答疑解惑。您对于性骚扰这一复杂问题的详细解释让我受益匪浅。
Many thanks for answering my questions and addressing my concerns, I learned a lot from your explanations on these complicated issues regarding sexual harassment.
