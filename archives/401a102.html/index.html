CCL产后忧郁

相·遇
30-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NAATI › CCL › Medical
CCL 

        

        



Dr. Bride is in a conversation with a patient, Mrs. Yu, about inviting her to join his research on post-natal depression.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Hello, Mrs. Yu. Good to see you today. How are you feeling?
12




M1
你好医生，我很好，谢谢。待会儿我准备和我先生去看场电影。
Hello Doctor, I am very well. Thank you. I am going to see a movie with my husband later on.




E2
That’s great. Your file indicates that you were diagnosed with post-natal depression early last month and have undergone treatment. It seems you are a lot better now, right?
28




M2
是的。记得我第一次来看您的时候，状况真的糟透了。我当时真的觉 得人生没有喜乐，很无助。要不是我先生逼我来看医生，我可能现在 还在痛苦中挣扎。
Yes. I remember when I first came to see you, my condition couldn’t be worse. I found no joy in my life and I felt helpless. I would still be suffering right now if my husband hadn’t urged me to see a doctor.




E3
I’m happy to hear that you’re making progress, Mrs Yu. I’d like to let you know that we are currently doing research on post-natal depression and we’d like to invite you to be part of this research.
37




M3
关于产后抑郁的研究，到底是个什么研究呢？我并不介意加入你们的 研究，不过你能向我简单介绍一下这项研究吗？
Research on post-natal depression? What is that exactly? I don’t mind being part of it, but could you tell me a bit more about it?




E4
The research is called “The ABX Discovery Program”. We’re now at Phase 2 of the program which involves clinical trials of a new drug ABX on patients with post-natal depression.
30




M4
就是说要我帮您试药是吗？这对我和胎儿来说，安全吗？我现在还在 吃你上一次给我开的药呢。
You mean you want me to try out this new medication for you? Is it safe for me and the baby? I’m now still taking the medication you prescribed for me last time.




E5
ABX is a new anti-depressant. It has been tested before with healthy volunteers and presented some very positive results. We now want to test the drug on patients with post-natal depression to find out if the new drug would work better than the drug you’re currently taking.
47




M5
我的另外一个担心是， 这药还没正式获得政府批准， 会不会有副作 用？万一出现了意料之外的状况怎么办？
I have another concern. Since this medication is not approved by the government yet, could there be some side effects? What if something happens out of the blue?




E6
This drug has gone through a number of laboratory tests, including tests on humans. As I indicated earlier, we know of no toxins or negative side effects at this moment. But if it is proven effective through our research program, it may help more women with similar cases to you.
50




M6
那我还是先回家和我先生商量一下再做决定吧。 毕竟还是有风险的， 对吧？所以，您也别抱太大希望了。
I think I should go back and talk with my husband about it before making any decisions. After all, it still involves some risks, right? So don’t get your hopes up.
