接机国宾

相遇
25-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mr. Thomas (T), an employee at Sydney Company and Mr. Yang (Y), a visitor from China.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Excuse me, sir. Are you Mr Min Yang from Beijing Yuntong Co. Ltd?
打扰了先生，请问您是北京运通公司的杨民先生吗？




M1
是的，我是。您一定是 Thomas 先生吧，如果我没有弄错的话。
Yes, I am. You must be Mr. Thomas, if I’m not mistaken.




E2
Yes. I’m Tim Thomas, the secretary of the Human Resource Department at the Gold Leaf Group. We have been looking forward to your coming, sir.
是的，我是 Tim Thomas， Gold Leaf 集团人力资源部的秘书。我们一直 都期待着您的到来。




M2
谢谢您亲自到机场来接我。这个机场真是太棒了，绝对是世界一流的机场。
Thank you for coming to meet me at the airport. This is a fantastic airport, absolutely one of the top airports in the world.




E3
I’m very glad to have the honor of meeting you in my hometown. This is indeed a first-class international airport, as everybody says. We’re very grateful that you took time from your busy schedule and come to visit us.
我很高兴能有这样的荣幸在我的家乡欢迎您。 正如大家所说的，这个机 场的确是世界一流的国际机场。我们也非常感激您能在百忙之中抽出时间来我们公司访问。




M3
我很期待能更好地了解贵公司。很感谢能有机会向澳大利亚的企业“取取经”。
I’ve been looking forward to learning more about your company. I really appreciate this opportunity to learn from an Australian company.




E4
How was the trip? It must be very tiring, flying for more than 10 hours.
您这一路还好么？飞了 10 个多小时，一定很疲劳吧。




M4
还不算太坏。但是我们比预期的时间晚了一些。起飞的时候就因为遇上了暴风雨而推迟了。但后来，飞行天气就非常好了，而且机上的服务当然也很不错。
Not too bad. But we were later than expected. Our plane was delayed taking off as we ran into a storm. But we had good weather for flying and the service on the plane was really good, of course.




E5
Well, you must be very tired after the long trip, and you have to get over the jet-lag. We have got all the luggage, haven’t we? Let’s drive directly to the hotel.
这样，长途跋涉之后您一定很累了。而且您还必须克服时差的问题。我 们是不是已经拿好全部的行李了，是吗？让我们直接开车到酒店去吧。




M5
是啊，是有点累。而且我很不习惯时差。但过个两天，我就没问题了。
Yes, I’m a bit tired. I’m very bad with jet-lag. But I’ll be alright in a couple of days.




E6
Good. We’ll take you to the hotel for a good rest. Here’s the schedule for tomorrow. We’ll host a reception lunch in your honour. Then you’ll meet with the chairman of the board in the afternoon. We’ll take you to an aboriginal show in the evening. Do you think that arrangement is suitable, Mr Yang?
好的。我们这就带您去酒店好好休息。您明天早上不需要起太早。这是明天的安排。我们会为您举办一个欢迎午餐，之后您将在下午和董事长会面。 晚上的时候我们会带您去看土著表演。您觉得这个安排合适么，杨先生？




M6
我喜欢这样的安排。我听说澳大利亚人民是非常友善好客的。恐怕我很快我就会被你们宠坏的。您想得非常周到，Thomas 先生。谢谢您啦！
I like that. I’ve heard Australian people are very friendly and hospitable. I will soon be spoiled, I’m afraid. You’re very considerate, Mr. Thomas. Thank you!
