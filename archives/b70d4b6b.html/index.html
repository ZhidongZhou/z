车祸调节

相·遇
24-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This dialogue is about a motor vehicle accident and takes place outside a courtroom between a woman and a barrister.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Mrs Wu, the insurance company has accepted liability for the accident which means we don’t need any witnesses to give evidence.
21




M1
这是什么意思？是不是意味着这场官司我一定会赢？我能拿到我想要的赔 偿金吗？
What does this mean? Does it mean I will definitely win the case? Can I get the compensation I want?




E2
ID They just admitted that they have responsibility to cover the damage caused by the accident, but they don’t consider the damage to be quite as serious as you do. I’d advise you to settle it out of court.
39




M2
我不明白。不是说我不需要证人了，那就说明我的要求是合理的。我打赢 这场官司的可能性是很大的，为什么要庭外和解呢？
I don’t understand. You said I don’t need any witnesses. That means I have a good chance to win this case, why should I settle it out of court?




E3
The problem is your wrist didn’t start to bother you until six months after the accident. The judge may decide your wrist injury was not caused by the accident and only award you a small sum of money for the injury to your leg.
44




M3
这不是真的。当救护车驾驶员把我从车里抬出来的时候，我的腿部已经受 了伤，正在出血伤得很严重。在医院接受 X 光检查时，医生发现我的手腕 也受了伤。
40That is not true. When the paramedics took me from the ambulance, my leg was injured and bleeding. It was very serious. When I had an X ray in the hospital, the doctor found that my wrist was also injured.




E4
Why didn’t you tell me this earlier Mrs Wu, I didn’t even know X-rays were taken. This is very important information.
21




M4
我怎么会知道？我压根儿忘了提它，你应该帮助我，你是律师，你应该是 知道法律在这个国家是如何运行的。它与我们国家完全不一样, 所以我一 头雾水。
44How should I have known this was important? I totally forgot about it. You should help me. You are a lawyer and you should know how the legal system works here. It is totally different from China, so I have no clue at all.




E5
I put forward an offer to them of $15,000 inclusive of all costs, but they didn’t agree to it. They have come back with a counter offer of only $7,500, which will leave you with approximately $1,500 clear after paying all medical expenses and legal costs.
46




M5
律师费和医疗费需六千澳元? 简直不可思议。这和抢劫有什么区别。人人 都想从中捞一把，我才是真正的受伤者。
Medical and legal costs are $6000? This is ridiculous, it’s almost like robbery. I am the victim but everyone else is benefiting from this.




E6
Please calm down. This figure is based on the evidence you have. You cannot just ask for whatever amount you want.
21




M6
好吧，事已至此，我还能怎么样！请你尽量帮我打赢 官司。
Alright. There is nothing I can do now. Please try your best to win the case.
