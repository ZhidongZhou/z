儿童津贴

相遇
14-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between a customer (C) and a Centrelink officer regarding the baby bonus.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, please take a seat. How can I help you?
11




M1
是这样，我和我的老公正在考虑从中国领养一个婴儿。我听说有新生儿的父 母可以领取儿童津贴。但是我不太了解如果我领养了孩子可不可以拿到，你 能给我提供更多的关于这个的信息?
Well, my husband and I are thinking of adopting a baby from China. I heard that parents with a new born baby are eligible to get the Baby Bonus, but I have no idea if I can get it for my adopted child. Can you please give me more information about it?




E2
Well, the Baby Bonus is a payment provided to parents who are having a baby or adopting a child. We also provide related services to parents in addition to the financial support which helps cover the many costs involved with raising a child.
43




M2
这就是说我领养的小孩也可以拿到补贴是吗? 你能在详细给我解释下我都应 该怎么做来申领这个津贴吗？
That means I am entitled to the Baby Bonus for my adopted child too, right? Can you explain in more detail what I should do to claim it?




E3
OK. If you adopt a child from overseas you can add them to your Medicare card from the date the child arrives in Australia. This is provided the child is either on a permanent resident visa or a temporary visa with an application lodged for permanent residency.
47




M3
哦，我明白了。除了这个还有别的什么要求吗? 还有什么其他的我可以领取 的津贴吗？
Oh, I see. Apart from this, are there any other requirements we need to meet? Are there any other payments available for me?




E4
An income test applies to the Baby Bonus. This is based on your family’s income for the first six months after your child is born or adopted. If your family does not qualify for the Baby Bonus, you may be eligible for the Paid Parental Leave scheme.
47




M4
带薪育儿假计划? 听说过。领养小孩可以享受产假听起来不错。我从来没有 过小孩，对于这些政策是一窍不通。
Paid Parental Leave scheme? I’ve heard of that. It’s pretty good that parents who adopt a child are also eligible for parental leave. I have never had a child before, so I know nothing about those policies.




E5
I know. If you are eligible for both payments, you can only choose one of them. This is based on which payment is the best decision for your family financially. So I suggest you think about which payment to claim before you send in the application.
46




M5
好的，我会好好计算一下每一笔津贴我能领取的金额。 还有一个问题， 如 果我带孩子回中国住的话，我还可以拿到津贴吗。
Sure, I will calculate carefully how much I can receive for each payment. One more question, what if I take my child to live in China? Can I still receive the payments?




E6
Perhaps, but please be aware that payments can be paid for temporary absences of up to 13 weeks outside Australia, in most circumstances. However, you won’t get any payment beyond that period.
32




M6
我明白了。谢谢您的详细解释。如果有什么问题我会再过来咨询的。
I see. Thank you for your detailed explanation. If I have any questions, I will come back to you.
