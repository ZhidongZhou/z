父母移民

相遇
04-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mr. Liu (L), who wants his parents to migrate to Australia, and a migration agent Henry (H).








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Mr. Liu, how can I help you today?
10




M1
早上好。我想申请签证让我父母移民来澳大利亚，这样我就好照顾他们了。所以我来见你。
Good morning. I’d like to apply for a visa so my parents can migrate toAustralia and I can look after them. That’s why I’m here.




E2
Sure. First of all, could you tell me your residential status? That is, are you an Australian resident and if so, when were you granted your permanent residency?





M2
是的，我是持庇护签证的永久居民。我 2005 年 3 月来澳寻求庇护, 在 2006 年 1 月获得了永久签证。
Yes I am a permanent resident on a protection visa. I came to Australia and sought protection in March, 2005 and I got permanent resident status in January, 2006.




E3
In this case, I think you are a qualified sponsor. How many children orstep-children do your parents have and where do they reside now?





M3
只有我和我哥哥。但他两个月前因癌症在中国去世。在中国没什么比晚年丧子更悲剧的了，所以我想把他们接到澳大利亚来照顾。
Just me and my older brother who died of cancer in China two months ago. You know, in China nothing is more tragic than losing your child when you’re old. That’s why I want to take care of my parents in Australia.




E4
I’m sorry to hear that. Now, I think you meet the basic requirements for sponsoring your parents to come to Australia since you have been a permanent resident for over two years and you are the only remaining child of your parents.





M4
我听说有两种选择，一种是一般父母移民签证，另一种是付费父母移民签证，你能解释一下这两者的区别吗？
I heard that there are two options, which are a parent migrant visa and a contributory parent migrant visa. Could you explain the difference between them?




E5
For the contributory parent migrant visa, applicants have to pay a much larger visa application fee and an Assurance of Support bond. Applicants or their sponsor contribute to Government revenue and help to offset some costs but the waiting period is much shorter than for the parent migrant visa.





M5
我可能选择付费父母移民，因为我想尽早把他们借来。他们同样需要满足健康状况要求，是吗？
I may choose the contributory one because I want to have them here as soon as possible. They also need to meet health requirements, right?




E6
That’s correct. You can find what the requirements are in this brochure. You also need to provide an Assurance of Support before their visa application can be granted.





M6
好的，感谢你的指点。我马上就去准备签证申请。
OK, thanks for this information. I will go and prepare everything for this visa application.
