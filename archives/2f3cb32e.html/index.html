预定团游

相遇
13-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



A Mandarin speaking man is making a phone call to a travel company named NT Adventures. He has an enquiry about making a tour booking to Kakadu National Park for a group of Chinese tourists.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, NT Adventures. How may I help you?
9




M1
你好，我想了解一下怎么样能够去卡卡杜国家公园。我是想给一群中国游客 来预定。
Hi there. I’d like to make an enquiry about booking a tour to Kakadu National Park for a group of Chinese tourists.




E2
Certainly. Could you start by telling me how many people are in your group and how long you’d like to tour the park? NT Adventures has a number of different tour packages available. I’ll try my best to find one most suitable for you.
44




M2
嗯，这个团总共有六个人，他们将会于 9 月 30 号从澳门飞去达尔文，然后 10 月 3 号的早上去飞回去。所以我们想要的是一个两日游。我去年的时候其 实用过你们公司而且觉得你们的工作做的相当好。
It’s a six-person group. They’re flying from Macau to Darwin on the 30 thof September and are flying back on the 3 rdof October in the morning. So we’re after a 2-day tour. I actually used your company last year and thought you did a fantastic job.




E3
Well, that’s good to hear. In that case, there is one particular tour that I think would be suitable for you. It’s a two-day 4WD tour that takes you to see some spectacular waterfalls and local wildlife, including crocs of course.





M3
听起来很棒，因为我们时间紧，可是又想游览到卡卡杜国家公园最美的景 点。那住宿怎么安排呢？这些客人都是来自大公司的企业家，住惯了五星级 酒店。这次他们想尝试些新鲜的体验。
It sounds great as we are short on time and want to see the best of Kakadu National Park. How about accommodation then? The guests are all entrepreneurs from big companies. They have gotten used to five-star hotels. But this time they want to try something different.




E4
Well, there are two options. One is to camp under the stars. The other is to stay in a cabin. Which do you think your group would prefer?
28




M4
我想一个小木屋对我们这个团应该更合适一些。那么，两日游总共有的花费 要多少呢？
I think a cabin would be more suitable for this group. How much will the two-day tour cost in total then?




E5
Well, in total it comes to $810 for twin share and $900 for single room. That price includes entrance to the national park, as well as GST. I’ve added a 10 per cent discount considering you are a repeat customer.





M5
很好。我们这个团说他们想看一下本地的这个传统土著居民的艺术和文化。 他们还说他们想爬爬山，但是我觉得我们可能没有时间去爬山。您怎么看 呢？
Great. They told me they’d like to check out some of the traditional Aboriginal art and culture. They also said they’d like to do some hiking but I don’t think we’ll have time for that. What do you think?




E6
Well, during the tour we actually take you to a local Aboriginal community to experience traditional Aboriginal culture, and see ancient rock paintings. There is some hiking during the tour on the second day, but nothing too strenuous.
38




M6
好，听起来不错。我们这个团里还有这个养鸟的爱好者，他们说如果可能的 话想去看看本地的鸟。
OK, that sounds fine. There are a couple of bird enthusiasts in the group. They said they’d like to do some bird watching if possible.




E7
We may be able to arrange something on the second day of the tour. I’d have to get back to you on that one.
24




M7
谢谢。我 15 分钟之后就给你打个电话过来，商量更多的预定信息。
Thanks. I’ll call you back in 15 minutes, to go through more details about the group booking.
