虫草检疫

相遇
20-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



A Chinese tourist, Mr. Wang, is stopped by a customs officer at the airport.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Mr Wang, the interpreter is here so now we’ll be able to understand each other.
15




M1
多谢你们的安排。我刚才在担心呢，害怕你们会因为我不会说英语，无法辩白而把我抓起来。
Thanks for arranging this. I was worried that you would arrest me because I don’t speak English and cannot defend myself.




E2
Now, can you tell me, what is your purpose for travel? I know you are on a visitor’s visa. Where will you be staying in Australia and how long do you intend to stay?
34




M2
我的女儿女婿在悉尼工作，我这次来看望他们，准备在他们家住两个月。入境卡上的地址就是他们家的地址。
My daughter and her husband work in Sydney. I came here to visit them and I will stay at their place for two months. It’s their address that I have put on the incoming passenger card.




E3
All right. We’ve stopped you at customs because we found this bag, with suspicious contents, in your luggage and we believe it contains pests. Can you tell me what is in this bag?
33




M3
这是一包中草药，需要煎着喝的。我女儿的肠胃功能比较弱，中药能帮她调理调理。
This is a bag of Chinese herbs, which need to be boiled in water before taking. My daughter has poor digestion and Chinese medicine is able to help improve her condition.




E4
But this is something you need to declare. Did your daughter ask you to bring this for her? Did you watch the Australian Quarantine video about what goods should be declared on the plane?
34




M4
不是我女儿要我带的，是我自己要带给她的。我不懂英语，所以视频里说了什么我也不知道。入境卡上除了个人信息，我也是看人家怎么填我就怎么填的。
My daughter didn’t ask me to. It was my own idea to bring her these. I don’t speak English so I didn’t understand what the video said. As for the information on the card, except the personal details, I just copied what others wrote.




E5
This dried plant material is very likely to harbour pests and diseases. Oh, there is a bug crawling. I think it is a khapra beetle, which is the number one pest we want to keep out of Australia.
38




M5
我不知道里面怎么会有活的虫子的。那你们要拿它怎么样呢？你们要没收就没收吧，但我真的是无意触犯贵国的法律。
I don’t know why there are living bugs in it. What are you going to do about it? You can confiscate it if you want, but I really didn’t mean to break the law.




E6
We have to seize and destroy it because it is contaminated with pests. I’m going to give you a written warning and please be aware, next time when you travel, you must declare if you are bringing in something like this.
41




M6
我知道了，我保证以后不会再犯同样的错误。
I understand. I promise I won’t make the same mistake again.
