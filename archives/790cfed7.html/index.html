工伤咨询

This is a dialogue between a solicitor and Mr. Ma, who is consulting the solicitor about his work-related injury.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
This is a dialogue between a solicitor and Mr. Ma, who is consulting the solicitor about his work-related injury. Mr. Ma, I’m sorry to hear about your injury. I do hope that I can help you with your compensation claim.
21




M1
多谢。我之前不知道我可以申请工伤赔偿，多亏我的领班告诉我。希望您能帮我讨个公道。
Many thanks. I didn’t realise that I could claim worker’s compensation. Lucky that my foreman told me about it. I hope that you can help me get a fair settlement.




E2
If you have suffered an injury at work, whether it is temporary or permanent, then you are entitled to claim compensation for any loss of income, medical expenses, rehabilitation costs and future economic losses.
34




M2
老板一定是有责任的，我也不太清楚员工是不是有保险。能不能先告诉我一般工伤赔偿申请的程序是什么?
Your boss is definitely liable. I’m not sure whether the employees are insured. First of all, can you please tell me the procedures of claiming worker’s compensation?




E3
“ First, you need to notify your employer. Then you need to obtain a WorkCover medical certificate from your doctor. Finally, you must notify the insurer of the injury. You also need to ensure that the medical certificate and any associated bills or expenses are given to your employer. Can you tell me what happened?”
54




M3
出意外的时候老板不在现场，只有我和领班在干活，我当时用电钻在砖墙上钻孔。有一小块金属碎屑飞到了我的眼睛里。
The boss was not there when the accident happened. Only the foreman and me were on the job. I was drilling holes through the brick wall using the drill. Some small bits of debris flew into my eye.




E4
Did you wear eye protection when you were drilling? Every employer has a duty to look after the safety of their employees in their workplace.
25




M4
我没有带任何护眼的东西。老板什么安全防护装备都没提供。我听说如果雇主没有在雇员安全问题上采取合理的防范措施，因此导致雇员事故的发生，雇员是可以申请工伤赔偿的。
I did not wear any eye protection and my boss didn’t provide us with any protective equipment. I heard that if the employer has failed to take reasonable precautions for the safety of employees, which then results in accidents, then the employee is eligible to make a workers compensation claim.




E5
That’s right. The employer has a duty to comply with all the relevant statutory requirements and regulations about safe working practices and safe premises in which to work.
28




M5
我当时感到一阵剧痛，领班把我送到了附近的医疗中心，我的眼睛现在经常流眼泪，眨眼时会有刺痒的感觉.
I felt a sharp pain in my eye. The foreman took to the medical centre nearby. Even now, I still have a watery eye and a scratchy feeling when I blink.




E6
Don’t worry, we have access to specialist compensation lawyers who will help you achieve maximum compensation for any injury or loss you may have suffered as a result of the accident.
31




M6
太感谢你了，如果需要的话，我会详细的告诉您所发生的一切.
Thanks a lot. If needed, I’ll tell you in detail everything that has happened.
