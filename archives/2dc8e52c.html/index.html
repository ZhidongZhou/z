庇护签证

相遇
04-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue is between Mr. Guan and Adam, an immigration officer. Mr. Guan is enquiring about applying for a protection visa.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
你好，我想申请庇护签证，希望你能帮我。
Hello! I’d like to apply for a protection visa and I hope you can help me with that.




M1
Well, I will see what I can do. First, please let me ask a few questions. When did you come to Australia and what visa are you on?





E2
我 2008 年 2 月持 573 学生签证来悉尼大学读传媒学的硕士。
I came to Australia in February 2008 on a student visa subclass 573. I came here to do a master’s degree majoring in media at the University of Sydney.




M2
So on what grounds are you applying for a protection visa then? You should be aware that you must be a refugee as defined by the Refugee Convention.





E3
我在中国时是个记者，来澳以后在博客上写了很多批评中国政府的文章。我担心回国以后会因政治原因受到迫害。
I was a journalist in China. After I came here I wrote many articles on my blog, criticising the Chinese government. I’m worried that when I go back there I will be persecuted for political reasons.




M3
How can you be sure you will be persecuted? Publishing some articles alone seems insufficient to support the claim that you will be in danger back home.





E4
我的家人已经被监视了！我现在已不敢和他们联系。我一个同事因为发表一些揭露政府丑闻的文章已失踪了。
My family has been under surveillance! I’m too afraid to contact them now. One of my colleagues has gone missing for publishing some articles exposing the government scandals.




M4
You should include documentary evidence to support your claims in your application. If the documents are not written in English, you will need to have them translated by an accredited translator. It’s strongly recommended that you seek legal advice before lodging the application.





E5
如果我的签证批准了，我可以把国内的父母接来澳洲吗？因为我会不可能回国了。
If my visa application is approved, can my parents come to Australia to join me as I will never get the chance to go back to my country?




M5
You can sponsor your immediate family, such as your spouse and children to come to Australia when you are granted with the protection visa. However parents are not included. You can choose to apply through the parent migration program after you have had the protection visa for over two years.





E6
那如果我的申请不被批准会怎样？我能够有上诉重申吗？要知道， 我要是被捕了，有可能会受到睡眠剥夺等非人道的折磨。
What if my application is rejected? Can I have my case reviewed? You know, if I am arrested, I may face various kinds of inhumane torture such




M6
Yes. You have the right to seek a review of this decision by an independent tribunal, either the Refugee Review Tribunal (RRT) or the Administrative Appeals Tribunal (AAT).
as sleep deprivation.
