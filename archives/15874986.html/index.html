看车试驾

Well, we can go outside and you can have a good look at this one. It has auto transmission, only the colour is not red. Everything else is exactly the same as the one you ordered. You can even take it out for a test drive if you wish.

Original
Translated





E1
Well, we can go outside and you can have a good look at this one. It has auto transmission, only the colour is not red. Everything else is exactly the same as the one you ordered. You can even take it out for a test drive if you wish.
这样，我们可以到外面来，您可以好好的看看这款车。只是颜色不是红色的除此之外，其他都一模一样。甚至您喜欢的话，还可以开出 去兜兜风作为试驾。




M1
那可太好了，我都等不及想开车体验一下了。等等，对不起，我刚才出 门比较急，好像忘记带驾照了。
That’s fantastic. I can’t wait to drive it and try it out. Wait a minute, I’m sorry. I forgot to bring my driver’s license as I was in such a rush when I left home.




E2
That’s alright. You can come back anytime this week. Can I ask you about finance? The cash price is going to be somewhere in the region of seventeen thousand. How would you like to pay? Are you in a position to pay cash, or would you need credit?
没关系。本周之内你可以随时再过来。能否让我问一下费用的问 题？现金购买的价格大概会在 1 万 7 这个区间左右。您想通过什么方式付款呢？您是想付现金呢？还是信用支付?




M2
我想通过信用卡分期支付。我刚支付了房子的首付，所以目前手头有点紧。 I’d like to pay by credit in instalments. I am a little bit short of money as I just recently paid the down payment for a property.
27




E3
Well, you can discuss that with my colleague in a moment; we have various methods of payment. Do you have a trade-in? I mean, would you be interested in us taking your present car as part exchange?
好吧，您可以稍后和我的同事一起商量一下这个问题。我们有很多种付款方式。您有没有要冲抵的车呢？我是指，您是否有兴趣把您现在 的车卖给我们冲抵一部分费用?




M3
是的。但我得知道你们是怎么估价的。只有我认为你们的估价是公平的，你们才能拿走它。否则，我们就自己找个车行把它卖掉。
Yes. But I need to know how you will value it. You can take it only if I think your valuation is fair. Otherwise, we will find a dealer ourselves and sell it.




E4
Ok, fine. So I will just need some personal details from you and then we can do a valuation. Please give me your full name, address, postcode and a daytime phone number.
好的，没问题。所以我需要您提供一些个人信息，然后我们会去 做评估。请告诉我您的姓名、地址、邮编和白天的联系电话。




M4
我名字是潘阳。住在 Norman 区，James Street 17 号，邮编 2234。我白天的联系电话是 9688 7601。这个号码只是留言的，我会尽快给你们回电话的。这样可以吗？
My name is Yang Pan, I am living at 17 James Street, Norman, postcode 2234, and my daytime phone number is 9688 7601. This number is just for messages and then I will get back to you as soon as I can. Is that ok?




E5
That’s fine. And how about the car you have now, what make is it? And do you know the year of production or the model name? And the mileage roughly? What colour is it?
没关系。关于您现在的那辆车，是哪家制造商生产的？您知道它的 出产年份或者型号名吗？还有大概的使用里程是多少？什么颜色的?




M5
是辆本田车。我想生产年份应该是 2006 年，型号是 Puma. 我不大确定里程数了，但我知道是少于 7 万公里的。灰色的，是金属灰。
It’s a Honda. I think the year of production is 2006 and it’s called a Puma. I am not sure about the mileage. But I know it is less than seventy thousand. And the colour is gray, metallic gray.




E6
Right, and one last thing – what sort of condition would you say it is in?
好的。最后一个问题，您觉得它目前的使用状况如何?




M6
我大概会说它现在用着还行。我手机里有几张我的车的照片，昨天刚拍的。你需要看看吗？
I’d probably describe it as reasonable. I have a few photos of that car in my phone which I took yesterday. Do you want to see?
