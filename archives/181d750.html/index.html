  1. 1. 重点:

操心妈妈

相遇
23-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



A head-teacher is talking to Mrs. Li, the mother of a student called John, regarding John’s recent changes.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good afternoon. You must be Mrs. Li, John’s mother. My name is Adam Taylor. How are you today?
下午好。你一定是约翰的母亲李太太。我叫亚当·泰勒。你今天好吗？




M1
你好， 老师。 我儿子最近整天做白日梦， 快把我急死了。
Hi teacher. My son has been daydreaming all day recently, it’s getting on my nerves.




E2
Well, I’m surprised to hear that John is having a problem. He is a brilliant boy, and has been doing quite well at school. What exactly do you mean by daydreaming?
嗯，我很惊讶听到约翰有问题。他是个聪明的孩子，在学校一直表现很好。你说的白日做梦到底是什么意思？




M2
我们花了那么多钱和精力送他进了这里最好的私立小学。 一开始他还好好地， 每天认真学习。 那是从上个月， 他就变了个人似的。 有许多不切实际的想法。
We spent a lot of money and effort sending him to the best privagte primary school. At first he was quite Ok, studying hard every day but since last month, he seems to have become another person and has many unrealistic thoughts.




E3
You said that John has changed a lot since last month. Were there any unusual incidents that led to these changes? Also, what kind of changes are we talking about here?
你说约翰上个月以来变化很大。有没有什么不寻常的事件导致了这些变化，我们在这里讨论的是什么样的变化?




M3
他先是一直说自己想成为一个足球明星。 然后这周又开始说自己想做一个歌星。 我又不知道下周他得蹦出什么个奇怪的想法了。
He first said he wanted to become a football star. But this week he said he wanted to become a singer. I’m not sure waht strange thoughts he will have next week.




E4
I see. Please don’t be too worried. Small children are so imaginative that they are bound to have many thoughts. I think it’s a good sign in some way. It shows that John is an enthusiastic little boy..
我知道了。请不要太担心。小孩子很有想象力，因此他们必然会有很多想法。我认为这在某种程度上是一个好兆头。它表明约翰是一个热情的小男孩。




M4
你这么说好像也有道理， 不过， 他这么小小年纪的， 就总想当明星， 想出名， 我也不知道他从哪里学来那么多稀奇古怪的东西。
What you just said seems to make sense, However, he always wants to be a star and be famous at such a young age. I don’t know where such strange thoughs come from.




E5
Children today are exposed to all kinds of information, good and bad, so parents should be aware of what information their kids are receiving. He’s at a formative age, where he is trying to discover his interests and aptitudes.
现在的小朋友能接触到很多不同的信息， 无论是好的还是不好的。 所以父母应该了解他们的还在就收到了什么信息。 你儿子正处于性格形成的年龄， 这个年龄的还则回去尝试发现他们自己的兴趣和才能。




M5
天资和兴趣？ 请告诉我， 我怎么才能更好的知道我儿子的天资和兴趣呢？
Aptitudes and interests? Please tell me how I can get a better idea of my son’s aptitudes and interests?




E6
There are many ways. Pay attention to what subjects John likes the most, what he enjoys doing after school, and what he likes talking about at home.
有很多方法。注意约翰最喜欢的科目，他喜欢放学后做什么，他喜欢在家里谈论什么。




M6
跟你谈完之后， 我感觉我可能确实是小题大做了。 我不是很了解儿童的心里， 所以孩子的一点变化就让我感到恐慌。 现在我明白一些了。 谢谢你， 老师。
after talking to you, I feel I was making too big  deal out of it. Since I don’t know much about children’s minds, a slight change can have me panicking. Now I have a better understanding. Thank you, Mr Taylor.
