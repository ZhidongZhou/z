入室砸抢

相·遇
31-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a conversation between Superintendent Watson and Mr Wu, who has just experienced a home burglary.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Hello Mr Wu, now we have an interpreter with us and we can understand each other. So please tell me what happened.
22




M1
你好警官，是这样的，我今天早上一起来，发现我家里被人进来过了。我的 电视机被人碎了，我才刚买 2 天啊，56 寸的最新 LED 电视就这么没了。你 一定要帮我找到这个混蛋啊。
Hello officer. This is what happened: when I woke up this morning, I found somebody had broken into my house. My TV was smashed. I’d just bought this latest 56’’ TV two days ago but now it’s broken! Please help me find the bastards!




E2
Well. Please tell me more details. Otherwise I’m afraid we won’t be able to help you. Please tell me when exactly you noticed you’d been burgled, and is there any other damage?
31




M2
我是早上 8 点起来，要去厕所刷牙，可当我路过客厅的时候发现有什么不对 劲。当我看见我新买的电视就这么被人砸了，我差点没晕倒。哪个混蛋做的 啊！除了电视，我发现我新买的 Ipad mini 还有所配套的配件都被人卷跑了。
I got up at 8 am and I found something strange when I walked through the living room as I was going to the bathroom to brush my teeth. The view of my new TV smashed almost made me faint. Besides the TV, my new iPad Mini and its accessories have all been stolen too.




E3
Well. That’s terrible. You have my sympathy. However, it seems like the burglar was targeting at your new home entertainment system. Does anyone know that youhave recently bought those things?
31




M3
我想很多人应该都知道吧。我一买回来安装好就拍照片上传到各种社交网络 上了，比如说推特，脸书还有微博。我当时很开心，就想和亲戚朋友分享一 下快乐，没想到这居然引来了犯罪。真是太倒霉了。
I think many people know about it. I set everything up as soon as I brought them home, then I uploaded photos to social media including Twitter, Facebook and Weibo. I was happy and I wanted to share this happiness with my friends and relatives. I didn’t expect it would entice a crime. What bad luck!




E4
Well. Then I believe there must be some sort of association between this and your posts on your social networking sites. We have noticed some new crimes, which are a result of people mistakenly showing their location information and wealth.
38




M4
是么。我没想到这种事情会发生在我身上。我并不知道我发送的图片会包涵 了我的地理信息。不过算了，现在我打算把那些东西都关掉了。你能帮我查 查么，录入指纹什么的，搞不好就是住在周围的哪个小毛贼干的。
Yes. I never thought this would happen to me. I didn’t know the photos I posted included my location. But never mind. I’m going to turn off these functions. Could you help me check things, maybe take fingerprints or something? It might have be done by a burglar who lives around my area.




E5
Sure, our constable is recording fingerprints now. Please be patient and we will give you a report when we finish. Could you please give me your photo ID? We want to keep it on record.
36




M5
好的，给你，这是我的驾照。请有任何消息第一时间通知我。谢谢。
Okay, here you are. This is my driver’s licence. Please inform me immediately if anything comes up. Thank you.
