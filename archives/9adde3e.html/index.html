照料津贴

相遇
07-06-2020
Hits

CCL 

        

        



Mr Gao, a Mandarin-speaking man, has come to a local Centrelink office. He has asked to speak to a Centrelink officer about applying for carer payments.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning. Please take a seat. Before we start could I confirm your CRN (Client Reference Number) and your name please?
18




M1
我上个星期才拿到卡，号码是 524 094 104K.我的名字是 Qiang Gao.
I only got the card last week. The number is 524 094 104K. My name is Qiang Gao.




E2
Cheers, Mr. Gao. So, what can I help you with today?
11




M2
是关于我的孙子，他叫查斯特，今年 12 岁了。他有唐氏综合症和智障。因为 我要全职的照顾他所以我没办法工作。最糟糕的是，我的太太最近又丢了工 作。如果 Centrelink 可以给我们一些帮助我们会很感激的。
It’s about my grandson, Chester. He is 12 years old. He has Down’s syndrome and an intellectual disability. Since I provide full-time care for him I am not able to work. The worst thing is, my wife has lost her job. We would be extremely grateful for any kind of assistance that Centrelink can give us.




E3
Well you may be eligible for Carer Payment, and I see on your file that you and your grandson are both permanent residents. So, may I ask, when did you first arrive and settle in Australia?
36




M3
我们是 2005 年一月从中国移民过来的。那年七月，我们全家搬到墨尔本边上 的一个小镇上，然后就一直住到现在。
We migrated here from China in January 2005. Then in July the same year, my family and I moved to a small town near Melbourne. We have been living there since then.




E4
The reason I asked about when you came to Australia is because to qualify for a Carer Payment both you and the person you are looking after must be Australian residents and they must have been living in the country for at least two years. Now, I can see this would not be a problem in your case.
58




M4
我想这份补助真的会帮到我们很大的忙。除了查斯特的医药费以外，我们还有 汽油费，每天开两个小时的车去特殊学校。您觉得我们每个月可以拿多少钱 呢？
I think this payment would help us a lot. On top of the cost of his medical expenses, there are also petrol expenses. We have to drive two hours every day to get to a special school. So how much money do you think we can get per month?




E5
Well I’m afraid I won’t be able to give you an exact figure today. The Carer Payment is income and assets tested, so individual payments do vary. I would advise you to register an ‘intent to claim’ form for the Carer Payment as soon as you can.
47




M5
那还有别的什么补助，我有可能符合资格领取吗？请你帮我查一下好吗？
Are there other kinds of payments that we may be eligible to receive? Could you find out for me?




E6
Now as part of the payment you will also receive a Pensioner Concession Card and Pharmaceutical Allowance. You may also be able to get a few other payments such as Rent Assistance and Utilities Allowance. And if you are eligible you will be notified automatically. You won’t need to apply for them yourself.
53




M6
嗯，那个太好啦。我这就会去填表。谢谢！
OK. I think I’ll go fill in the form right away. Thank you!
