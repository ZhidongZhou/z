虐待儿童

相遇
25-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




Mr. Wang, a parent, is being interviewed by an officer from the Department of Community Services about the welfare of Mr. Wang’s child.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Mr. Wang, we’ve called you in because we are concerned about your son. He has bruises on his legs. Can you tell us how he got them?
27




M1
我不清楚啊，我每天都很忙，平时都是他妈妈照顾他。我猜，一定是他在学校时摔了跤吧？
I have no idea. I am pretty busy every day. His mother is the one who is looking after him. I guess he must have had a fall at school.




E2
Well, it’s been reported that he has had these bruises before. If he’s fallen over that many times perhaps there’s something wrong with him that the doctor needs to check.
30




M2
我的儿子可没有问题，不需要医生检查。另外，是谁报告的？这是我们家里的问题，不管别人的事。
There’s nothing wrong with my son and he doesn’t need to see any doctor. Also, who reported this? It’s our own domestic affairs, none of other people’s business.




E3
It was reported by your son’s school, because they are worried it might be a matter of child abuse. Do you understand that in Australia physical punishment upon children is illegal?
31




M3
虐待儿童？他是我的儿子。他做错事的时候，我必须要管教一下他。我那是在教育他该如何尊重父母 。
Child abuse? He is my son and I have to discipline him if he misbehaves. I was teaching him how to show respect to his parents.




E4
Discipline is one thing but causing bodily harm to your child is not acceptable under the law. You may face some serious criminal consequences.
25




M4
没人可以告诉我怎么管教我的儿子。我必须让他知道好好学习很重要。如果现在读书不好，将来一辈子没出息。
No one can tell me how to discipline my son. I have to let him know the importance of studying hard. If he is not doing well in his studies, he will be a loser for the whole of his life.




E5
We are not questioning your authority over your son. It’s the way the punishment is given which is wrong under the law. If you cause bruising to your son, you can be charged.
33




M5
可我认为, 做为父亲就是要对儿子严厉一些。但是，你刚说的刑事后果具体是什么意思？
But I think as his father, it’s my responsibility to raise my son strictly. What do you mean exactly by criminal consequences which you just mentioned?




E6
You could be charged with assault or causing actual bodily harm and if found guilty, sent to jail. At the very least, your son will be taken into care if you continue to abuse him like this.
37




M6
这么严重啊？那我以后注意。我当然不希望儿子被别人带走。
20
