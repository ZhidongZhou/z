反复补牙

相遇
18-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




A woman is visiting a dentist.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
医生您好，我的牙龈总是出血，口腔还发出一种臭味，这种情况已经差不多一年了。 Hello. My gums are always bleeding and I have bad breath. It has been like this for almost a year.
Hello. My gums are always bleeding and I have bad breath. It has been like this for almost a year.




M1
Does the bleeding occur by itself or is it induced by some irritation? That is to say, do your gums bleed when you brush your teeth or when you eat or do they bleed for no apparent reason at all?
40




E2
不知道，我只知道牙龈流血。还有，我后面的牙有个洞，我补的牙里面的填充物掉出来了。所以牙又开始疼了。 (I don’t know, I just know that my gums have been bleeding. Also, one of my back teeth has a cavity. The filling in it fell out so I have a toothache again.
I don’t know, I just know that my gums have been bleeding. Also, one of my back teeth has a cavity. The filling in it fell out so I have a toothache again.




M2
Let me have a look. Please sit in the chair. Lean your head back against the chair. It is a deep cavity. We’ll have an x-ray taken of that tooth just to make sure about the apex. If it is in good condition, I will treat it.
48




E3
这颗牙还可以再补吗？如果不能补, 我可真的没辙了。近来每天晚上，我都疼得睡不好觉。 (Can it be filled again? I don’t know what to do if it can’t be filled. I haven’t been sleeping well due to the pain.
Can it be filled again? I don’t know what to do if it can’t be filled. I haven’t been sleeping well due to the pain.




M3
Yes, it can be filled. I’ll clean it now. Please let me know if it hurts. Bite please. Now please open your mouth again. Do you feel anything abnormal when you clench your teeth?
34




E4
感觉不舒服，而且我吃冷的或热的东西，牙就疼得更厉害了。晚上的时候疼痛也会加重。即使我有时候吃止痛药，也没多大作用。
I’m feeling uncomfortable and the pain gets worse when I eat cold or hot things. And it’s more serious during the night. Even though I sometimes take painkillers, it doesn’t help much.




M4
From my observations, I’d say you have inflammation of the gums as well as pulpitis. We’ll have an x-ray done as soon as possible to see whether there are any wisdom teeth that need to be extracted.
35




E5
我之前没有做过牙齿的 X 光检查，会疼吗？另外，这个费用能用政府的保健医疗卡报销吗？
I’ve never had a dental x-ray before. Does it hurt? By the way, is it covered by Medicare?




M5
I’m afraid not. If you have private health insurance, you may be entitled to claim some of the money back. I am not an expert though, you probably need to consult your private health insurance provider.
36




E6
我没有私人保险，不过现在申请可定也来不及了，看来这次看牙将会是一笔很大的支出了。不管怎么说，谢谢您医生。
I don’t have any private health insurance but it is now too late to apply. It seems that this time this dental problem will cost me a lot. But anyway, thank you, doctor.




M6
You’re welcome. I’ll get my secretary to make another appointment for you.
12
