预定航班

相遇
20-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This  dialogue  takes  place  between  a  travel  agent  at  Asian  Explorer  Holidays  and  a  Mandarin speaking client, who wants to book a business trip to Hong Kong.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Mr. Wu. Thanks for dropping in. Please take a seat.
12




M1
早上好，Jacob 小姐。很高兴昨天给我打电话。我想不明白要提前这么长的时间来预订去香港的航班。
Good morning, Mr Jacob. I’m very glad you called yesterday. I don’t know why I have to book a flight to Hong Kong so far in advance.




E2
Hong Kong is always very popular with holiday-makers and January is the peak season. When exactly would you like to travel and which class of seating would you like?
29




M2
我想订商务仓。我不喜欢太挤了。让我看看。会议是一月 11 号开始，但我想有几天的时间来买东西，见一见朋友。一月 8 号星期五有没有空位啊？
I would like Business class. I like to have more personal space. Let me see… The meeting starts on the 11th of January but I’d like to have a few days to shop and meet friends. Do you have any seats available on Friday the 8th of January?




E3
That’s fine. There are a couple of flights with seats available on the 8th. You could fly direct, leaving at 10 in the morning or go via Melbourne, leaving here at 1 in the afternoon.
35




M3
我想我愿意直飞。我不喜欢在机场里等待。随便哪家航空公司都行，只要不是中国南方航空的就行，他们的服务太烂了。
I’d like to fly direct! I don’t like waiting at the airport. Any airline is fine with me as long as it’s not China Southern. Their service is terrible!




E4
I’d recommend Cathay Pacific as it is a Hong Kong owned airline. I will just key this into the computer. Now, Mr. Wu, can I please have your first name and the initials of any other names, if you have them?
41




M4
国泰不错的，我以前坐过的。我的个人名字是 Ming。我没有其他的字首缩写。
Cathay Pacific is pretty good. I have flown with them before. My first name is Ming and I don’t have any other names.




E5
Ming Wu; Cathay Pacific, flight CX131 to Hong Kong departing Sydney, on the 8th of January 2012, at 9.15 am. That’s all in order. Now, how would you like to pay?
31




M5
信用卡吧。对了，如果我要改变行程，我是跟你联系还是跟航空公司联系呢？需不需要收取费用呢？
By Credit card, please. By the way, if I need to change my itinerary, should I contact you or the airline? Are there any fees involved?




E6
You can ring the airline directly to make changes to your itinerary. You need to tell them your Booking Reference Number. They won’t charge you if there are seats available in business class.
33




M6
我明白了。多谢你的帮助。
I see. Thanks for your help.
