工伤复查

相遇
12-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




A doctor is interviewing a patient, Mr. Xue, about an injury he received as a result of a work accident.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Mr. Xue. Thanks for coming. I will ask you some questions about the accident and then we will examine your eyes. How is your right eye?
28




M1
感觉涩涩的。不舒服。您刚才一开灯我的右眼就感觉到疼痛。我现在对强光特别敏感。
It’s dry and it feels uncomfortable. My right eye started to hurt as soon as you turned on the light. I am now especially sensitive to bright light.




E2
I understand. From the file I can see that you were a gyprocker and you got injured on the 18th of March this year while you were working. Is that right?
31




M2
对。我当时正在一块金属板上钻洞突然间就感觉到一个很尖的东西飞进了眼睛里。可能是金属碎屑。我疼痛难忍我的同事马上送我去了医院。
Yes. I was drilling holes on a metal sheet and suddenly something very sharp got into my eye. It could have been a piece of metal. The pain was killing me and my co-workers immediately took me to the hospital.




E3
According to the file here, you stayed in the hospital for about two weeks. You had two operations after the injury. Have you gone back to the hospital or the specialist since you were discharged from the hospital? Are you on any medication now?
44




M3
出院后我还没有回过医院也没有去看过眼科医生。但是医生要我九月份的时候回去进行复查。
I haven’t gone back to the hospital or seen an ophthalmologist after being discharged. But the doctor asked me to see him again in September.




E4
I see. I will have a look at your right eye in a second. Before we do so, can you please tell me all your concerns about your right eye and how it affects your daily life?
37




M4
如果看到强光，眼睛就会疼，最主要的问题就是我看东西都是重影的，看东西都很模糊。眼睛有问题对我的生活有很大的影响。
It’s painful whenever I see bright light and the main problem is I have double and blurred vision. This eye problem has caused me a lot of trouble in my daily life.




E5
Can you give some examples, like what things you were able to do but can no longer do since the injury?
19




M5
我现在已经不能再回到原来的地方工作了，而且也不能开车，害怕出危险。两只眼睛经常涩涩的，还经常发红。
I can’t go back to my previous job anymore and I’m not able to drive anymore because I am afraid of having a car accident. My eyes are often dry and they often turn red.




E6
Thanks Mr. Xue. Please follow me to another room and I’ll examine your eyes. We should be finished very soon.
20




M6
好的，谢谢你，医生。
Alright. Thanks Doctor.
