A worker’s compensation solicitor is speaking with Mr. Li about his case.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Mr Li. I’ve asked you to come in because your worker’s compensation case is being heard next week. You have a conference with the barrister on Monday, but before you see her, I’d like to check some information about your accident.
李先生，早上好。我已要求您参加，因为下周将审理您的工人的赔偿案。您星期一与大律师举行会议，但是在您见到大律师之前，我想查看一些有关您的事故的信息。




M1
好的， 不过事情已经过去一年多了， 我不一定记得所有的细节了。 法院也算是认真的， 拖了这么久才开庭。
Ok, but it’s been more than one year ago, So I may not be able to remember all the details. The court has delayed the hearing for so long.




E2
Not to worry. I’ve got your file here, so we can use that to refresh your memory. It says here that the accident at CMB Manufacturing happened in August 2008. Can you tell me how long you’d been working there before you had the accident?
不要担心。我这里有你的档案，我们可以用来帮助你回忆。 这里说事故发生于2008年8月在CMB 制造厂。 你能告诉我在发生事故之前你在那里工作了多长时间吗？




M2
我之前在另一家公司工作， 后来我回国了， 因为我母亲生病了， 那好像是 ··· 我想想 ··· 2007 年底。 我大约在2008年3月开始在CMB工作。
I used to work for another company, afterwards I went back to China because my mother was sick. That was … let me see … at the end of 2007. I started working for CMB in March 2008.




E3
you’d been at CMB for about five months. Now, can you tell me exactly what you were doing when the accident happened
您已经在CMB待了五个月。现在，您能确切告诉我事故发生时您在做什么吗？




M3
当时我正在给金属圆筒钻孔。 有个叫Frank的同事走了过来， 开始开始跟我说话。
I was drilling on a metal cylinder. A workmate called Frank came and began talking to me. He is kind of person like making joke. He did this to everyone. Sometimes, he did stupid things.




E4
It would be better to restrict yourself to describing exactly what happened on the day in question. So Frank came over to talk to you. What happened then.
你最好限制自己只是描述当天发生的事。 所以 Frank 过来跟你讲话。 然后发生了什么？




M4
他评论了几句我身上的T恤。 然后他开始挠我的痒。 我很怕痒的， 所以我挥动手臂来制止他。 我拿着钻头的手打到了墙壁。 我觉得很疼， 手一松， 钻头就掉在我的脚上。
He made some comments about the T-shirt I was wearing before tickling me. I am quite ticklish so I waved my arm to stop him. My hand that was holding the drill hit the wall and it was very painful so I lost my grip and the drill fell on my foot.




E5
Then what happened?
然后发生了什么？




M5
经理来了， 他叫了救护车。 急诊室的医生清理了伤口， 但是几周之后不得不动了手术， 因为有些神经和肌腱损伤了。 我现在还在做理疗。
The manager came and called the ambulance. The doctor in the emergency room cleaned up the wound. but I had to have surgery a few weeks later because some nerves and tendons were injured. I am still taking physiotherapy (理疗).




E6
Fine. You’ll probably be appearing as a witness, which means you could be cross-examined by CMB’s barrister. Unless, of course, we reach an out-of-court settlement. Now, are there any question?
好的。您可能会作为证人出庭，这意味着您可能会被CMB的大律师交叉盘问。当然，除非我们达成庭外和解。现在，有什么问题吗？




M6
没问题了， 非常感谢您。
No, Thank you very much.







重点:





拓展：
Cross examination by prosecution
