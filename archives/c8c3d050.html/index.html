戒烟专线

相·遇
14-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between Mr. Bennett, a health advisor from I-Can-Quit NSW, and Mr. Lin, who is planning to quit smoking.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Mr Lin. I’m glad to see you here today. How can I help you?
16




M1

———————————————————————————————————————–
 早上好，Bennett 先生。我太太劝我戒烟，因为我们打算要宝宝了。所以，我今天过来寻求一些专业的建议。		
Good morning, Mr. Bennett. My wife is urging me to quit smoking as we’re planning to have a baby. So I’m here today to get some professional advice.




E2
That’s good to hear. There will be many benefits to you if you quit. Tell me about your smoking; how long have you smoked.”
24




M2
我今年 30 岁了。但我刚 20 岁出头的时候，就开始吸烟了。一开始，我就是觉得吸烟是一件很酷的事，但没想到会上瘾。
I’m 30, but I started smoking in my early twenties. In the beginning, I was only doing it as I thought it was cool. But I didn’t expect that I would get addicted.




E3
Well, that’s often the case for many young adults who smoke. They are tempted to try smoking for fun without thinking much about the severe consequences, until one day they find it’s impossible to stop.
35




M3
对，就是这样。我现在呢，每天都至少要抽一整包的烟。我太太总抱怨说我就像个烟囱似的。
Yes, exactly. Now I have to smoke at least one pack a day. My wife often complainsthat I smoke like a chimney.




E4
If you are already aware of the health risks associated with smoking, you should also understand that kicking this bad habit is not just good for you but for those around you.
32




M4
是的，我懂。其实，我很早以前就想戒烟了。我甚至自己都尝试过几次，但所有的努力最终还是失败了。
Yes. I do. Actually I’ve been wanting to stop for a long time. I have even tried a few times by myself, but all my efforts ended in failure.)Yes. I do. Actually I’ve been wanting to stop for a long time. I have even tried a few times by myself, but all my efforts ended in failure.)Yes. I do. Actually I’ve been wanting to stop for a long time. I have even tried a few times by myself, but all my efforts ended in failure.)Yes. I do. Actually I’ve been wanting to stop for a long time. I have even tried a few times by myself, but all my efforts ended in failure.)




E5
Don’t worry. We are here to provide you with professional guidance. We’ll make a plan together and support you with follow-up calls and consultations, until you are able to make a positive change.
33




M5
但我还是没有信心。我怕我抵抗不了烟瘾。之前我尝试戒烟的时候，整天都头痛、恶心，吃饭也没有胃口。太难受了！
But I still have no confidence. I’m afraid I won’t be able to resist the cravings. I was suffering from headaches, nausea and a poor appetite all day long last time I tried to stop smoking. It’s awful!




E6
Those are common withdrawal symptoms caused by the addiction to nicotine. I believe with your willpower and our tailor-made program, you will win this battle eventually.
26




M6
那好吧。希望这次真的可以戒烟成功。那就请您为我制定合适的计划吧。
Alright. I hope I can really quite for good this time. So please make a suitable plan for me.
