签证拒签

相遇
04-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue is between Mr. Lu, and Henry, an immigration officer. Mr. Lu is enquiring about the visa rejection decision on behalf of his family.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good afternoon, Mr. Lu. I understand that you have come in to clarify some matters regarding your family’s visa applications.





M1
是的，没错。我们刚受到了你们移民局发来的签证拒签通知。我们很震惊，也很失望。
Yes, that’s right. We have just received the visa rejection notice from you. We feel shocked and very disappointed.




E2
I’m sorry to hear that. However, we cannot comment on the rejection of their applications apart from the information contained in the decision letter.





M2
我知道，你之前就说过了。今天我想问的是移民部是否能重新考虑这个案子。我赚钱养国内的家人。我很久没见到他们了。
I know, you told me that already. What I’m asking you is whether your department can reconsider the case. I am supporting my family back in China. I haven’t seen them for a long time.




E3
That’s really quite unfortunate. However, all we can do is to consider applications, then accept or refuse them. One path for you would be to encourage your family to reapply.





M3
好吧，那他们重新申请了会怎样？会有什么不同吗？他们第一次申请时就满足了所有要求，但你们还是拒绝批签。
Ok and what if they reapply? Will it make any difference? They met all the requirements in the first application but you still refused to grant them the visa.




E4
I have already said that I can’t comment. I appreciate your circumstances and I sincerely wish that I could help. Believe me, I meet people in your position every day and I have nothing but sympathy for them.





M4
我不是对你无礼，但我觉得你有点不讲道理。一开始你告诉我你们部门可能有失误，然后你又说我家人的文件有问题。你也不告诉我是哪些问题。
Look, I don’t want to be rude but I think you are being a bit unreasonable. First you told me your department might have made a mistake. Then you said that there are some problems with their documents. But you won’t tell me what these problems are.




E5
No, no. Let me make this clear. With respect, Mr. Lu, I think you have the facts a little distorted. I said that I suspected that there had been some problems with the documentation. If you want to keep on arguing, this interview is a waste of your time and mine.





M5
好，那我冷静一下。但他们在家等移民部的回复已经浪费了几个月了。想想他们吧。你让他们建立起很高的期望，然后又让这期望破灭。至少给我们一个机会吧。
Fine. I’ll calm down, but my family have wasted months sitting around waiting for a reply from your department. Please think of them. You built up their expectations and then you destroyed them. Give us a chance at least.




E6
Nobody’s expectations were built up. They should not have made any assumptions until their visas were granted.





M6
我要拿头撞墙了。给我个直截了当的答案，是还是否。他们重新申请签证到底有没有意义？你一定能给我点有用的信息。
Oh, come on! Just give me a straight answer – yes or no. Is there any point in them reapplying for a visa? Surely you can give me some idea.
