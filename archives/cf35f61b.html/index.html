车险咨询

ZDZ
31-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between Adam, a staff member at the RTA and Mr
Wang, who wants to buy a car and an insurance policy. * * * | ID |
Original | Translated | | --- | --- | --- | | E1 | Hello. My name is
Adam. How can I help you today. | 你好。我叫亚当。今天我能为您做些什么?
| | M1 |
你好。是这样的，我今天来想咨询一下我能否在这个国家开车。因为我想买
一个二手车。所以我还想知道一下，我需要上怎样的保险 | Hello. I’m here
today to inquire about whether I can drive in Australia. I want to buy a
used car, so I’d like to know what kind of insurance policy I need to
take out. | | E2 | Sure, I can assist you with these matters. But let’s
deal with them one at a time. First, can I have your photo ID please?
Are you a visitor or a permanent resident | 33 | | M2 |
好的。给你，这是我的护照。我还不是永久居民。我朋友告诉我说我只能用
我国内的驾照在这里开 3 个月，在这 3
个月之内我必须转换成澳洲驾照才能继续在新南威尔士州开车，对么？ | Okay.
Here you are. This is my passport. I’m not a permanent resident yet. My
friend told me I can drive here with my Chinese driver’s license for
only three months. Then I have to convert it into an Australian license
after that, right? | | E3 | No, since you are not a permanent resident
of NSW, you can still drive with your overseas licence until you become
a permanent resident or citizen of this country. So you don't need to
worry about that at this stage. Now, what do you want to know about car
insurance | 50 | | M3 |
那太好了！我来这里发现很多郊区的公共交通并不是很好，如果不开车还真
麻烦，所以我打算买一个二手车，但是我朋友告诉我，我还得买一个强制的
保险？我发现保险有很多种类，不知道怎么选 | That’s great! I’ve found that
public transport in many suburbs is quite poor and it’s very
inconvenient if you don’t have a car. That’s why I’m planning to buy a
second hand car. But my friend said I have to buy compulsory insurance.
I’ve found there are many types of insurance and I don’t know what to
choose. | | E4 | Yes, you need Compulsory Third Party insurance, which
is also known as a Green Slip. This is a prerequisite of registration
for every vehicle in Australia. Without it, you cannot renew your
vehicle’s registration, even if it is second hand. But in terms of other
types of insurance, they are not compulsory, but I strongly recommend
that you should at least have Third Party Property Insurance if you are
a new driver in this state. | 76 | | M4 |
就是这里我搞不太清楚，网上的一堆资料和广告都搞得我头晕眼花了。还有
那个第三方强制责任险和第三方财产保险，到底有什么区别？ | This is where I
got confused. I’m confused by the information and advertisements I found
on the internet. And what is the difference between the Compulsory Third
Party Insurance and the Third Party Property Insurance? | | E5 |
Compulsory Third Party insurance covers compensation for the life and
general health of others if there is an incident or accident due to your
negligence. Third Party Property Insurance is responsible for any damage
to another’s vehicle or public property. But please be aware that this
will not cover damage to your vehicle | 53 | | M5 |
哦，我明白了，看来我的确是有必要买一个第三方财产保险，要不然万一我
撞到一劳斯赖斯我就死定了。那我是否一买车就必须开始上保险呢，还是我
想什么时候买就什么时候买？ | Oh I see. It seems that the Third Party
Property Insurance is quite necessary, otherwise I’ll be in big trouble
if I crash into a Rolls Royce. So do I need to buy the policy as soon as
I buy the car, or can I buy it at any time I want?) | | E6 | You don't
have to buy the insurance unless you think it is necessary. But for the
CTP, it will automatically transfer from your previous driver's policy
to yours. So you don't have to buy it until your rego is expired | 40 |
| M6 |
哦，原来是这样啊。我明白了非常谢谢我回去研究一下。如果有什么不懂得
我再来找您。再见。 | I see. Thank you very much. I’ll go back and do
some research. If there is anything I don’t understand, I’ll come back
to you. Good bye. |
