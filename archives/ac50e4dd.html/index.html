钻脚出庭

相·遇
23-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mrs. Wang is being cross-examined at a NSW District Court hearing for a work-related injury.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
You swore yesterday to tell the truth and nothing but the truth. Lying under oath constitutes perjury and it could incriminate you.
你昨天发誓要讲真话，而且只讲真话。宣誓后说谎构成伪证罪，可能会使你受到牵连。




M1
没问题。 我是个守法和信奉宗教的人， 从来都说真话， 而且我已经当着法官、 律师和陪审团的面， 手按圣经发过誓了。
No problem. I am a law-abiding and religious person who always tells the truth. Also I have sworn on the bible before the judge, lawyers and jury.




E2
You said during examination-in-chief that on the 23rd of Aprial, 2009 you were drilling holes in some cylinders. Then you had accidently hit the workbench and you dropped the drill which landed on your right foot. Is that right?
您在总检查中说，2009年4月23日，您在一些钢瓶上钻孔。然后，您不小心碰到了工作台，钻头掉落了在你右脚上的， 是这样么？




M2
那一天呢， 有一批货到了， 外面的工人正在用叉车卸货， 然后， 他们把这些东西堆放到最高的架子上。
That day, a shipment just arrived. The workers outside were using the forklift to unload all the cargo. They then stacked on the highest shelf.




E3
According to your legal representative, you suffered excruciating pain in your right foot, Correct?
根据您的法律代表 / (律师)，您的右脚遭受了极大的痛苦对吗？




M3
不仅仅是砸到了我的脚上， 我的腰部和头部也受了伤。 急救人员把我送到了， 我的头部缝了五针。 大夫为我安排了康复计划。
Not only my foot was hit, but my back and head were also injured. The first aid people / (Paramedic) sent me to the hospital where I had five stitches on my head. The doctor arranged a rehabilitation program/ (recovering plan) for me.




E4
I put it to you that you have exaggerated your injuries in order to get more money for compensation. The specialists for the insurance company stated in their reports that you fully recovered and you could return to your normal duties.
我告诉你，你夸大了自己的受伤程度，为的是得到更多的赔偿。保险公司的专家在他们的报告中说，你已经完全康复，可以恢复正常工作了。




M4
虽然我的脚已经恢复， 但是腰部仍然痛的厉害。 X光照片显示， 我的椎间盘突出。
I Though my foot has recovered, my back still hurts badly. The X-ray shows that I have a protruded disc.




E5
I put to you that your back problem is due to pre-existing degenerative(退化) changes caused by age. I have evidence to prove that you’re as fit as a fiddle (非常健康；身体很好) now. A video shows you power-walking with your workmate in your suburb on the 8th of February 2010.
我告诉你，你的背部问题是由年龄引起的预先存在的退行性变化 (退化) 引起的。我有证据证明你现在身体很好。一段视频显示，2010年2月8日，你和你的同事在郊区快步走。




M5
在播放录像之前， 我必须说明一下这人的身份， 据称她长得很像我， 但你必须注意， 我有一个孪生姐姐， 她就住在我这条街上， 就在我隔壁。
Before playing the video I have to clarify the identity of this person. It is said that she looks very much like me. But you need to realize that I have a twin sister who lives on the same street, right next door to me.




E6
I haven’t seen any information regarding this person in the file. Can you please give me more details about your twin sister?
我在文件中没有看到有关此人的任何信息。你给我能提供更多关于你的双胞胎妹妹的详细信息吗？




M6
她叫王琳， 我们2004年一起来的澳洲。 她现住在我那条街的18号， 现在在一家超市做会计。
Her name is Wang Lin. We came here together in 2004. She lives in ID 18 on the same street as me and works as an accountant in a supermarket.
