入籍咨询

相遇
05-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue is between Mr. Guan and Allan, an immigration officer. Mr Guan is enquiring about applying for Australian citizenship.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
你好，我今天来是想咨询一下入籍的事。我有几个问题想向你请教。
Hello! I’m here today to enquire about citizenship. I’ve got some questions to ask you.




M1
Sure, I can help you with that. I’m Allan, a citizenship department officer. Please go ahead.





E2
我去年 2 月成为了永久居民。我现在在犹豫要不要入籍。你可以告诉我成为澳大利亚公民有哪些好处呢？
I became a permanent resident last February. Now I’m considering whether I should apply for citizenship. Can you tell me the benefits of becoming an Australian citizen?




M2
Australian citizenship offers a range of benefits. You can vote in federal and state or territory elections and in a referendum and seek election to parliament. You can also apply for work in the Australian Public Service or in the Australian Defence Force.





E3
同样，我也可以持有澳大利亚护照了吧。凭着这本护照，我可以免签去很多国家，对吗？我对于我来说是个比较实际的好处。
Also I can receive an Australian passport with which I can visit many countries visa-free, right? For me this is a real benefit.




M3
That’s correct. An Australian passport will enable you to visit over 100 countries on a visa-free basis. Also, you can receive help from Australian consular and embassy officials while overseas.





E4
嗯…那何时可以申请入籍呢？是当我成为永久居民满两年吗？还是有其他什么具体规定？
Hmm… Then when can I apply for Australian citizenship? Is it after I have been a permanent resident for two years or are there other conditions?




M4
Actually, you have to meet the residence requirements. This means you must have 4 years lawful residence in Australia. This period must include 12 months as a permanent resident immediately before making an application for Australian citizenship. Also, you must have absences from Australia of no more than 12 months in total in the 4 years prior to application, including not more than 90 days in the 12 months immediately prior to the application.





E5
除了我的身份证件以外，我还需要递交哪些文件呢？是否需要无犯罪记录证明呢？这个申请不会向永久居留的申请那样等那么久吧？
What documents should I submit besides my identification documents? Do I need to provide a certificate of no criminal record? Will the processing time of this application be as long as the application for permanent residency?




M5
Yes you need to include documents proving you are of good character. I can’t tell you the exact processing time but I think it should be much shorter than the processing time for permanent residency.





E6
最后我要参加入籍仪式对吧。你可以跟我说一下入籍仪式有哪些内容呢？
The final step of citizenship is to attend the citizenship ceremony, right? Could you tell me what will happen at the ceremony?




M6
After you are seated there will be a formal introduction, speeches and an address that precedes making the pledge. Then the presiding officer will ask you to repeat the pledge of commitment out loud. Once you have made the pledge you will be an Australian citizen.
