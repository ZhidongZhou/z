丧妻社福

相遇
11-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue is between Mr. Hu and Larry, a Centrelink customer service officer. Mr. Hu has recently lost his wife.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Mr. Hu. Please come in and have a seat. Now, how can I help you today?
17




M1
我的妻子最近因为乳腺癌去世了。我的朋友建议我来这里咨询一下，看看能否获得什么帮助。
My wife passed away of breast cancer recently. My friend recommended that I come here and make an enquiry about what kind of help I can get from you.




E2
I’m very sorry for your loss, Mr. Hu. Are you receiving any income support payments at the moment? If not, you may be entitled to a Bereavement Allowance.
29




M2
没有，我一直都在工作，开大巴，虽然工资不高，但还比较稳定。我妻子在世的时候有领疾病津贴，因为她身患癌症不能工作。
No. I have been working the whole time as a coach driver. The pay is not high but it’s stable. My wife was receiving a Sickness Allowance before she died because she was diagnosed with cancer and was unable to work.




E3
In that case, you should be eligible for the allowance but the amount you can get depends on your assets and income. I’m sure you have gone through these kinds of tests before.
33




M3
是的，但我只知道你们需要评估我的资产。但我还是有些疑问。比如，哪些资产是需要被评估的？我有一处投资的房产，这要算在内吗？
Yes, but I only knew that you would assess my assets. I still have some questions. For example, which assets should be included in this assessment? I have an investment property. Does it need to be included?




E4
Yes, we need to assess the value of your assets, which includes any money you have, any real estate you own other than your principle home and any motor vehicles you own. You can find the complete list on this fact sheet.
42




M4
那收入测试是怎样的呢？我现在一周大概能挣个六百来块钱。我在银行还有五万元的定期存款，每年能有大概三千元的利息。您觉得我满足收入测试的要求吗？
How does the income test work? I earn about 600 dollars a week now and I have a 50,000-dollar term deposit in the bank and it yields about 3,000 dollars annually. Do I meet the requirements of the income test?




E5
Well, I can’t give you a definite answer right now before the test is done. However, if your fortnightly income is less than $1647.60 you may be eligible for a part pension.
32




M5
其实钱有多少我无所谓。最大的痛苦是以后的人生我只能一个人度过了。我不知道我能否忍受这样的孤独。我要是跟我妻子一起去另一个世界就好了。
In fact, it doesn’t matter how much I can get. The most painful thing is that I have to live alone for the rest of my life. I don’t know if I can stand such loneliness. I wish I could have gone with my wife.




E6
You really have my sympathy Mr. Hu. I understand this is a really tough time for you. We can refer you to an expert grievance counsellor or other such services. I’m sure they can help you get through all this.
40




M6
好的，非常感谢你的热心帮助。等我准备好材料再来见你。
Ok. Thank you for your kind help. I will come back to see you when I have the documents ready.
