初诊肥胖

相遇
19-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between a doctor and a patient who has just been diagnosed with diabetes.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, thank you for coming in today. As I explained to you last time, you have been diagnosed with diabetes; Your body can’t produce enough insulin to break down the sugar in your body, so the sugar remains in your blood.





M1
但是我还是不明白，我怎么会有糖尿病哪？ 我不喜欢吃甜食，怎么会有那么多的糖在我体内哪？ 还有为什么我最近会觉得有些虚弱，疲倦，而且有时还会头晕？
42




E2
Well, how much sugar remains in your body has little to do with how much sweet food you eat. Basically, without enough insulin, your body can’t break down the sugars in food into a form it can absorb, so the cells are not getting enough energy, which is why you get tired.
But I still don’t understand. How come I have diabetes? I don’t like sweet food. Why is there so much sugar stored in my body? Also, why do I often feel weak, tired and sometimes dizzy?




M2
我明白了，那有什么方法可以完全治愈吗？有什么药物还是我必须要打针呢？ 我有一些朋友也有糖尿病，他们每天吃饭之前都要打针，真是太可怕了！
52




E3
You have been diagnosed with diabetes type 2. There is no cure for this type of diabetes at the moment. However, you can control it with medication and you will also have to follow a strict healthy diet. If you are careful enough with your diet and you also do exercise you shouldn’t need to have any injections.
I see. So is there any way to cure it? Is there any medicine for the disease or do I need the injection? Some of my friends have diabetes too and they have to take a shot before meals every day. It’s horrible!




M3
那我就放心了，那么在饮食上我都需要注意些什么呢？是不是一点甜的都不能吃啊？
55




E4
You need to eat food that has sugar but does not taste sweet at all, and which releases sugar into your body very slowly. And the “sugar” we are talking about here is not the sugar you use in cooking or put into your coffee.
I’m relieved to hear that. What do I need to pay attention to regarding my diet? I can’t eat anything sweet, right?




M4
那我怎么样才能知道我的血糖指数呢？ 每次都需要来医院检查吗？
45




E5
Not really, you can check it with a monitor that you can carry around. It is very easy to use.
So how can I find out what my blood sugar level is? Do I need to come to the hospital to check it every time?




M5
我最近总是感觉很累，所以基本上已经不怎么锻炼了，但是我以前经常打兵乓球和羽毛球，您说我是不是应该继续呢?
20




E6
Yes, of course. These exercises will keep you fit and help you control your diabetes. Just make sure you eat enough food before doing exercise so that your sugar levels wouldn’t get too low.
I always feel very tired lately, so basically I don’t do much exercise. But I used to play ping pong and badminton very often. Do you think I should take them up again?
