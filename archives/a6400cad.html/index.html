目击陈述

相遇
25-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue takes place between a policeman, Constable Stevenson and a Mandarin speaker, Mr. Lin. It concerns a recent robbery at Mr. Lin’s house.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
谢谢你这么快就来了。我请来了一位专业口译。我在电话里已经告诉警察我们所有的电器设备都被偷走了。
Thank you for coming at such short notice. I hired a professional interpreter. I told the police that all our electronic appliances were stolen.)




M1
No worries. Now if you can just describe what happened. From the look of things, there has been forced entry at the side window.
24




E2
是的是的。窗户上有保险锁。但我们认为小偷是用我太太放在车库里的一根高尔夫球棍砸开的，那根高尔夫球棍还躺在那呢。
Yes, yes. The window has safety locks. But we think the burglar used a golf club that my wife put in the garage to smash it open. The club is still lying on the floor over there.




M2
Right. We’ll have to take that away to check for finger prints. We will also check the door for finger prints. Now tell me when you first noticed something was wrong.
31




E3
我五点下班回家看到前门被打开了而且旁边的窗户被打碎了，我就去按邻居的 门铃，是跟邻居一起进来的。
?




M3
According to your description, it seems the burglar went in through the window and came out from the door. Have you checked what was taken?
25




E4
有录像机，光碟播放机和我太太的笔记本电脑。小偷知道他要拿什么，因为他根本没有碰像微波炉和首饰这样的东西。
A video recorder, CD-player and my wife’s laptop. The burglar knew what to take because they didn’t touch other stuff like the microwave or jewellery at all.




M4
Yes, that’s often the case. We’ll check with the neighbourhood watch but have you noticed anything lately or anyone suspicious in your street?
23




E5
没有 ，我想不起来有什么。也许你应该等一下，问问我太太。她常常白天在家里工作，或许可以提供什么线索。
No, nothing I can think of. Maybe you can wait for my wife to come back home. She often works at home during the day, so she may have some idea.




M5
Yes we’ll make a point of speaking to her as soon as possible. Where is she now by the way? Also, is this the first time you have been burgled like this?
32




E6
她可能是去买菜了。其实这是我们今年第三次被盗了。
Maybe she has gone grocery shopping. In fact, it is the third time we’ve been burgled this year.




M6
You should always report any break-ins. You’ll be surprised how many times a thief strikes one neighbourhood in a short period of time.
23
