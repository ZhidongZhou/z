结核检查

相遇
09-08-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




A patient comes to see the doctor about difficulties with breathing. The doctor asks questions about the patient’s condition, gives a diagnosis, orders some tests and prescribes medication.








ID
Original
Translated





M1
医生，这几天来我一直感觉很难受。我觉得呼吸困难，尤其是晚上的时候。咳嗽得也很厉害。
Doctor, over the last couple of days I’ve been feeling terrible. I find it hard to breathe, especially at night. I also have a terrible cough.




E1
That sounds no good. Do you also get wheezy or short of breath?
13




M2
是的。当我吸气的时候，就好像有人坐在我的胸脯上似的。我呼气的时候呼吸声也很大。
Yes. When I try to breathe in, it feels as if someone is sitting on my chest! Also, it’s very noisy when I breathe out.




E2
It sounds as if you might have a chest infection or it may be bronchitis. When you cough, do you bring up any sputum and if so, what colour is it?
31




M3
是的，咳嗽时会有很多痰，特别是在早上。颜色是黄绿色，很粘稠。什么是支气管炎呢？
Yes, there’s quite a lot of phlegm when I cough, especially in the mornings. It’s a greenish yellow colour and quite solid. What is bronchitis?




E3
Bronchitis is an inflammation of the bronchi in your lungs. If it’s that, we need to treat it quickly otherwise you may have long term complications.
26




M4
过去，我甲状腺出过问题。有可能跟这个有关吗？
I had a problem with my thyroid some time ago, could it be linked to that?




E4
No. But I need to do some blood tests to check what kind of an infection it is, and we can test your thyroid at the same time, just to be sure.
32




M5
噢，那不错。支气管炎真的会让我感觉病得这么严重么？
Oh, that is good. Can bronchitis really make me feel so sick?




E5
Yes. It could even be that you have a mild case of pneumonia but, either way, you need to start taking antibiotics to control the infection.
26




M6
我对盘尼西林/青霉素过敏，会起红疹。一年前，我足部有严重的感染，那时候我才发现的。
I am allergic to penicillin, I get a red rash. I found that out when I had a serious infection in my foot a year ago.




E6
Then I’ll make sure I prescribe antibiotics that don’t have penicillin in them. Make sure you take the full course of tablets. That’s very important.
25




M7
那好吧，医生。我会再做一个预约，下周来找你拿化验结果。
Alright then doctor. I’ll make an appointment to come and see you next week to get the results of the tests.
