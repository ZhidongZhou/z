120分， 14道题， 3小节， 2+1小时

You have 15 minutes reading time (as is traditional provided) and this assessment has been extended by 1 hour to be a 3hour timed assessment. This is to provide time required to utilise technologies and take into account possible network bandwidth issues. This timed assessment has been designed as a two hour exam, the total marks equals 120 marks, which has been designed to allocated 1 minutes per 1 mark. It is advised you allocate your time wisely and spend no more than 30minutes on the 30mark question and 1 minutes on a question worth 1 mark. But, yes you do have 1 extra hour, because we have moved this exam from an envigilated exam at the showground to online timed quiz in CANVAS!

Question 1 please provide your video authentication 0
