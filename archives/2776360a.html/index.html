  1. 1. 重点:

工作歧视

相·遇
09-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between an employee and a legal consultant about a workplace discrimination.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Mrs. Huang. I am the consultant taking care of your case. What can I do for you?
早上好，黄女士。我是你案件的顾问，有什么可以帮到您？




M1
律师, 你好。我来是想咨询一下关于歧视的问题我觉得我受到了不公正的待遇。
Hello. I‘m here to enquire about discrimination. I think I have been treated unfairly.




E2
I am sorry to hear that, Mrs. Huang Could you please give me some more details? Then we will see if the treatment has been unfair
很抱歉听到这些，黄女士。请问您能不能再给我一些细节，然后我们再来看看是否有不公平的待遇




M2
当然可以。我在一家公司做全职文秘。工作两年后， 我决定要小孩。合同上说我是有产假的。可是等我 休完产假回来，我的职位已经有人顶替了
Certainly. I am a full time secretary in a company. Having worked for two years, I decided to have a baby. According to the contract, I have (/mə’tɜːnɪtɪ/) Maternity Leave. But after returning from leave, my position had been taken by someone else.




E3
What happened then? Did you talk to your employer about it? How did your employer respond ？
接下来 发生了什么呢？您有没有跟您的雇主谈过这个事情呢？您的雇主是怎么回复的呢？




M3
我去找过经理。他说，我离开的那段时间，公司缺人手， 必须招新人。顶替我职位的人现在已经适应了这份工作， 不能让她走 .
I talked to the manager. He said the company was understaffed and had to recruit a new person during my leave. The new person has adapted well to the job so they don’t want to make her leave




E4
Then how was your problem solved? Did the  company dismiss you or was it settled in  another way?
您的问题后来是怎么解决的呢？您的公司解雇了您或者把您安排到其他的岗位呢？




M4
我现在还是可以回去上班，但是由全职改为兼职，  一周只有20小时。我的经济压力大。我现在这样的情况能算是工作场所的歧视行为吗？
I can still work there, but only part-time, not full-time, and only for 20 hours a  week. I am under a lot of financial pressure  now. Is this kind of situation considered to be  workplace discrimination?




E5
Unlawful workplace discrimination> occurs when an employer takes adverse action (不利的, 不好的行为) against a person who is an employee or prospective employee (准雇员;潜在雇员). It covers various conditions which include altering an employee’s position to their detriment (造成损害; ‘detrɪm(ə)nt/).
非法的工作场所歧视是指当雇主对雇员或者潜在雇员做出不好的行为。 这涵盖很多种情况， 包括将员工调整到对员工不利的岗位。




M5
这么说来，我受到的待遇的确是不公平的。可是我现在应该怎么办呢?  我又不能在公司闹，万一他们把我解雇怎么办？
So that means I have been subjected  to unfair treatment. But what should I do  now? I cannot make a scene at work. What if  they fire me?




E6
You do not have to take any aggressive action. All you need to do is apply for an investigation. If it is found that your employer has discriminatory practices that are linked to adverse actions for employees, The Fair Work Ombudsman may take enforcement action.
您不用采取任何的过激行为。您所要做的就是申请一个调查。如果发现您的雇主做出对雇员不利的并和歧视有关的行为，申诉专员可能采取强制措施。




M6
好的。那我就去申诉专员那里投诉，我要捍卫自己的权益。
Ok. I’ll make a complaint to the ombudsman. I would like to protect my rights and interests.
