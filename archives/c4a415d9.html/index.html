探亲签证

相·遇
07-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mrs. Lu is being interviewed by a member of the Migration Review Tribunal (MRT) regarding a rejected visa application.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
How are you, Mrs Lu? Good to see you today. Please have a seat.
14




M1
謝謝，您一定得幫我。 我帮我的弟弟申请了短期停留探访签证，但 是申请遭到了拒绝。我们现在很绝望。
Thank you. Please help me. I helped my brother to apply for a short stay visitor’s visa. But our application was rejected and we’re really desperate.




E2
Don’t be upset. Our job in the MRT is to review unfavourable decisions made by DIAC. However, in making its decisions, it must have regard to the relevant laws, and to government policy. Does that make sense?
37




M2
恩，我明白。我想我来对地方了。我认为移民局的拒签決定太不合 理了。我肯定，他们错了。
Yes, I understand. I think I have come to the right place. I think DIAC’s decision to reject the visa is so unfair, and I am sure they have made a mistake.




E3
I can’t say anything about that until I get the details. Now, this matter relates to the refusal to grant a Short Stay visitor’s Visa. I know you wanted to sponsor your brother to come to Australia for a three-month  period. Why was that?
44




M3
是這樣，我母親跟我們一起住在澳洲，她一直有糖尿病，去年開始 又有了一系列的併發症，半年前還發作過一次心臟病，身體越來越 差了。她很想再見一次我的弟弟，所以我弟弟需要一个签证。
Well… my mother is living with us in Australia. She has diabetes and she’s started to suffer a series of complications since last year. Her health has been getting worse since her heart attack 6 months ago. But she really wants to see my brother again, so my brother needs a visa.




E4
According to the decision, they were not satisfied that your brother would leave Australia when his visa expired. The reason is firstly, that your brother has never travelled overseas before; and that he had limited incentives to return home. Why do you think the decision is incorrect?
47




M4
我弟弟在中國經營著一家書店， 有自己 的 房產 ， 有妻子和一個孩 子。我們已經在申請里提交了銀行證明，顯示我弟弟有超過 70 萬人 民幣的银行存款。
My brother owns a bookshop and has his own property in China. He has a wife and a child. We did provide the bank statement with our application, showing that his bank savings are over 700 thousand RMB.




E5
That makes sense. Looking through the file, I can see that you provided very detailed documentation with your brother’s application. And now you have provided me with a letter that states you will accept the imposition of a security bond for your brother, if necessary.
45




M5
是的，先生。我們是 好公民，並沒有任何不良行為。澳洲移民局爲 什麽這麼冷酷？我只是想让我的弟弟来这里探望生病的母亲而已。
Yes, Sir. We are good citizens here with no record of bad behaviour. Why is DIAC so cold-hearted? We just want to bring my brother here to visit my mother as she is sick.




E6
You are absolutely right. It is quite possible that the MRT will set aside the decision of DIAC.
18
