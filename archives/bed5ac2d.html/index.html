为女贷款

相·遇
06-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mrs. Shan is speaking to a Legal Aid officer about helping her daughter financially.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Mrs. Shan. Sorry to have kept you waiting. The receptionist wasn’t very specific when she mentioned what you’re here for today. Am I correct in saying that it has something to do with taking out a loan?
39




M1
是的，是这样的。我的女儿最近和女婿在 Burwood 买了一套房子，借了不 少钱。// 前不久她和我打电话时说她失业了， 女婿一个人打工还房贷还 要养家很辛苦。我想用我在 Kensington 的房子抵押贷款，来帮他们一把。
Yes, you’re right. My daughter and her husband bought a property in Burwood recently. They borrowed a lot of money for that.// She called me not long ago, saying she has just lost her job, so her husband is now struggling to make the loan repayments and support the family all by himself. In order to help them out, I want to use my property in Kensington to get a mortgage.




E2
I understand that it’s very difficult to watch your daughter struggle financially, but you also need to protect your own interests and make sure that whatever is asked of you is realistic. With that in mind, how would you describe your own financial situation?
44




M2
我的经济状况？一般吧。退休以后我就靠养老金生活，省点用基本能够凑 合。不过 Kensington 的房子是我自己的，房贷也已经还清。我女儿说如果 现在拿去抵押贷款的话可以借到一笔不小的数目。
My financial situation? It’s pretty average. I’ve been living off the pension since retiring. I have no problem getting by if I save a bit of money. But the property in Kensington is mine and the mortgage has been paid off. My daughter said that if I use it to get a mortgage I would be able to borrow a lot of money.




E3
I think I am getting to understand the whole story. Are you saying that, your daughter was the one who suggested the loan, is that correct?
26




M3
是的，虽然她和她丈夫的个人信誉评价都很好，但由于房贷的关系，各大 银行都不愿意再借钱给他们，所以不得不求我帮忙。
Yes. She and her husband both have very good creditworthiness, but no bank wants to lend them money due to their current mortgage. So they had to turn to me for help.




E4
Whilst I can empathise with your situation, I would still like to highlight that you will be the one who bears most of the risk.
25




M4
我明白，但是最近房价涨得这么厉害，如果银行因为他们还不起房贷而回 收他们的房子，以后再要买房就更困难了。
I know, but the housing prices are soaring. It would be difficult for them to buy another property in the future, if the bank took back their house away just because they couldn’t afford the loan.




E5
But if you take out a loan to help your daughter, you will be relying on her to make the repayments, as your pension is only enough for you to get by. If she can’t pay, you might lose your house.
41




M5
可是她毕竟是我唯一的女儿， 我不帮她谁帮她呢？我这次来也是她的提 议，因为我们都想不出更好的办法。您对此有什么建议吗？或许我应该把 房子的产权转一部分给她？
But she is my only daughter. If I don’t help her, who else will? Coming to see you was also her suggestion, as neither of us could come up with a better idea. What’s your advice? Should I transfer part of my property title to her?




E6
That would still carry the same risk as if you take out the loan yourself. You can consider taking out a reverse mortgage, where you only pay back the loan when you die or sell your home. But that might leave too little equity in the property to pay for your health care or to leave as an inheritance to your daughter.
62




M6
反向抵押贷款？我从没听说过。麻烦您给我一些关于这种贷款的信息，我 得回家和我女儿讨论一下。
A reverse mortgage? I have never heard of it. Could you please give me more information about this kind of loan? I’d like to discuss it with my daughter when I get home.
