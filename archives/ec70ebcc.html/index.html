劫匪抢包

相遇
31-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between Ms Phillips, an officer at the Victim Support Service, and a woman who is seeking assistance because her bag has been stolen.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Hello, I’m Sarah Phillips, from the Victim Support Service. How can I help you?
14




M1
是这样的。上周六，在伊丽莎白购物中心，有两个小伙子抢了我的包。警察 说我应该来找你们反应一下。
Well, last Saturday I had my bag snatched by two young boys at Elizabeth Shopping Centre. The police said I should come and talk to you.




E2
I understand. Can you give me some more details about what happened? I take it you have already reported this to the police, is that right?
26




M2
是的。昨天我去警察局做了说明。我跟他们讲述了事情经过也描述了那两个 人的长相。警察还给我头上的伤口拍了照。
Yes, I went to the police station to give a statement yesterday. I told them everything and described the two boys. The police also took photographs of the cut on my head.




E3
Oh, were you injured when they snatched your bag? How did that happen?
13




M3
他们抢我包的时候，把我推倒了。我的头撞到了长条座椅上，当时都流血 了，所以我就去了医院。在这个位置，我缝了五针呢。
They pushed me over when they grabbed my bag, and I hit my head on a bench seat. It was bleeding, so I went to the hospital. I have four stitches, just here.




E4
That must have been painful. It’s good that you got some medical attention straight away. How are you feeling now?
20




M4
对啊。现在还是很疼。但主要的问题还是，当包被抢走的时候，我刚刚才从 银行取了钱，准备是去买菜的。现在，我的钱都没有了。
Yes, it’s still quite sore. But the main problem really is that, when they took my bag, I had just withdrawn money from the bank to do our grocery shopping. Now I have no money.




E5
Right. Well I can certainly assist you with making a compensation claim, but that usually takes some time. In the meantime though, I can give you some supermarket vouchers you can use to buy groceries.
35




M5
那可太好了。太谢谢你了。我朋友也说他们也会接济一下我的，但我清楚他 们自己也不宽裕。
That would be wonderful, thank you so much. My friends also said they will help, but I know they don’t have a lot to spare.




E6
There may be some other things we can help you with in the longer term, but this is the priority for now, isn’t it? Let’s just fill out an application form to get the process started.
36




M6
再次感谢你！我这就去。
Thank you again! I will do that right away.
