授权详谈

相遇
01-08-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




Mr Fan (F) is having a consultation regarding Power of Attorney with a solicitor at a Legal Aid office.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Morning, Mr Fan. Good to see you here today. You look great! What do you think about the new aged care facility you just moved into?
早上好，范先生，今天能在这里见到你真好。你看起来很不错。你觉得你刚搬入的那家老年人照料中心怎么样?




M1
啊，这个敬老院挺好的。那里的社工把我照顾得很好，而且每天都有很多活动，我还能结识一些朋友。
Uh, this nursing home is very good. The social workers here are taking good care of me; they also arrange many activities every day for us to enjoy and I am able to make new friends.




E2
It seems to me that you really enjoy your life there, that’s good to hear. So what brings you here today?
看起来你很喜欢那里的生活，这很好。 那么，你今天是为什么事情而来呢？




M2
就是我老人院的一个朋友，跟我建议弄一个什么，叫什么来着？对了，授权书的一个东西。她说我已经 78 岁了，最好安排一个人来替我管理我自己的事务。
One of my friends in the nursing home suggested that I arrange a thing … a … what is it called? Right, a power of attorney. She told me I’d better get someone to look after my own affairs since I am already 78 years old.




E3
Ok, I see … A power of attorney entitles a person to act on behalf of another and may include the power to make financial, personal or medical decisions. The purpose of a power of attorney is to make sure that a trusted person attends to essential matters at the right time.
好，我明白了。授权书是让某人有权利来代表另一人行事，这些权利可包括对财务、个人或医疗事务做出决定的权利。授权书的目的在于确保接受授权的人士在适当时间管理基本的事务。




M3
那您觉得我一定要这样做吗？我现在独立生活得挺好的，目前为止都没有任何的问题。但我的身体状况，的确是大不如前了。
Do you think I have to do that? I am doing very well living independently. There has been no problem at all. However, my health is indeed not as good as before.




E4
No, you don’t have to unless you want to. It is your decision. Most people prefer to look after their own affairs and retain their independence for as long as possible. However, for some people there comes a time when they lose the ability to make decisions for themselves.
不是的，除非你愿意，否则你不一定要这样做。这你自己的选择。大多数人都喜欢自己管理自己的事务，尽可能长时间保持独立。但一些人到某一时间会丧失自己做决定的能力。




M4
丧失做决定的能力？我并没有得老年痴呆，所以从来没考虑过这些情况。能给我举些例子来说明吗？
Lose the ability to make decisions? I don’t have senile dementia so I have never thought about this scenario. Could you explain this to me using some examples?




E5
Sure. For some people, change may come quickly with the sudden onset of an illness or accident. With a power of attorney, responsibilities can be gradually passed on to an adult family member, a non-family member or a partner.
当然。对于一些人来说，由于突发疾病或事故，他们可能遭遇突然的变故。如果有了授权书的话，当事人就可以将责任逐渐转给成年家庭成员、非家庭成员或是配偶。




M5
那么具体说来，我可以委托哪些人，来代表我行事呢？我老伴儿已经去世了，我也无儿无女。
So who exactly can be appointed as my attorney to act on my behalf? I am widowed and I don’t have any children.




E6
Under a power of attorney arrangement, the person acting on your behalf can be a close friend, a relative, a Public Trustee, a solicitor or an accountant. If you choose to appoint a Public Trustee, a solicitor or an accountant to act on your behalf, some costs may be involved.
按照授权书的安排规定，代表你行事的人士可以是亲友、公共受托人、律师或会计。如果选择指定的公共受托人、律师或会计，就可能要支付一些费用。




M6
我明白了。我可以再来找您吗？因为我现在还没想好该委托谁。
I see. May I make another appointment with you? Because I haven’t decided which person I should appoint.
