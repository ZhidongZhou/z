就业赞助

相遇
05-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




A lady is talking to a shop manager, asking about the possibility of employment sponsorship.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
你好，你是这家按摩店的经理吗？我是 Lisa.
Hello,areyouthemanagerofthismassageshop?MynameisLisa.




M1
Yes I am. How are you? How can I help you, Lisa?





E2
我是一名按摩师，在中国有将近 10 年的工作经验。听说你这儿在招聘，所以我过来试一试运气。
I’m a masseuse with nearly 10 years work experience in China. I heard that you are hiring, so I’m here to try my luck.




M2
Yes, currently we are short of experienced staff. I believe you understand that one requirement for this position is being able to communicate with clients.





E3
当然了。我正在上英语课，也达到了一定的水平。但为了保证今天和你沟通的质量，我觉得还是通过口译员比较好。
Of course. I’m taking an English course and have made some progress. But I thought I’d better speak to you via an interpreter to maintain smooth communication.




M3
That’s fine. You will have to be familiar with the terminology related to remedial massage and aware of the specific needs of our clients. They’ll be more willing to come back if you can go the extra mile.





E4
我明白。我之前一直在做按摩，所以我深知客人的不同需求。我现在非常想在这里定居。我真的不适合呆在大城市，我喜欢小镇的宁静与安逸。
I understand. I’ve been doing this for a while so I know about the varying demands of customers. Now, I’d like to settle down here. I’m really not a big city person. I prefer peaceful and cozy small towns




M4
Well, I suppose you’re interested in the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme. I heard of this at a massage association meeting. They said under this scheme, qualified massage therapists can migrate here permanently.





E5
是的，我之前也去中介咨询过。他们说我必须有这里的学历，所以我就去一家学院学了一个证书，并通过了这里的技术评估。
Yes, I consulted an agent before, who said I had to have a local qualification. So I got a certificate at an institute and passed the skill assessment.




M5
If we are to sponsor you, it will involve a financial commitment on our part as well as the time needed to prepare the application. We really need some time to consider it.





E6
我完全理解。这样，我把我的简历给你，上面有我的联系方式，你们随时可以联系我。我也可以先工作，担保的是我们慢慢来。
I totally understand. Let me give you my resume first, which has my contact details. You can contact me anytime. I can also start working first and discuss the issue of sponsorship later.




M6
That would be perfect. Thank you very much. I’ll be in touch with you.
