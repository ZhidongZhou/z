邻家被盗

相遇
19-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




Mrs. Liu, a Mandarin-speaking woman is telephoning Crime Stoppers to report a crime currently being committed outside her residence.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Thank you for calling Crime Stoppers. How can I help you?
11




M1
我要报案，有人正在入室行窃。我从我卧室的窗户能看到三个人，他们闯进了我邻居的家里。
I’d  like  to  report  a  crime,  a  burglary.  I  can  see  three  people  out  of  my bedroom window. They’ve broken into my neighbour’s house.




E2
OK. Stay where  you  are  and don’t let them see  you looking at them. Can you please tell me your address so I can send out a police car immediately?
31




M2
是 Strathfield 区皇后大街 56 号。三个男人正在把液晶电视、音响，还有台式机电脑往一辆面包车里搬呢。奇怪，报警器怎么没响？
No.  56,  Queens  Street,  Strathfield.  Three  men  are  now  carrying  the  LCD TV,  hi-fi  system  and  desktop  computer  into  a  van.  It’s  very  strange.  How come the alarm wasn’t set off?




E3
Could you describe their appearance to me as well as the van – and can you see the license plate? Any details you provide will be of great assistance to the police.
31




M3
他们身穿黑色 T 恤衫，运动裤。是一辆老款的白色丰田车。天色太暗了，我看不清楚车牌。警察怎么还没来？
They’re wearing black T-shirts and sports shorts. It’s an old model Toyota. I can’t see the plate clearly because it’s too dark outside. How come the police are not here yet?




E4
I’ve notified the police already and they should arrive there in the next five minutes or so. Do you know  your neighbour’s name, where  they are right now and when they will come back?
34




M4
她叫 Alice  Green。她现在在马来西亚度假。她请我在她不在家的时候帮她收邮件和喂猫。她要过两个星期才回来。
Her name is Alice Green and she is on holiday in Malaysia. She asked me to pick up her mail and feed her cat while she’s away. She’ll be back in two weeks.




E5
Thank you. Now, you may want to remain anonymous, but if you’re willing to  give  us  your  name  or  talk  to  the  field  officer  later,  it  may  be  of  great assistance to the police.
34




M5
我不介意。我叫刘晓红，如果警察有什么问题，我很乐意和他们聊聊。我这辈子还从来就没遇到入室行窃的事儿。
I don’t mind. My name is Liu Xiaohong and I’m happy to talk to the officers if  they  have  any  more  questions.  I’ve  never  come  across  a  burglary  my whole life.




E6
If you worry about your safety, please call your local Neighbourhood Watch Centre.  I’m sure they can help  you  with how to  protect  yourself  and  your home.
27




M6
话是这么说，但我还是小心一点好。糟糕，他们正要开车跑了！
That being said, I’d better be alert. Oh no, now they are driving away!
