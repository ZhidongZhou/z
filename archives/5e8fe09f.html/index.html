教育系统

相遇

A Chinese education official is asking an Australian counterpart about the education system in Australia.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
感谢您能抽空回答我关于澳大利亚教育系统的问题。首先我想问的是在澳大利亚，教育是联邦政府的责任还是州级政府的责任？
Thank you for taking time to answer my questions regarding the Australian education system. My first question is, in Australia is it the Federal Government or the State Government that is responsible for education?




M1
Each state government manages the school system within their state. This means that they provide funds for and regulate their schools. Also, each state has a Vocational Education and Training (VET) or Technical and Further Education (TAFE) system. However, funding for universities is the federal government’s responsibility.
47




E2
中国的教育系统有三级，即初级教育，中等教育以及高等教育，请问澳大利亚也是采用类似的模式吗？
China has a three-tier education system which includes primary education, secondary education and tertiary education. Do you follow a similar model?




M2
Yes, Australia follows a similar three-tier model and school education, including primary education and secondary education are both compulsory.
19




E3
哦，这跟中国不一样。我们实行的是九年制义务教育，即小学和初中是强制的。那你们中小学的课程大纲是由州教育部编写吗？
Oh, that is different from us. China has implemented a nine-year compulsory education, which means primary school and junior secondary school are compulsory. Is the state education department in charge of preparing curriculum for schools?




M3
No. Each state has its own Board of Studies which sets the core curriculum by developing syllabuses for Kindergarten to Year 12 and provides support materials for teachers and parents. It also manages the School Certificate external tests and the Higher School Certificate examinations each year.
46




E4
说到 HSC，它是不是指大学的入学考试，就如同中国的高考呢？在学生课业方面，有怎样特别的安排呢？
Speaking of the HSC, is it a university entrance examination? Just like China’s Gao Kao? And are there any special arrangements in terms of the courses for students?




M4
The Higher School Certificate (HSC) is the highest award in secondary education in New South Wales. To be eligible for the award, students must complete Years 11 and 12, satisfy HSC course requirements and sit for the state wide HSC examinations.
41




E5
那学生能进什么样的大学，读什么样的专业，是不是由 HSC 考试的成绩决定的呢？
So the result of the HSC determines which university a student enters and what major he or she can study, right?




M5
Not exactly. The HSC Mark is a 50:50 combination of a student’s HSC examination and school-based assessment mark for each course. Then they have to use this final mark to work out what their ATAR is before applying for undergraduate university programs. Notes: Australian Tertiary Admission Rank (ATAR)
42




E6
那您可以说说澳洲的高等职业教育吗？我听说职业教育在这里非常受欢迎，而且蓝领工人的薪水往往比白领们要高。
Can you talk about Australia’s tertiary education? I heard vocational education is very popular here and blue-collar workers often earn more than white-collar workers.




M6
In Australia, most vocational tertiary education courses are provided by TAFE. Studying in TAFE has many advantages including a focus on applied rather than abstract skills, practical teaching strategies and seamless pathways between TAFE qualifications.
35
