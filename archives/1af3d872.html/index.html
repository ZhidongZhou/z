房屋保险

相遇
28-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mr. Wang approaches a customer service officer of an insurance company to ask about the home insurance they provide.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
你好，我最近新买了一间三房两卫的房子，想上个保险。正好最近在电视上看到你们的广告，所有就过来了。
Hello, I bought a house with three bedrooms and two bathrooms recently and I want to have it insured. I just saw your ads on TV, so I’m here.




M1
You’ve come to the right place as our company provides a comprehensive property policy. We recommend you consider insuring both the building and its contents.
26




E2
我知道很多公司都提供这种类型的住房保险的，我要货比三家再决定。那你们这种综合险可以保哪些东西呢？
I know there are many companies providing this kind of home insurance. I need to compare before deciding which one to go for. So what does your comprehensive policy cover?




M2
It will cover your home against any of these events: fire, lightning strikes, earthquakes, theft, impact by any vehicle or falling trees, etc. You can get more details on our website or you can take a brochure home.
39




E3
我拿一本小册子回去慢慢看。另外，如果我房子里的家具或电器损坏了，且无法维修，那怎么办？如果，连房子都完全损毁呢？
I’ll take a brochure and read it carefully. Also, what if the furniture and appliances in my house are so damaged that they cannot be repaired? What if my house is completely destroyed?




M3
You must provide all the invoices of your damaged appliances. The policy will enable you to build a new home of an equivalent style, material and size. Also, should you decide to purchase your property insurance policy with us we can offer a lot of other benefits.
48




E4
对了，你能跟我说说其他的可报销项目吗？比如，如果我的房屋受损需要维修，这段期间你们会提供临时的住所吗？
Oh also, can you tell me more about the other benefits? For example, if my house is damaged and needs repair, will you provide me with temporary accommodation?




M4
Yes, we can help you with the cost of temporary accommodation for up to 12 months. Other benefits include any debris removal costs, architects’ and surveyors’ fees and accidental breakage of fixed glass and mirrors.
35




E5
那还真不错。难怪，我有一个朋友向我推荐你们公司。你们的套餐确实很吸引人。有一件事我还不清楚，应该如何如何查询我的保险额呢？
That’s pretty good. No wonder a friend of mine recommended your company to me. Your policy is really appealing. One more thing, how can I find out the amount insured?




M5
You need to measure the house and get the total area. Then multiply the number of square metres by the approximate building costs listed in the table provided.
28




E6
假如我还不是知道如何计算，你们有同事可以帮我吗？还有，我可以选择垫底费是多少吗？
If I still don’t know how to calculate it, will your colleagues help me with that? Also, can I choose how much the excess is?




M6
Yes, we can arrange to have one of our agents make the calculation for you. The excess will vary according to the sum insured. For example, if you are insured for $100,000 the excess is $200.
36
