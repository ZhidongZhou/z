百日咳症

相遇
19-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mrs. Li and her GP, Dr. Smith, regarding her daughter’s whooping cough.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Mrs. Li. How can I help you today?
10




M1
医生你好,我今天带了我的大女儿来看你。她最近一直咳嗽，不能停的那种，而且鼻子不通气，打喷嚏还有点发低烧。我的女儿到底是怎么了？
Hello,  doctor.  I’ve  brought  my  oldest  daughter  here  today.  She  has  been coughing and couldn’t stop. She also has a stuffy nose, sneezing and mild fever. What’s wrong with her?




E2
Well, based on what you have just described, I am afraid that your daughter may be in the early stages of whopping cough. The symptoms could develop further if it is not treated.
33




M2
真的？这听上去太可怕了！那这种病是不是很严重呢？我的女儿是怎么患上这种病的呢？最近她们学校感冒的人特别多，和这个有没有关系呢？
Really? That sounds horrible! Is it a serious disease?   Where did she get it? Currently, so many students in her school have caught colds, could that be the cause?




E3
Whooping cough is a serious respiratory infection that causes a long coughing illness. In babies, it can lead to pneumonia and brain damage and it can be life- threatening. Older children’s lives are not at risk, but they can spread this illness to others, including babies.
45




M3
我家里还有一个小宝贝儿子呢，是不是就是说我的小宝宝现在也被传染上百日咳了呢？ 但是我的小宝宝没有咳嗽的症状，所以我今天就没有带他来。
I have another little baby boy at home. Do you mean my little baby has also contracted  whooping  cough?  But  he  has  no  symptom  of  coughing  so  I  didn’t bring him here today.




E4
Yes, it’s possible! Whooping cough is highly infectious in the first 3 weeks; it spreads easily through families, childcare centres and schools so it is likely that your baby boy has already been infected. Some newborn babies do not cough at all. Instead, they turn blue. So it is important that you bring your baby boy here for a check up.
61




M4
百日咳是怎样传染的呢？那我怎样防止我的孩子患上百日咳呢？ 我的小儿子在 2 个月，4 个月和 6 个月的时候打过疫苗，这一点用也没有吗?
How  does  whooping  cough  spread?  What  do  I  need  to  do  to  prevent  my children from getting the disease? My youngest son was immunised against it when he was 2 months, 4 months and 6 months old. Was that helpful at all?




E5
Whooping cough is spread when the infected person coughs and the bacteria coughed into the air is inhaled by babies, children or adults nearby. These people are in danger of getting whooping cough. Just keep in mind that even immunised children can sometimes catch whooping cough.
46




M5
那我知道了，但是你还没有告诉我我都需要做什么去保护我的孩子。 有什么药物治疗的方法吗?
Oh,  I  see.  But  you  still  haven’t  told  me  what  I  need  to  do  to  protect  my children. Is there any medicine that can treat the disease?




E6
If whooping cough is detected early enough, like in your daughter’s case, a course of antibiotics could be prescribed, which I am going to do in a second. After 5 days of treatment, enough bacteria will be killed to stop it spreading to others. The best way to protect your children would be keeping them away from the infected.
59




M6
我知道了，医生，谢谢。我明天就带小宝宝来见你。
I  understand.  Thank  you,  doctor.  I  will  bring  my  little  baby  here  to  see  you tomorrow.
