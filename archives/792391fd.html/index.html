斐济换钱

相·遇
07-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a conversation between a financial advisor and Mr Wang about exchanging money into Fijian Dollars.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
G’day Mr Wang! I’m David McDonald, financial adviser of the Commonwealth Bank of Australia. How can I help you today?
20




M1
你好，麦当劳先生。我今天是想来咨询一下关于我去斐济旅游的问题，我是 否需要提前在澳洲兑换一些斐济币。
Good morning Mr McDonald. I’m here to inquire about my trip to Fiji. Do I need to buy some Fijian dollars here in advance?




E2
Certainly you could, but as the Fijian dollar is not a common currency, we need you to make an appointment. But, let me check for you and see how much cash we have on hand. So, how much do you want?
41




M2
我想兑换大概价值澳元 3000 元的斐济币。大概是多少钱呢？我要去那边呆 1 个月呢，而且可能去外岛度假，所以可能会花不少钱。我怕那边太偏远，没 法用信用卡，所以我还是比较想换好现金再去。
I’d like to convert about AU$3000 to Fijian dollars. How much can I get? I’ll need to stay there for about a month and I may spend some time during the holiday going to outer islands as well, so it may cost quite a lot of money. I’m worried that the place is too remote to use my credit card, so I want exchange some cash to take with me.




E3
Yes, you are right. If you go to some remote islands, we would suggest that you to take enough cash to last the whole time. However, if you arrive in Fiji at Nadi International Airport, there are ATM facilities and exchange booths for traveler’s cheques and Australian dollar notes. Umm …. According to our records, we have only 1000 Fijian dollars at this branch.
64




M3
这不够啊！我是会在楠迪机场降落，但是我要立刻转机，可能没有时间出去 找两替店了。我知道信用卡可以用，但是我听说不论走万事达还是走维萨的 通道，都会收取高达 3%的手续费。刚才你说的旅行支票是什么？
Ah that won’t be enough! It is true I will land at Nadi Airport, but I’m going to transfer straight away, so I may not have time to find an exchange booth. I know I can use my credit card, but I heard that both Visa and Master card charge a transaction fee as high as 3%. You just mentioned traveller’s cheques. What is that?




E4
Yes, you are right. You will be charged at least 3% of the transaction as a processing fee. And we are not sure whether they accept American Express since we don’t have any ATMs there. As I know, ANZ has some ATMs at Nadi Airport. You can enquire with them about this. In terms of traveller’s cheques, it’s like a bank cheque, which allows you to withdraw cash overseas in a safe and reliable way.
75




M4
那好的，我看不必要去问 ANZ 了。我可以兑换你现有的 1000 斐济元现金 么？然后其余的 2500 澳币左右我想要旅行支票。这样我可以一降落就马上 把旅行支票兑换成当地货币。
Okay. I don’t think I need to ask ANZ. Can I buy the 1000 Fijian Dollars you have now? And I’d like a travellers cheque for the remaining AU$2500 so that I can exchange it for local currency right after landing.




E5
Sure, I will process this for you now. So you want all 50 dollar notes or a combination of 50 and 100 dollar notes?
24




M5
我想要 50 一张的纸币作为零花钱，免得到了当地老拿出大钞来，别人以为 我很有钱，抢劫我。
I’d like to have 50 dollar notes as pocket money, so that I don’t have to use large notes all the time. Otherwise people may think I’m very rich and want to rob me.




E6
Yes. That’s a good way to avoid any risk when you are travelling overseas. Here you are, that’s your $1000 Fijian dollar cash and your traveller’s cheque. Please keep it safe and if any is stolen or lost, please report it to us at the earliest opportunity.
47




M6
好的，如果真的掉了我会跟你们电话报失的。谢谢。再见。
Fine. If I were to lose it I would report to you. Thank you, bye.
