失眠就医

相·遇
09-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



A patient is seeking help from a GP about a sleeping disorder.








ID
Original
Translated





A1
Good morning. Please have a seat. You look very tired. problem What’s the Problem?





Z1
我最近不太容易入睡。我觉得累，但是却睡不着。就算到了后半夜 终于睡着了，也睡得很浅。到了白天，就无精打采。
I’ve been having difficulty falling asleep. I feel tired but I can’t fall asleep. Even if I finally fall asleep after midnight, my sleep is very light. By the morning, I feel exhausted.




A2
How long have you had this problem? Is there anything that may have caused this sleeping disorder? Have there been any significant changes in your life recently?





Z2
三个月了。我觉得没有。只是最近比较忙，别的也没有什么事情。
Three months. I don’t think so. I’ve just been very busy lately. That’s all.




A3
What about your diet? Have you changed your diet? You know, changing diets may affect your sleeping patterns.





Z3
没有。最近食欲倒是有些下降。体重也有减轻。但最让我头痛的问 题还是睡不着觉。我晚上睡不着，白天就觉得更加累，而且精神无 法集中。
ID I have however been losing my appetite and I have lost some weight. But the thing troubling me most is not being able to fall asleep. If I can’t sleep at night, I feel more tired during the day and I can’t concentrate.




A4
There are many reasons for the unexplained weight loss and poor appetite. Have you taken any sleeping pills for this? Do you get headaches?





Z4
试过安眠药，但是对我一点儿都不起作用。 我偶尔会头痛，但都不 太严重。我只要闭目养神一会儿头痛就好了。
I’ve tried sleeping pills but they did not work for me. I get headaches occasionally but they are not too severe and the pain goes way if I close my eyes and have a rest.




A5
Well, there is nothing to worry about. You are just a little exhausted from overworking, and it may have caused some pressure that you are not aware of.





Z5
我该怎么办呢？我需要吃更好的安眠药吗？我怕我现在安眠药吃多 了， 以后会有药物依赖。 而且中国不是有一句俗话叫是药三分毒 嘛。
What should I do then? Do I need to take more effective sleeping pills? I’m worried that if I take too many sleeping pills I will develop a drug dependency. And isn’t there a Chinese proverb that says, “no medicine is without side effects”?




A6
You need more rest. You’re experiencing stress as a result of pressures at work, which is causing your insomnia. You don’t have to take sleeping pills at this stage. Milk will help. If you still have trouble sleeping after one week, you could start taking sleeping pills.





Z6
好的，大夫，我会照你说的去做。谢谢你，再见！
Alright, I’ll do as you say. Thanks, goodbye!
