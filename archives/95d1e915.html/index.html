失业社福

相遇
11-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mr. Liu (L), who is recently unemployed, and a Centrelink officer Harry (H). The dialogue begins now.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Mr. Liu. I’m Harry, your customer service advisor. How can I help you today?
16




M1
我工作的工厂最近倒闭了，所以我失业了。因为劳动成本的原因，制造业在这个国家正在消亡。所以我来请你们帮助我。
Good Morning, Harry. The factory I worked for was closed down recently so I’ve lost my job. The manufacturing industry is dying in this country because of rising labour costs.




E2
Well, if that’s the case, I think we may be able to help you through this with the Newstart Allowance. But firstly, I need to determine whether you’re eligible for the allowance. Can I have a look at your ID please?
41




M2
给你，这是我的驾照。我从来没想到我会来求你们施舍。这种感觉真不好。
This is my driver’s license. Here you are. I never thought I would come to you asking for charity. I feel terrible about it.




E3
Please don’t think about it that way. It is our responsibility to help people get through difficult times. You meet the age requirement so you’ll need to enter into an Employment Pathway Plan.
33




M3
你能给我解释一下这个计划吗？是说只要我加入了这个计划，你们就能帮我找到工作吗？它需要我去做什么呢？
Can you explain this plan to me? Are you saying that you will find a job for me if I join this plan? What does it require me to do?




E4
An Employment Pathway Plan explains what you need to do to get a job or improve your employment prospects and what assistance you will be offered. The plan may contain activities such as job searching, vocational education and participation in a labour market program.
44




M4
所以，这个计划是由你们制定的，然后要求我严格遵行吗？如果我觉得某些活动不适合我怎么办？这样听起来似乎是你们想控制我的生活。
So is it true that this plan is made by you, and then I need to follow it strictly? What if I think some activities are not suitable for me? It sounds like you will control my life.




E5
That’s not how it works. We’ll negotiate with you to identify your individual needs but once we have both agreed on the plan you’ll have to participate in the activities. Otherwise, there will be a ‘No Show, No Pay’ penalty.
40




M5
既然是根据我的个人需求制定的计划，我肯定会欣然执行。我会珍惜一切机会来提高我找到工作的可能性。
As long as it’s based on my personal needs, then I will definitely be happy to abide by it. I would appreciate every opportunity to improve my chances of getting employed.




E6
Excellent. Please be aware that you need to regularly send us evidence that you are actively looking for suitable paid work and attend all job interviews.
26




M6
好的，我知道了。你能跟我说一下津贴的数额有多少？我如果偶尔做一些临时工，会不会影响我领取福利金？
Alright, I see. Can you tell me how much the allowance is? If I occasionally do some casual work, will it affect my payment?
