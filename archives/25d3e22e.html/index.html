亲戚移民

ZDZ
05-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



The following dialogue is between Mr. Li and Herbert, a migration
agent. Mr. Li is seeking advice on sponsoring an overseas relative to
come to Australia to work. * * *











ID
Original
Translated






E1
Hello Mr. Li, you said to me last time that you wanted to sponsor
overseas workers to come here, right?





M1
是的。我在悉尼拥有一家食品加工厂，生产各种中式点心。我希望我的侄子能来澳洲帮我做事。
Yes. I own a food processing factory in Sydney, producing various
kinds of Chinese dim sims. I hope my nephew can come here and work for
me.




E2
Both the employer and the employee need to meet certain requirements
to be eligible for the employer nomination scheme. As the employer, you
must be actively and lawfully operating a business in Australia.





M2
这个肯定没问题，我这个厂已经开了快 10
年了。我的产品非常受欢迎，你在各个华人超市都能买到。我的年营业额有四百多万呢。
This is not a problem at all. I’ve been operating the factory for
almost 10 years. My products are very popular and you can find them in
every Chinese supermarket. The annual turnover is more than $4
million.




E3
Then the position you provide must be full time, ongoing and
available for at least three years. It must also be a highly skilled
occupation that is on the Employer Nomination Scheme Occupation List
(ENSOL).





M3
这份清单是什么？我没听说过。我给我侄子安排的职位是食品技术员，您能帮我查查这个职位是否在这份清单上吗？
What’s this list? I have never heard of it. The position I’m going
to offer my nephew is food technologist. Could you check for me and see
if it’s on the list?What’s this list? I have never heard of it. The
position I’m going to offer my nephew is food technologist. Could you
check for me and see if it’s on the list?




E4
You can only nominate overseas workers to fill a position in an
occupation that appears on the ENSOL. Let me have a look. Well, food
technologist is on the list. That’s very good!





M4
那我的侄子需要满足哪些条件呢？他在国内大学学的就是食品科技，毕业后至今已经有了五年的相关工作经验。在技术与资质方面
他应该是没问题的。
What requirements does my nephew need to meet? He studied food
technology at university in China and has five years’ relevant working
experience after graduation. There shouldn’t be any problems in terms of
his skills and qualifications.




E5
That seems good enough. You should also be aware that he needs to
meet the vocational English language requirements. That is to say, he
needs to score 5 in each of the four sections of the IELTS test.





M5
我不太了解他的英语水平。不过雅思 5
分对于他这样一个读过大学的人来说应该不是什么难事吧。
I’m not sure about his English level. However, for a university
graduate, scoring 5 in the IELTS test should not be very difficult, I
reckon.




E6
Other requirements are more general. For example, your nephew needs
to show evidence of good health and character and sign the Australian
Values Statement.





M6
多谢你啦，让我有了一个大致的概念。看来这并没有我想像的困难。
Thank you for giving me a rough idea. It seems like it’s not as
difficult as I thought it would be.
