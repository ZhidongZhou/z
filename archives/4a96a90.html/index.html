A business man named Mr Brown has just moved to Beijing and is
looking for a new house to rent. He is having a discussion with Mrs Lu,
a Mandarin-speaking real estate agent. * * * | ID | Original |
Translated | | --- | --- | --- | | M1 | 您好， 有什么可以帮到您吗？ |
Hello, how can I help you today? | | E1 | Hello. My name's Gary Brown.
I've just moved to the area and I'm looking for an apartment to rent. |
你好。我叫加里·布朗。我刚搬到这个地区，正在找一套公寓出租。 | | M2 |
您好， 布朗先生。 你想住在北京的哪一块而呢？ | Hello, Mr. Brown. Where
do you want to live in Beijing? | | E2 | Well, I'm starting my new job
as a DJ for Tango Club in Chaoyang district, so I would like to get a
place relatively close to my workplace and the subway. | 好的，
我的新工作是朝阳区探戈俱乐部的DJ
(音乐主持人)，所以我想找一个离工作地点和地铁都比较近的地方。
| | M3 | 嗯， 我知道这个俱乐部， 很受外国人的欢迎。 来，
您看看这份传单， 上面列出了目前朝阳区可以租的房子。 您想住几间卧室的呢？
有什么特殊需要么？ | Ah, I know that club. It's very popular with
foreigners. Here, have a look at this leaflet, it lists all the
properties currently available in Chaoyang. How many bedrooms would you
like? Do you have any special requirements? | | E3 | I'm looking for a
place with a decent-sized kitchen, as well as an extractor
fan. A one-bedroom or studio apartment would be fine, but I'm open to
two even three-bedroom apartments if they're available |
我正在找一个地方，有一个像样的厨房，以及一个排气扇。一居室或一室一厅的公寓都可以，但如果有两个甚至三个卧室的公寓，我也可以接受。
| | M4 | 我知道很多外国人不喜欢蹲式厕所， 对吧？ 嗯，
您看第四页， 上面是位于光华路目前可以租的房子。 | I know many foreigners
don’t like squat toilets, right? Hmm, Have a look on the
fourth page, It lists information about properties currently available
for rent on Guanghua Road. | | E4 | I see that's a
one-bedroom place. You guys are asking ¥3000 a month. Well, that's way
out of my budget. |
我知道那是一居室。你们要求3000块人民币一个月。嗯，这超出了我的预算。 | |
M5 | 朝阳区的价格贵， 这是肯定的。 但是您看下755号这间，
才¥2200元而且刚刚装修过， 里面还有壁炉呢。 | Chaoyang is an
expensive district, that's for sure. But have a llook at the one on 755.
Only 2200 yuan and it's recently renovated. It even has a fireplace. | |
E5 | When was the building built? I rented an apartment in Macau last
year that was built sometime in the 80s. The elevator only worked until
midnight. You can imagine coming home after a night shift and having to
walk 10 flights of stairs. |
大楼是什么时候建成的我去年在澳门租了一套公寓，是在80年代的某个时候建的。电梯只开到午夜。你可以想象当你上完晚班回家，还得爬10层楼梯。
| | M6 | 这没问题的。 这楼是2001年建成的， 电梯一周七天24小时运行。 |
That won't be a problem. This building was built in 2001, and the
elevator runs 24/7. | | E6 | Well that's good. Well I'm guessing, then,
that the utilities bills are extra? And how about the bond? How much is
that? | 好,很好。我猜，水电费是要另外算的， 那么押金如何呢？那个多少钱？
| | M7 | 对， 租客需要自己负担水电煤网的费用。 押金的话，
相当于三个月的租金， 还有一个月的押金， 签字的时候交。
这是在中国约定俗成的。 | That's right, tenants pay for their own
electricity, water and gas. As for bond, it's 3 months' rent plus a
one-month security deposit when you sign. This is standard practice all
over China. |
