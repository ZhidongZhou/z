分行投资

相遇
26-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This dialogue is between Mr. Johnson, a branch manager of a bank, and a Chinese woman, Mrs. Li, who wants to find a good investment option.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Mrs. Li. It’s my pleasure to meet you here today. Do you want anything to drink?
18




M1
不用了，谢谢。您电话里说有很多好的投资选项给我，今天我想跟你了解一下具体情况。
I’m fine. Thank you. You told me on the phone there are a lot of investment options. I would like to know more about those options today.




E2
You will have peace of mind if you choose to make a term deposit with us. I would recommend a term deposit of 6 months. The interest rate for today is 5.3% per annum.
34




M2
我其实希望找到一种资金灵活性比较高的投资项目，我想随时可以把钱拿出来，以备不时之需。
Actually I was hoping to find an investment project with higher flexibility. I want to be able to take my money out in case of emergency.




E3
We have a wide range of other products. You can take a look at our rates here for term deposits of 30, 60 and 90 days.
27




M3
恩，这些时间投资产品时间上比较适合我。但是利息有点太低了，有没有利息更高的选项呢？
Term deposits are suitable for me, but the interest rates are too low. Are there any options with higher interests?




E4
Our bank is in a long-term partnership with a few investment banks here in Australia. They could come up with investment portfolios for you with much higher returns. But higher returns carry higher risks.
34




M4
那都有哪些投资组合呢？有哪些风险呢？投资都会有风险的，也许几个投资方案组合在一起可以降低风险。
What investment portfolios are available? What are their risks? There are always risks when you invest. Maybe the risks can be reduced if I combine several investment options.




E5
Every investment is subject to credit risk, interest risk and default risk, etc. I think it would be better if you talk to our partners in person.
27




M5
好的。那麻烦您帮我约一下时间。现在把钱放在网上银行利息很低，我得尽快找到合适的投资产品。希望能够找到回报高，风险相对较低的产品。
Ok. Could you please make an appointment for me? The interest for online banking is very low. I want to find a suitable investment product as soon as possible. Hopefully I can find a product with high returns and relatively low risks.




E6
Take this term deposit sheet with you. They will help you can get an idea of our term deposit rates.
20




M6
非常感谢您的建议。今天我学到了很多。再见！
Thank you for your advice. I have learnt a lot today. Bye.
