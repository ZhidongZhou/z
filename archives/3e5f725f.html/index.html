丧亲社福

相遇
11-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue is between Mr. Guan and Adam, a Centrelink officer. Mr. Guan is taking care of his nephew who has just lost both parents.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good afternoon, Mr. Guan. You said on the phone that you were in a very difficult situation. Can you tell me what happened?
22




M1
我是为我的侄子 Andrew 而来。他的父母，也就是我的弟弟和弟媳，两周前在一场惨烈的车祸中丧生了。所以我来看看是否能从这儿获得一些帮助，来帮我们度过难关。
I came here for my nephew, Andrew. His parents, who are my brother and sister-in-law, passed away in a horrible car accident two weeks ago. So I’m here to see whether I can get some assistance from you to help us manage a little better.




E2
I’m very sorry to hear that. I hope Andrew is coping well. So unfortunately, he is an orphan now. Is he living with your family? Does he have other relatives in Australia?
33




M2
对，他现在住在我们家。除了我们，他仅有的亲戚就是爷爷奶奶，外公外婆。但他们都不住在澳大利亚，而且很年迈了，不能照顾他。所以我是他的监护人。
Yes, he lives with us. The only relatives he has besides us are his grandparents. But they are all living overseas and too old to look after him. So I am his guardian.




E3
Well, as a relative caring for the child, you may be eligible to get various types of assistance from the Family Assistance Office and the Child Support Agency, in addition to assistance provided by Centrelink.
35




M3
也就是说，一般父母能够获得的补助，我都能获得是吗？我现在在领家庭税务福利 A，每两周能领 214 刀。对于 Andrew，我也可以领取同样的一份津贴吗？
That is to say I can receive all the benefits that parents normally get, right? I’m receiving the family tax benefit A and receiving $214 per week. Can I receive one more payment the same for Andrew?




E4
Yes, you are eligible for another payment of the Family Tax Benefit Part A but the amount may be different since it depends on the child’s age.
26




M4
Andrew 才 5 岁，白天一直需要人来照顾。但我和我妻子都要上班，所以必须要把 Andrew 交给别人照看。你们能在这方面帮我吗？
Andrew is only 5 and he needs to be taken care of during the day. But my wife and I both have to work, so we have to find someone else to look after him. Can you help us with that?




E5
Yes, we can help you with our Childcare Benefit, which is available for children who are looked after by approved or registered childcare centres. It can be claimed as reduced childcare fees or as a lump sum payment.
38




M5
这太好了。Andrew 真是一个可怜的孩子，我想尽力给他营造一个好的成长环境。幸亏有你们的帮助，不然这是不可能的。
That’s great. Andrew is a poor child. I want to try my best to offer him a good environment to grow up in. However, I wouldn’t be able to do that without your help.




E6
Supporting people in need is what we do. By the way, I think you are also eligible for the Double Orphan Pension, since both of Andrew’s parents are deceased.
29




M6
好的，谢谢。那我马上就填表申请吧。要是有什么问题我再来问你。
Great, thanks. Then I’ll fill out the application form right away. I’ll get back to you if I have more questions.
