呼气测试

相遇
13-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between a police officer and Mr. Zhang at an information session about random breath testing (RBT).








ID
Original
Translated





E1
By law, probationary drivers (P-platers) must maintain a zero BAC (Blood Alcohol Concentration). New alcohol laws affecting P-platers are expected to be introduced in January 2012. Does anyone have any other questions?
32




M1
我有。根椐现行法律，警察是否有权叫我靠边停车，让我做随机呼吸测试？ 或者这么说吧， 法律是不是规定他们随便什么时候想做随机呼吸测试都可 以？
Yes. According to the current legal system, does a police officer have the authority to pull me over and randomly breathalyse me? Or let me put it this way, are they authorized by law to perform a RBT whenever they want to?




E2
Thanks for your question. As you might already know, the police do have the power to randomly stop vehicles, but it does not mean they can perform random preliminary breath tests in any circumstances.
34




M2
我知道了。 也就是说他们并不能在任何情况下都有权利命令我们做呼吸测 试。那么，在什么情况下他们可以要求司机停车接受检查呢？
I see. So they can’t perform breath tests whenever they want. Then, in what circumstances can they ask drivers to pull over and take a breath test?




E3
They have the authority to require a person to comply with a preliminary breath test if they have reasonable suspicions that a person is driving, attempting to drive or is in charge of a motor vehicle on a road or other public place while under the influence of alcohol.
49




M3
很多人都认为喝了一点点酒再开车是没有那么危险的。但我并不这样认为。 您能否具体介绍一下什么是 BAC，它又是怎么个测算法的呢?
Many people think that driving after drinking a little bit is not that dangerous. But I don’t agree. Would you please explain in detail what BAC is and how BAC is measured?




E4
Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC) is a measurement of the amount of alcohol in the body. BAC is measured in grams of alcohol per 100 millilitres of blood. The legal blood alcohol concentration (BAC) limit is 0.05. This means that a driver’s body must contain less than 50 milligrams of alcohol per 100 millilitres of blood.
55




M4
我觉得我有必要记住这些具体的规定。非常感谢您的解释。我还想问一下， 如果警察有合理的怀疑的话，是否有权在我家进行呼吸测试呢？我之前听说 有类似的情况。
I think it’s necessary to memorize those specific regulations just in case. And I thank you for your clarifications. I wonder whether they are also authorized to require a breath test in my own home if they have reasonable suspicions. I have heard of such cases before.




E5
I fully understand your concern. The truth of the matter is, as long as a police officer is not trespassing on a suspects property then any breath test requirement they make will be lawful, meaning you would have to comply with their requests.
43




M5
这个倒是说得通。还好，那如果是青少年犯罪的情况呢？我是说如果青少年 酒后驾驶的话，需要其监护人出面吗？
Fine, that makes sense. What if the offender is a teenager? I mean if a teenager commits drink-driving, will their guardian need to be present?




E6
In cases where a juvenile has been detained at a police station for a drink driving related offence there is no requirement for the police to wait for an appropriate adult to be present to commence the breath testing procedure. // The procedures do not constitute an interview and therefore should not be unduly delayed.
54




M6
你所提供的有关酒后驾驶的信息非常有用。谢谢您抽时间向我解释。
Your explanations regarding drink-driving is very helpful and I greatly appreciate  your time.
