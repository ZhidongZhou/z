女儿选课

ZDZ
21-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mr Lee (L) is talking to a school teacher (T) regarding his
daughter’s subject choices. * * *











ID
Original
Translated






E1
Good morning, Mr. Lee. You said you want to talk to me about your
daughter, Cindy. What’s the problem?
19




M1
早上好,Tomas 老师。Cindy 明年就升入 11
年级了，但是我对这边的高考制度不是很了解，所以想跟你谈一谈，听取些建议。
Good morning, Mr. Tomas. Cindy’s starting Year 11 next year but I’m
not very familiar with the “Higher School Certificate” examination
system here. That’s why I want to talk to you about this and get some
advice.




E2
I see. Cindy’s a very capable student and I’m sure she’ll do well in
Years 11 and 12. I’d like to know whether you know anything about the
HSC system before I start explaining it.
35




M2
我不是在这里受的教育，但我听我朋友说，12
年级的学生要参加一个州级范围的考试。而这个考试的结果会决定他们去哪所大学就读。是这样吗？
I wasn’t educated here but I was told by my friends that Year 12
students need to sit a state-wide examination. The result of that
examination determines which university they can go to. Am I right?




E3
You’re quite right. But there are some more specific requirements
that students need to follow in order to graduate. The basic rule is
that they need to complete at least 10 units of study in their HSC.
37




M3
10 个单元学分的学习？是 10
个科目的意思吗？这对她来说会不会太困难了？那她一周要去学校上课上多久呀？
10 units of study? Does that mean 10 subjects? Will that be too hard
for her? How long does she need to go to school for each week?




E4
Mr. Lee, I think you’ve misunderstood me. “10 units of study”
doesn’t mean 10 subjects at all. In the HSC, each subject can have
between 1 to 4 units and students can go beyond 10 units if they want
to.





M4
我明白了。Cindy
跟我说过，英文是必修科目，所以除了英文，她还想选读生物、化学、物理还有四个单元学分的数学。这样加起来够十个学分了吗？
I see. Cindy told me that English is a compulsory subject and she
wants to study biology, chemistry, physics and 4-unit mathematics apart
from English. Do they make 10 units altogether?




E5
That is 12 units in total. In this case, her final mark for HSC will
be calculated using her English result and the best marks for eight of
her other units.





M5
不过呢，我想让她学语言，特别是中文。因为我们都是炎黄子孙，她能够讲中文不是会更好？请问
HSC 里面有语言类考试吗？
By the way, I want her to study languages especially Chinese. This
is because she is Chinese and it’d be good for her to be able to speak
the language. Are there any language examinations in the HSC?




E6
Definitely, there are more than 30 languages for students to choose
from in the HSC; but our school only offers Chinese, Japanese, Korean,
Italian, Latin and Spanish.





M6
好的，我明白了。太谢谢你了！
OK. I understand. Thank you very much!
