    • 肠镜腹痛

    相遇
    10-05-2020
    Hits
    学习笔记 › CCL
    CCL 
    
        
    
        

    

    This is a dialogue between the patient, Ms. Wang and the specialist Daniel Anderson.
    

    

    


    
ID
Original
Translated

    
    

    
E1
Good Morning Mr Wang, How are you today? I am doctor Daniel Anderson.
早上好王女士，我还是 Daniel Anderson 医生， 你今天感觉如何？

    

    
M1
不怎么好， 我今天来看您是因为我总是肚⼦痛， ⽽且经常腹泻， 有时候还便秘不知道是怎么回事。
Not very well unfortunately. I am here today because I always have pain in my stomach and I regularly have diarrhoea, I sometime also have consipation. I don’t know what is going on.

    

    
E2
How long have you have this symptom, have you had any test done, is anyone in your family has bowel ((肠道; /‘baʊəl/)) problems?
你有这个症状多久了，有没有做过测试，你家里有人有以上问题吗

    

    
M2
已经持续三个⽉了， 我没做过什么测试， 我爸爸是得肠癌去世的， 我这是得了肠癌么？
It has been going on for three months. I haven’t done any tests yet. My father died of bowel cancer. Did I have bowel cancer (肠癌)?

    

    
E3
It is too soon to tell, but if you have a family history of bowel cancer, you will be at a higher risk of getting this disease. I suggest you have a colonoscopy (结肠镜检查; /ˌkəulə’nɔskəpi/) to investigate.
现在说还为时过早，但是如果您有肠癌家族史，则患此病的风险会更高。我建议您进行结肠镜检查。

    

    
M3
肠镜， 那要怎么做啊， 您能跟我解释⼀下么， 我从来没做过， 有点害怕。
Colonoscopy (结肠镜检查), how to do it, can you explain it to me? I am a bit scared.

    

    
E4
That Physician (内科医生) will insert a long flexible tube into your rectum (直肠), the scope transmit a image of the inside of the column, so that the physician can carefully examine your intestinal (结肠； /ɪnte’staɪn(ə)l/) lining (内膜).
内科医生会在您的直肠中插入一根长的软管，示波器会传输结肠内部的图像，这样内科医生可以仔细检查你的结肠内膜。内科医生会在您的直肠中插入一根长的软管，示波器会传输结肠内部的图像，这样内科医生可以仔细检查你的结肠内膜。

    

    
M4
谢谢您的解释， 需要多⻓时间， 会痛么， 安不安全，有没有什么⻛险， 需要住院么？
Thank you for explaining that. How long do colonoscopies take? Do they hurt? Are they safe Are there any risks and will I need to be hospitalised (住院)

    

    
E5
A colonoscopy is a Low risk procedure (手术), its takes about 30 minutes, the sedative (镇静剂; /‘sedətɪv/) should keep you from feeling much discomfort during the examination and you can go home on the same day.
结肠镜检查是一种低风险的 手术 (Procedure)，大约需要30分钟，镇静剂应使您在检查过程中不会感到很大不适，并且您可以在当天回家。

    

    
M5
好的谢谢，我明⽩了，做之前有什么忌⼝的么， 或者做之前有什么需要注意的么？
Okay, thank you, I understand now. Is there anything I should avoid eating ( 忌口 ) / (Having Dietary Restrictions)? Or is there anything that I should pay attention to, before the procedure?

    

    
E6
To prepare for this operation, you may need to follow a liquid diet (流食) for 3 days.
要为这个手术作准备，您可能需要遵循流质饮⻝3天。

    

    
M6
好的医⽣谢谢你， 检查结果出来我再来看您
Okay, thank you doctor. I will back to see you once the result are ready.
