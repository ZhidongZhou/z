乐器检疫

A Chinese tourist, Mr. Zhuang, is stopped by a customs officer, Alfred, at the airport.

ID Original Translated
E1 Hello, Sir. Please put your bags over here, and please open and unpack them for us. 16
M1 到底怎么回事？为什么我们需要打开自己的行李给你们检查呢？ What is going on? Why do we need to open our luggage and have it checked by you?
E2 Just put them up here and unlock them please. This is a routine quarantine inspection. What are in these bags? According to your passport, you’ve been visiting Thailand. Did you visit any farms while you were away? 37
M2 这是一个脏衣服袋子。那一个装了一些化妆品在里面。我们没有参观任何的农场。我们只和我的岳父母呆在首都或者一些大城市。 It’s a laundry bag. That one has some cosmetics in it. We didn’t visit any farms. We just stayed in the capital and other big cities with my parents-in-law.
E3 I see. What is in this case? Did you pack all these tea packets in between the contents of this case? 21
M3 那是小提琴箱子，给我妹妹的。一定是我的岳母把它们放在那里的，用来保护小提琴。她知道我妹妹喜欢绿茶，她没有意识到这是不允许的。 That’s a violin case for my sister. It must have been my mother-in-law who put them in there to protect the violin. She knows my sister loves green tea but she wasn’t aware that it’s not allowed.
E4 We’ll have to remove them. You can’t bring any loose plant material into Australia. In fact, you could be fined $200 but this time we’ll simply confiscate it. Have you got any souvenirs made of plant or animal products? 39
M4 我们在曼谷机场买了这个编织的麦杆草帽和相配的手袋。我还有两个木制的鼓在那边的袋子里。 We bought this wheat straw hat and the matching handbag at Bangkok airport. We also have two wooden drums in the bag over there.
E5 The hat and the bag are okay but we’ll have to fumigate the drums and we’ll return them to you in a week or so. 25
M5 这个熏蒸消毒过程会不会弄坏鼓的？它们很值钱的。我们是从一个非常有名的声誉很好的做鼓的人那里买的这个鼓，我有收据在这里。我们需要它们为两周后的音乐会排练。 Will my drums get damaged by fumigation? They are pricey. We bought them from a very reputable craftsman and we have got the receipt here. We need them in the rehearsals for a concert we have in two weeks.
E6 No. It won’t damage them at all. We fumigate musical instruments regularly. Please fill in this form and these tags with all the details and don’t worry, they’ll be ready in time for your rehearsals. 42
M6 那好吧，谢谢，希望你们能尽快把他们交还给我。 Alright then. Thanks. I hope you can return them as soon as possible.
