我真是不明白为什么我会得乳腺癌呢？ 我一直认为癌症应该是老年人的问题。 |
I really don't understand why I got breast cancer. I always thought
cancer is a Geriatric problem. | | M1 | Breath cancer is very
common among middle age people. There many factors that can leads to
breast cancer. For example, never having given birth to a
baby, and having had problem with breast feeding. |
乳腺癌在中年人中非常普遍。有很多因素可以导致乳腺癌。例如，从未生过孩子，并且母乳喂养有问题。
| | E2 | 我还以为没什么大不了的， 我当时要是注意一点就好了，现在想来，
那些得乳腺癌的名人好像都没有生过小孩。 | No wonder I haven't
had any milk since I gave birth to my daughter. I thought it was no big
deal. Now I come to think of it, none of those celebrities with breast
cancer seem to have had children. | | M2 | Well that's not the only
reasons, modern lifestyle, dite and environmental factors can also be
risk factors. I have your X-Ray here. From these pictures we can see
there is an abnormal growth in your left breast. |
嗯，这不是唯一的原因，现代生活方式，饮食和环境因素也可能是危险因素。我这里有你的X光片。从这些图片中我们可以看到您的左乳房有异常生长。
| | E3 | 是不是把肿块切除就可以了，
我听说乳腺癌还算是治愈率比较高的癌症， 不像肝癌什么的，
得了就基本上等于判死刑了。 | if I have the lump removed,
would I be fine? I heard that the cure rate of breast cancer is higher
than others. No like liver cancer, if you get it, you will
basically be sentenced to death. | | M3 | The problem is the
growth (增生), compare with the last month this lump has grow
bigger. So Mastectomy (乳房切除术) will be the best option
for you. Otherwise the cancer cells may invade (入侵) other
tissue with in the body. |
问题是增生，和上个月相比这个肿块变大了。所以乳房切除术将是你最好的选择。否则癌细胞可能会侵入体内的其他组织。
| | E4 | 乳房切除术？ 真的需要这样么？
看来我的问题还很严重可是我不愿意做这个手术你知道这将影响到我的体型的。 |
Mastectomy? Is this really necessary? It seems that my problem is still
very serious, but I don't want to do this operation. You know it will
affect my body shape. | | M4 | Well, this is the only way to cure your
breast cancer I am afraid, otherwise Metastasis (转移) can
cause great damage to other part of your body, and could be much
difficult to control. | 好吧，
恐怕这是治愈乳腺癌的唯一方法，否则转移会对身体其他部位造成很大的损害，而且很难控制。
| | E5 | 好的那术后需要多长时间来恢复呢？ | Okay, so how long can I
recover after the surgery. | | M5 | You need to have review, within 3
month of the surgery, this is to make sure there is no Metastasis in the
peripheral areas. |
你需要在手术后3个月内进行复查，以确保没有转移到周边区域。 | | E6 |
三到六个月也不是很长的时间， 我想我可以做这个手术，
那手术会安排在什么时间呢？ 我这周五有一个很重要的会议。
我想等那以后再手术。 | Three to six months is not a long time. I think I

very important meeting this Friday and I want to wait until then。 | |

很好，但是您需要在手术前24小时禁食。 |




背景:


Breast Cancer 乳腺癌: Symptoms of breast cancer
include a lump(肿块) in the breast, bloody discharge
(出血) from the nipple (乳头) and changes in the shape
or texture of the nipple or breast.
乳腺癌的症状包括乳房内的肿块，乳头流出的血以及乳头或乳房形状或质地的改变。


Treatment depends on the stage of cancer. It may consist of
chemotherapy (化疗), radiation (方疗) and surgery.
治疗取决于癌症的发展阶段。它可能包括化疗、放疗和手术。
