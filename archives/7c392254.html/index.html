女儿水痘

相遇
12-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This dialogue is between a woman, Mrs. Wang, and her GP, regarding some rashes on her daughter’s skin.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good afternoon Mrs. Wang. This must be your daughter Lucy. How can I help you?
15




M1
我女儿发烧 37.8 度有两天了。她身上和脸上都长了很多小红点儿。但她的食欲很好，睡眠也没问题。您快帮她看看吧。我很担心。
My daughter has had a temperature of 37.8 degrees for two days now. There are many red spots over her body and face. But she still has a good appetite and has no sleeping problems. Please have a look at her. I am so worried.




E2
When did you first notice the rashes? Does she feel itchy or have any pain? I would like to find out some more details.
24




M2
昨天她从幼儿园回来说她不舒服。当时我看到她胸前有一些粉红色的皮疹。今天早上看到她脸上也起了很多疹子和水泡似的东西。我女儿觉得很痒，昨天晚上都影响她睡觉了。
She complained she was not feeling well after coming back from kindergarten yesterday. I saw some pink rashes on her chest and I found many rashes and something like blisters on her face this morning. She said they were so itchy that she didn’t sleep well last night.




E3
Let me have a look at Lucy. The rash appears in crops and papules, and there are blisters on the body, extremities and face, looking like ’dewdrops’ on a red base. She has chickenpox, which is an infectious disease.
38




M3
水痘？估计是幼儿园有其他的孩子得了水痘，我的女儿被传染了。那会不会留下疤痕啊? 要多久能好？我应该怎么在家照顾她啊？
Chickenpox? It seems that my daughter contracted chickenpox from other kids who have it in the kindergarten. Will it leave any scars? How long does it take for the disease to be cured? What do I need to do at home to take care of her?




E4
The crusts fall off in one to three weeks, leaving no scars. Please keep her away from other children until the crusts fall off completely. Give her plenty of water to drink. Keep her skin clean in order to prevent infection. Don’t let her scratch her skin.
47




M4
好的，我会的。不过要是特别痒她肯定会不停的抓的。您能给她开些口服药或者药膏吗？我不想我的女儿那么难受。
OK. I will. But she will keep scratching if it is too itchy. Could you please prescribe some oral medicine or ointment for her? I don’t want to see her suffering.




E5
I will give her some lotion and some medicine to control the itching. Lucy should start feeling better after about a week.
21




M5
好的，谢谢医生。我女儿昨天还和一个小朋友一起玩儿，希望她的朋友不会有什么事。
OK. Thank you, doctor. My daughter played with one of her friends yesterday. I hope she won’t get infected.




E6
If the fever persists or the symptoms get worse after a few days, please bring her back to see me again.
21




M6
我知道了。但愿我不用再过来见您。再见。
I understand. I hope this is the last time that I see you for this matter. Good bye.
