投资咨询

相·遇
23-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NAATI › CCL › Business
CCL 

        

        



A Mandarin-speaking customer has come into the office of an investment broker to make some enquiries about investment options.








ID
Original
Translated





M1
您好。非常感谢你这么短时间就过来见我。我过来是想聊一聊投资的事情。 首先我想知道自住房需要付资产增值税吗？
Hello. Thank you for coming to see me at such short notice. I have some investment enquiries to discuss with you. Firstly, I’d like to know if I need to pay capital gains tax on a property I’m buying to live in myself?




E1
No, you don’t and thank you for coming in today. Could you tell me what kind of plans you have for your investment?
不，你不用，谢谢你今天来。你能告诉我你有什么样的投资计划吗？




M2
那就好。我打算买些投资房， 我听说买公寓的镂花是不用付印花税的， 对吧？
That’s good. I’m planning tobuy some investment properties. I heard that you don’t have to pay stamp  for buying apartmetns off-the-plan, is that right?




E2
Yes, you’re right. You may be eligible for a First Home Owner Grant. When your property is settled, you can hand it over to real estate agents to manage. They will give you a set return on investment.
你是对的。您可能有资格获得“首次置业者补助金”。财产结算后，您可以将其交给房地产经纪人进行管理。 他们会给你固定的投资回报。




M3
那么， 投资回报一般是多少呢， 够我还房贷么？
How much return can I get? Would that be enough for me to pay my mortgage?




E3
It should be enough. ROI varies from 4% to 6%. The good thing is depreciation of your preperty and the furniture you buy can be used to offset the tax.
应该足够了。投资回报率从4％到6％不等。好处是您的财产贬值，您购买的家具可以用来抵税。




M4
那太好了。 我也在担心交税的问题， 和每个季度要填写的商业活动报表， 真是复杂啊。
That’s great. I’m also concerned about paying tax, and the Business Activity Statement that I have to fill in each quarter, it seems really complicated.




E4
You’re right. The ATO stipulates that if the turnover of a business is over $76,000, you’re advised to register for GST and lodge your BAS every 3 months.
你是对的。 澳大利亚税务局(ATO)规定，如果企业营业额超过$ 76,000，建议您每三个月注册一次商品及服务税(GST)并提交您的(BAS)营业活动报表。




M5
嗯， 我记住了。 如果净收入10万以上的话， 成立家庭信托账户比较有用， 对吧？
Yeah, I remember that. If the gross is over $100,000, a family trust would be useful, is that right?




E5
Ah, yes. You can also use your ABN for all of these. However, if you want to register a formal company, then you will need to apply for a ACN.
是的。您也可以将澳大利亚企业注册码(ABN)用于所有这些。但是，如果要注册正式公司，则必须申请澳洲公司注册号(ACN)。




M6
好的， 我想这些我都明白。 商会的人建议我可以考虑买农场， 屠宰场或葡萄酒厂， 你觉得我该选哪一个？
Ok, I think I understand everything you have talked about. Someone in the Chamber of Commerce recommended that I consider buying a farm, abbattoir or winery. Which do you think I should choose?




E6
Well I think a winery would probably be a good Choice. The market is growing exponentially.
嗯，我想酿酒厂可能是个不错的选择。市场呈指数级增长。
