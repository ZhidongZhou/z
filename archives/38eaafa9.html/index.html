耳鸣就医

相遇
30-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Ms Wang is having a consultation with an ENT Doctor, about treating her blocked ear.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
医生，我右边耳朵堵了，现在听声音很费劲。有时候还耳鸣。
Doctor, my right ear is blocked and I have trouble hearing. Also, sometimes my ears are ringing.




M1
I see. Firstly, could you tell me how long you’ve had this problem? Has it happened before? Are you in any pain?
22




E2
这是第一次，两天前开始的。 目前还不痛，可是听得不是很清楚，好像耳朵被人蒙着那样，听到的声音都变得很低沉。 这种感觉在人多、嘈杂的地方就更加明显。
This is the first time and it stared two days ago. I feel no pain yet, but I can’t hear clearly. It’s as if my ear is covered and everything sounds so low and deep. This feeling becomes worse when I am at noisy, crowed places.




M2
I see. Let me have a look at this one first. Umm, it’s inflamed. And the right one is completely blocked by wax too. And it seems the wax has been there for quite a long time. Do you clean your ears regularly?
43




E3
原来是耳屎。我还担心自己得了中耳炎， 听说中耳炎也会有类似的症状。我基本不经常掏耳屎， 因为我一直都是油耳，不是康耳。 耳朵堵住之前，我侧躺着看电视看了一天。
It’s the wax? I thought I had otitis media, as I heard the symptoms are similar. I don’t clean my ears very often, because I’ve always had oily ears not dry ones. Before my ear got blocked, I was lying on my side and watching TV for a whole day.




M3
That might be the cause. We have to put eardrops in. This is to soften the wax so that it can be removed. We can’t do it now while it is still hard.
33




E4
滴耳药水？我从来没有滴过, 去会疼吗？我直接去药房买就可以了吧？ 需要您开一张处方给我吗？
Ear drops? I have never put ear drops in before. Does it hurt? Can I get them from the chemist straightaway or do I need a prescription from you?




M4
If the wax keeps building up it causes too much pressure. It may cause damage to the ear drum and then impair your hearing. This procedure is common. It won’t be painful at all. It is a non-prescriptive medicine, so you can get it easily from any chemist.
48




E5
说实话，我挺讨厌往耳朵里滴药水。 光想想我就觉得有点毛骨悚然。一定要这样做吗？还有别的办法吗？
To be honest, I hate putting drops into my ears. It makes me creep all over just at the thought of doing so. Do I have to do this? Are there any other solutions?




M5
I’m afraid not. This is quite important for your hearing. You really must do this. Apply about five drops in both ears and leave it for ten to thirty minutes. Do that for a couple of days and come back to see me.
43




E6
请问滴了这个药水之后， 我是不是该用小棉塞堵住耳朵， 不让药水流出来？之后要怎样才能把耳屎清出来呢？
After applying, should I put a small cotton plug into my ear to stop the liquid from running out? So how is the wax removed after that?




M6
Yes. It depends on how severe the ear is impacted. In some cases, the loosened ear wax may be cleared from the ear itself. In more severe cases, we may need to remove it with a syringe.
37
