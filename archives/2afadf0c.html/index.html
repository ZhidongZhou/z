静脉曲张

ZDZ
01-08-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mr Gao (G) goes to see a surgeon (S) after being treated for varicose
veins by his G.P. * * *











ID
Original
Translated






E1
Good morning, Mr Gao. Let me read your referral letter from Dr.
Ruiz. I see, you’ve had some trouble with your legs. Tell me, what is
troubling you?
早上好，高先生，让我看一下 Ruiz
医生给您写的转介信。我知道了，您的腿有些不好。告诉我究竟是什么困扰着您？




M1
我的两腿痛得厉害。夜里经常痛得睡不着觉，有时一个晚上痛得醒过来五六次。天气变化时，我觉得更难受。
My legs are so sore. I can’t ever sleep due to the pain. Sometimes,
the pain wakes me up five or six times during the night. And I feel much
worse when the weather is changing. 




E2
Would you take your trousers, shoes and socks off, Mr Gao? I want to
have a look at your legs. Yes, you have an infection and you suffer from
varicose veins. Are you familiar with this condition?
高先生，能否请您脱去裤子和鞋袜，我想检查一下你的腿。是的，
你有感染，还有静脉曲张。您了解这样的病况吗?




M2
其实从我小时候起就一直有这个毛病了。但以前并不经常发病，而且严重程度也远远不如今天。
Actually, I have had this problem since I was little. But used to
not happen very often. And it was far less severe than it is
today. 




E3
Right. This condition is hereditary. You now need to wear elastic
stockings and take antibiotics to fight the infection. You may also need
an operation if it gets any more serious.
是的，这种病是会遗传的。现在你需要穿弹性橡皮袜，并服用一
些抗生素来抑制感染。如果再恶化下去，你可能还需要动个手术。




M3
大夫，我最怕的就是手术了。如果能用药物控制的话，请尽量避免手术吧！顺便问一下，动手术是否有后遗症呢？
Doctor, an operation is the last thing I want. If we can control it
with medication, please try to avoid an operation. By the way, will the
operation cause any after-effects? 




E4
Mr Gao, you may not have a choice. I can give you some drugs, but
only for today and tomorrow. But you have to come back to see me the day
after. If there is no improvement, you need to have your varicose veins
removed as soon as your infection is under control. 
高先生，你可能没有选择。我能给你开点药让你今明两天服用。但你后天必须回来见我，如果没有好转，一旦我们控制住了感染，就需要将静脉曲张移除。




M4
我之前一直服用抗生素，然是作用似乎不大。是不是因为我经常用抗生素，使细菌产生了抗药性呢？
I used to take some antibiotics, but they didn’t seem to be very
effective. Is it because I take antibiotics very often, so the bacteria
have gained resistance to the drugs?




E5
You are partially right. It looks as if bacteria have begun to
outwit antibiotics, and hospitals are now fighting a battle against
germs.
您说对了一部分。看起来，细菌正在逐渐战胜抗生素，医药正在跟
病毒进行着对抗。




M5
还是不动手术的好！我从来都没做过手术。
再说，手术也可能造成感染的吧？
I’d still prefer not to have the operation since I’ve never had one
before. What’s more, operations may also cause infections, right? 




E6
But unfortunately, you must have this operation, or you may run the
risk of losing your legs. Hospitals are the cleanest of places, and the
risk of microbes being transmitted from patient to patient is still
low.
但是，很遗憾，这个手术你必须得做，否则你可能会失去你的双
腿。医院是最卫生的地方了，微生物在病人中传播的可能性也很低。




M6
让我回去和家里人商量商量，再来请教您吧。您能先帮我开药吗？
Please let me go home and first discuss this with my family. Then I
will come back to you. Could you just give me the prescription?
