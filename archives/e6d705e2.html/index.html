三方财险

相遇
28-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mr. Huang is calling AAMI to make a claim.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, you’ve called AAMI. My name is Harry. How can I help you?
14




M1
你好，我叫黄晓明。我买了你们的第三方财险。今天早上发生了起事故，我想索赔。
Hello, my name is Huang Xiaoming. I purchased your Third Party Property Damage insurance policy. I had an accident this morning, so I want to make a claim.




E2
OK，Mr. Huang, could you please tell me your date of birth so I can verify your identity? Also, could you please tell me the reference number of your policy?
31




M2
我的生日是 1983 年 7 月 14 日。请稍等一下，我找一下保单号…有了，是 TPPD21853.
My birthday is the 14th of July, 1983. Please hold on, let me find my policy number. Got it, it’s TPPD21853.




E3
Yes you are holding our Third Party Property Damage Insurance. Now, Mr. Huang, could you please tell me what happened this morning?
22




M3
我住在一个带地下车库的公寓里。今天早上我开车出门时，一不小心蹭到了对面车库的门，撞凹进去了一块。
live in an apartment with an underground garage. When I drove out this morning, I accidentally scraped my car against the gate of another garage right in front, leaving a dent on it.




E4
Your policy covers you for damage caused by your car to property owned by a third party in the event of an accident. In this case, we can pay for the cost of repairing the gate. However, the cost of repair for your car is not covered.
46




M4
我知道。不过我车也没受什么损伤，就车头有一点划痕。那我是不是要付垫底
I know that. But there is little damage to my car, just a bit of a scratch on the front. Do I have to pay the excess?




E5
Yes, you need to pay the excess of $550. If you have a credit card on hand, we can process the payment right now. Alternatively, you can ring us later to update your payment information.





M5
我现在就付吧。可是，如果修理车库门所花的实际费用低于这 550块垫底费怎么办？
I’ll pay it now. But, what if the actual cost of repair is lower than $550?




E6
If that’s the case, I can simply cancel your claim and notify you. Then you can pay the owner of the garage directly. However, according to my experience, that kind of repair normally costs more than $550.





M6
我了解了。我公寓大楼管理员的电话是 0439 872 109，你可以和他联系来确定维修的费用。
I see. The number of the building manager for this apartment block is 0439 872 109. You can contact him to confirm the cost.
