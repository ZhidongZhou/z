入院手续

相遇
18-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following is a dialogue between a nurse and a patient who’s just been admitted to hospital. The nurse is taking the patient’s personal details and asking about the patient’s general state of health.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Jason, my name is Jessica. I’m a registered nurse and I’ll be looking after you today. How are you feeling at the moment?
23




M1
早上好。我现在感觉还可以。谢谢关心。因为之前没有在澳大利亚住过院，所以感觉有一点紧张。
Good morning. I am feeling fine now. Thank you for asking. But I’m a bit nervous as it’s my first time to be hospitalised in Australia.




E2
Don’t worry. There’s nothing for you to be concerned about. Now, I’ve got a few questions to ask you and some paperwork to go through for your admission. Shall we start now?
31




M2
是的。当然！你有什么问题尽管问，还好你们今天请了口译员来，不然我会很担心如何沟通的。
Yes,Ofcourse!Pleasegoahead.Iwouldhavebeensoworriedabout communicating with you if you hadn’t hired an interpreter for me today.




E3
No worries. Firstly, I’d like to take your personal details. Your surname is Lee, and your given name is Yang, am I correct? Could you tell me your marital status as well?
32




M3
是对的。不过我可以看一下你怎么拼写我的名字吗？想确认一下。哦，谢谢。是对的。我结婚了，但是太太现在在国内。
Yes, that’s right. But can I have a look at how you spelled my name? I just want to make sure. Thanks. That’s correct. I am married, but my wife is in China at the moment.




E4
Are you still living at the same address, Unit 16, 38 Adelaide St, Thomas Town? Could you tell me your date of birth and your next of kin in Australia as well as your religion, if you have one?
39




M4
好的。我还住在这个地址。我出生于 1958 年 11 月 13 号。我和我女儿住一起，我信佛教。
OK. I am still living at that address. I was born on the 13th of November, 1958. I live here with my daughter and I am Buddhist.




E5
Can you give me a few details about your medical history? Have you or any close relatives ever had hypertension, diabetes, asthma, heart disease, or cancer? Have you had any operations?
31




M5
我七年前得了高血压，两年前确诊得了糖尿病。我用严格的饮食和锻炼来控制。5 年前得了心脏病我做了一次手术。
I was diagnosed with hypertension 7 years ago and diabetes 2 years ago. I am managing these conditions with a strict diet and regular exercises. I had heart surgery 5 years ago.




E6
Are you taking any medication? Are you allergic to any drugs or food? Do you smoke or drink?
18




M6
我只是在吃一些维生素。到目前为止，我没有任何过敏的药物。我 5 年前心脏手术过后就不吸烟了，只是偶尔喝一点红酒。
I’m only taking some vitamins and I am not allergic to any medication. I quit smoking after I had the heart surgery 5 years ago but I do drink some.
