CCL老母授权

23-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NAATI › CCL
CCL

ID Original Translated
E1 Good afternoon, Mr. Lu. What can I do for you? 下午好，陆先生。我能为您做些什么?
M1 我这次来是因为我的母亲。她已经83岁了， 患阿尔兹海默症已经五年了。 最近她的病情已经恶化到她无法管理自己财政的地步了， 所以我想在她还有认知能力的时候签一份授权书， 让我做她的代理人。 The appointment is for my mother. She is 83 and has had Alzheimer’s disease for five years. Her condition is so serious that she can’t manage her finances. So we’d like her to arrange a power of attorney while she is still capable, appointing me as her executor.
E2 I see. And you have discussed this with her already? 我知道了。您已经和她讨论过了吗？
M2 是的。 上次她忘了付电话费， 结果电话线被公司切断了， 我无法打电话跟她练习， 还以为出了什么事， 吓了一大跳。 从那之后她也承认授权书有它的好处了。 Yes. She forgot to pay her telephone bill last time so the telephone company cut off her service. We couldn’t get in touch with her, so we were terrified at the thought that something bad might have happened. So since then she has also accepted that signing a power of attorney is in her interests.
E3 I’m sorry to hear about her decline. Given the circumstances, I gather you would be wanting an enduring power of attorney rather than a general one then? 听到她的衰退， 我很遗憾。 这个情况下， 我想你应该是想要一份持续授权委托书而不是一份普通的授权委托书。
M3 我并不知道授权书分为两类。 这两者之间有什么区别呢？ I didn’t know there are <>two types of power of attorney. What’s the difference between them?
E4 A general power of attorney will terminate when the person who made it loses mental capacity. An enduring power of attorney can continue to pay her bills, manage her accounts etc. on her behalf. 普通授权委托书将在授权委托书的订立人丧失精神能力时终止。 持久授权书可以代表她继续支付账单、管理账户等
M4 对我们需要的就是第二种。 这种持久授权书该如何办理呢？ 我是不是得将我母亲带到您这儿呢？ Yes, the later is what we need. How can we get a power of attorney? Do I need to bring my mother here?
E5 Yes. Your mother would need to sign the enduring power of attorney in the presence of an appropriate witness. I would have to explain the document and its implications> to her and assess whether she has understood before witnessing her signature. 是的。你母亲需要在合适的证人在场的情况下签署永久授权,。我必须向她解释文件及其含义(可能的影响)，并在她签字之前评估她是否理解。
M5 行， 您下周有没有空？ 有空我就带我的母亲到这里来。 还得麻烦您此草这份授权书。 Fine. Are you available next week? If so I’ll bring my mum here. Please also draft the document.
E6 You can download a Power of Attorney form from the NSW Guardianship Tribunal website, and get her to either fill it out at home, or fill it out here with my help. 你可以从新南威尔士州(NSW)监护人仲裁庭的网站上下载一份授权书表格，让她在家填写，或者在我的帮助下在这里填写。
M6 这份表格这么重要， 我想如果您能帮忙填的话就不太容易出错。 This form is so important that I think it would be less error if you can help us fill it out.
