初始咨询

相遇
04-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue is between Mrs. Lu, a new migrant, and Henry, a community service officer. Mrs. Lu is seeking advice regarding settling in Australia.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Mrs. Lu. What can I do for you today?





M1
你好，女士。我这个月初刚移民到澳大利亚。我儿子告诉我你可以就如何适应这儿的新生活给我一些建议。所以我跟你约了这次会面。
Hello, I just migrated to Australia early this month. My son told me you can give me some tips on how to adapt to my new life here. That’s why I made this appointment with you.




E2
Right. Everyone who’s new to this country needs to go through the process of becoming established and independent in Australia. We can give you some advice to get through this process smoothly.





M2
我想我最大的问题是英语。我一点儿也不会说，而且我觉得像我这么老的人学习英语也很困难了。
I think my biggest problem is English. I don’t speak English at all and I think it will be very difficult to learn English as I’m already this old.




E3
There is a national translation and interpreting service for non-English speakers. You are also eligible for our free Adult Migrant English Program (AMEP) since you’re holding a permanent resident visa.





M3
听起来不错。但我如何参加课程呢？课程有多长呢？我需要大老远的来市中心上课吗？
It’s good to know that but how can I join the program? Also, how long will the course last? Will I have to go all the way to the city to attend classes?




E4
Well you can find your nearest course provider in this booklet. You can study English with the AMEP for up to 510 hours or until you reach functional English, whichever comes first.





M4
其他我还需要做什么吗？我听说我需要申请一个税号。如 果我不工作的话也需要税号吗？
What else do I need to do? I heard that I need to apply for a Tax File Number. Do I have to have this number if I don’t work?




E5
Anyone who receives any type of income, including a Centrelink payment needs a Tax File Number. You can visit the website of the Australian Taxation Office to do that.





M5
你之前提到了Centrelink。那是什么？类似于中国的社保局吗？
You mentioned Centrelink before. What is it? Is it similar to the Social Security Bureau in China?




E6
Kind of, I guess. Centrelink is a government agency which delivers a range of services to the community. You need to register with them but as a newly arrived migrant, you are not immediately eligible for social security.





M6
我了解了。感谢你抽时间来见我。澳大利亚公务员确实更知道如何为人民服务啊。
I understand. Thanks for your time. It’s true that Australian public servants know how to serve the people better.
