女儿入学

Mrs. Lin is enquiring about the admission procedure for an Australian university.

ID Original Translated
E1 Good morning. Mrs. Lin. What can I help you with today? 11
M1 我的外甥女在中国，高中快毕业了，想来澳洲念大学。说实话，虽然在这呆了这么多年，我对这里的大学也不了解，所以来问问你。 My niece has almost finished high school in China and she wants to study at a university here. To be honest, even having stayed here for many years, I have little knowledge about universities here. So I came to ask you.
E2 Sure. What would you like to ask about? Is it regarding the areas of study, admission, or applying for a student visa? 24
M2 我想她已经决定要学心理学了。现在的问题是她不知道入学须满足那些条件。她也不清楚申请你们大学的流程是怎样的。 I think she has decided to study psychology. The problem now is that she doesn’t know the admission requirements. She isn’t clear on the application process for your university either.
E3 To be eligible to apply to our university for an undergraduate program, your niece must have a recognised secondary qualification or have finished a recognised foundation course. She also needs to provide evidence of proficiency in English. 37
M3 那对她的高中成绩又有怎样的要求呢？她正在备考雅思，请问要考多少分才能在你们学校学习心理学呢？ What are the requirements regarding her high school results? She is preparing for the IELTS test. What does she need to score in order to study psychology at your university?
E4 We use the Australian Tertiary Admissions Rank (ATAR) to determine whether the candidate meets our academic requirements. The required ATAR score for undergraduate psychology study is 84.5, which is equivalent to 520 points on the Chinese Gao Kao. For IELTS, she needs to get 6.5, with a minimum of 6.0 in each section. 50
M4 在我外甥女确定她满足一切条件时，她就可以递交申请了对吧？她可以在网上申请吗？申请费多少呢？什么时候递交申请为宜呢？ She can lodge her application when she meets all the requirements, right? Can she apply online? How much is the application fee? When is a good time to lodge the application?
E5 Yes, she can apply for the course online and upload scanned copies of the required documents. There is a reduced application fee of only AU$55 if you submit your application online. It is a good idea to apply at least two to three months before semester begins, in order to avoid disappointment, and to give you enough time to organise her student visa. 64
M5 申请之后她就只需要等待你们发录取意向书了吧？但我听说光凭录取意向书还不能去申请学生签证，对吗？ After that, she will only need to wait for your offer, right? But I heard that an offer alone is not enough to apply for a student visa, right?
E6 She needs to accept the offer and pay the required commencement fee and organise her compulsory Overseas Student Health Cover. Then we can issue an electronic Confirmation of Enrolment for you to proceed with the visa application. 38
M6 我了解了。我回去告诉她这些信息，看看她还有什么问题，然后再来问你。谢谢！ I see. I’ll tell her this information and see what other questions she has. Then I’ll come back to you. Thank you!
