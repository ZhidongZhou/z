误会贩毒

相·遇
31-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mrs Wang is stopped by a customs officer, who suspects she is smuggling drugs.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
喂，你们为什么把我带到这里啊，我的飞机马上就要起飞了，我错过飞机谁负责？
Hey, why have you brought me here? My plane is going to take off soon. Who’s going to take responsibility for me missing the plane?




M1
Ma’am, please calm down. The reason we brought you here was because we have reasons to believe that you were trying to smuggle drugs out of the country.
28




E2
什么！这不可能，我连烟都不抽，怎么可能碰毒品呀。你们一定弄错了。
What? That’s impossible! I don’t even touch cigarettes, let alone drugs. You must have made a mistake!




M2
According to your boarding pass, your name is Wang Chen, is that correct? And is this your bag?
18




E3
对，我叫王晨，是我的包呀。但是看着有点别扭，怎么好像被打开过，因为 我记得我的锁不是这样的。
Yes, I am Wang Chen and this is my bag. But it looks a bit odd and as if it has been opened, because I remember my lock was not like this.




M3
Really? You actually think that I will believe that? Did you pack your bag or did someone else do it for you? Or did someone give you this bag?
29




E4
我自己装的包啊，里面是一些巧克力，是带给我女儿的。我不可能给我女儿带毒品吧。你们肯定搞错了。不信我打开给你看。
It was me who packed the bag. There are some chocolates for my daughter in there. It would be insane if I brought my daughter drugs. You must be wrong. I can open it if you don’t believe me.




M4
Please go ahead. We will need to examine the contents of this bag; we have been informed that you are an international criminal, and we believe there is cocaine in it.
31




E5
放屁，我是来旅游的，我一直都是跟旅游团在一起，而且我一直是一个遵纪守法的人，怎么可能是罪犯。包打开了，你们自己看，里面都是什么。
Bullshit! I’m here for a trip and I stayed with the group all the time. Also I’ve been always a law-abiding person and there is no way I’m a criminal. Now I’ve opened the bag. You can check what’s in it.




M5
We are just following procedure; we need to go through the bag. Let me just ask you, did you lend the bag to anyone while you were in Australia?
29




E6
借给过我们一个旅游团的人，他说他东西多借我的包装下东西，我没多想就借给他了。这有什么问题嘛?
I lent it to another guy in our group. He said he had too much stuff and wanted to borrow my bag to put something in. I did not think anything of it before I lent it to him. Is there any problem?




M6
Yes, we detected traces of cocaine on the bag. And we were of the belief that you were trying to take drugs out of the country. But it is clear that we have made a mistake. Sorry, madam, you can catch your flight now.
44
