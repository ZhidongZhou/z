眼睛测试

相遇
18-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




Mr. Wang is having an appointment with Dr. Jackman, an ophthalmologist, at the public hospital for an eye test.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning. Mr. Wang, isn’t it? I am Dr. Jackman. Follow me please, and I will  check  your  vision.  Is  this  the  first  time  you  have  visited  our ophthalmology department?
30




M1
是的，我是第一次来这里。最近我看东西越来越模糊了，看一会儿眼睛就很酸，而且经常流眼泪。
Yes. It’s my first time here. My vision has become more and more blurred lately. My eyes get very sore after looking at anything for a while and I have been getting watery eyes.




E2
Okay, let’s take a look. Do you wear glasses for reading, driving, or watching TV or have you ever worn contacts?
21




M2
我没有隐形眼镜。平时一般不戴眼镜，只有看书的时候才戴，看电视也不用戴。医生，我是不是得了什么很严重的眼病呀，是白内障吗？
I don’t have contact lenses and I usually don’t need to wear glasses unless I’m reading. I don’t need them for watching TV. Doctor, do I have a serious eye condition? Is it cataracts?




E3
We won’t know until we’ve done all the tests. Now, please put on your glasses and look at the eye chart. If you can’t see it clearly, just blink and look at it again.
34




M3
我看头两排没有什么问题，但是第三排就比较费劲了，更别说第四排和最后一排了。
There’s no problem for me to see the top two lines but the third line is a bit difficult for me, let alone the fourth and last lines.




E4
OK. Please let me have your glasses. I need to measure the two lenses and then I’ll return them to you. If you can wait outside – you’ll be called when I’ve finished.
34




M4
大概要等多久？我还要回去接孩子，买菜做饭呢！还需要做什么别的测试吗？
How long do I have to wait? I need to pick up my child, shop for groceries and prepare dinner. Do I need to take any further tests?




E5
It won’t be long as all the work will be done with the assistance of a computer. After that, we’ll complete the optometry tests with you and check your eye pressure.
31




M5
眼压测试？听起来有点吓人。你能告诉我这个测试具体是怎么做的呢？会很疼吗？
Eye pressure test? Sounds a bit scary. Could you please tell me in a bit more detail how the test is done? Will it be very painful?




E6
You don’t need to be scared. It’s a very easy and safe eye procedure. A device blows air into your eye and you just keep your eye as open as possible.
31




M6
那好吧。我会尽力配合的。谢谢你医生。
All right. I’ll try my best to do as you’ve said. Thank you, doctor.
