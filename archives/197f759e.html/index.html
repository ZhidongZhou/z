开附属卡

相遇
28-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mr Chen (C), a Mandarin speaking customer, and John (J), a credit card specialist from the Commonwealth Bank. Mr Chen is enquiring about his credit card over the phone.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Thank you for calling the Commonwealth Bank Card Centre, my name is John. How can I help you today?
Hello John. I’m calling because I’d like to know whether I can apply for an additional card for my younger brother. I’d also like to know if there are any charges.




M1
你好, John。我打电话来是想问一下我是不是可以为我弟弟申请一张附属信用卡。我还想知道是否会产生费用？





E2
Definitely, there may be a cost involved depending on the type of credit card that you are holding. Now, may I have your name, date of birth and customer reference number please?
32




M2
我叫 Tim Chen。我的生日是 1980 年 11 月 26 日。客戶號碼呀，你等等…我的客户号码是 5353 8092。
My name is Tim Chan. My date of birth is 26 November 1980. My customer reference number? Just a second…my customer reference number is 5353 8092.




E3
Thank you Mr. Chen. Our records show that you have a ‘low-fee’ credit card which allows you to add one additional card holder without any extra charges.
27




M3
那真是太好了。我弟弟下個星期會来澳洲读书。我想给他一张附属卡，以备不时之需。那你能否帮到我呢？
That’s great! My younger brother is coming to study in Australia next week. I want to give him a card in case he needs it. Will you be able to help me with this now?




E4
Sorry Mr. Chen, I’m not authorised to do it over the phone for security reasons. We need to check your ID documents at a branch before we can process this.
30




M4
好，我明白了。那就等他来了之后，我再带他到你们在这附近的分行去申请吧。我还想问问这卡的限额是怎么分配的。
OK, I understand. After he has arrived, I’ll take him to the branch near our place to apply for the card. I’d also like to know how the card limit is split.




E5
The card limit will be shared between the two cardholders. That means the total amount spent by both of you must not exceed the card limit, say $7000 in your case.
33




M5
多谢你回答我全部的疑问。但听起来似乎很麻烦啊。有没有什么我要注意的地方呢？
Thank you very much for answering all my questions. But it sounds very troublesome. Is there anything else that I need to be aware of?




E6
He’ll get a card in his own name but with your account number. That means he can do most of the things you can do as the primary cardholder, such as setting up regular payments.
35




M6
我需要考虑一下。不管怎么说，非常感谢你。
I’ll need to think about it. Anyway, thank you very much!
