盗版光盘

相遇
05-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mr Philip, an officer from Customs and Border Protection and Mr. Lin, an Australian Citizen who just came back from a recent trip to China and has now arrived at Sydney International Airport.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Excuse me, sir. Can I see your passport and your Incoming Passenger Card please?





M1
怎么了? 我没带什么不合法的东西，缉毒犬也已经过来闻过一遍了，没有任何特殊的反应。你们为什么把我拦下？
What happened? I don’t have anything illegal with me and a sniffer dog has already checked around and it did not show any particular reactions. Why have you you stopped me?




E2
The X-ray shows there may be some undeclared prohibited items in your baggage. If you are caught carrying undeclared, prohibited or restricted goods, you could be fined or prosecuted. Do you understand?





M2
我既没有走私，也没带任何的违禁品。不过，您看我的行李都捆绑好了，要是把东西都拿出来再放回去是很费劲的。你们海关就是小题大做，专找我们亚洲人的麻烦。
I’m neither smuggling, nor carrying any contraband. You see my luggage has been strapped up. It will be very troublesome if I have to take everything out and put it back in again. You Customs officers are just making a fuss about everything and picking on us Asians.




E3
Do you know there are certain restricted goods you need to declare on arrival, such as firearms, weapons and ammunition, and currency of AUD$10,000 or over? Do any of those apply to you? Besides, if you are carrying certain kinds of medicines and substances, food, biological goods, or wildlife products etc, you must declare them.





M3
这样啊。我就带了一些土特产给我父亲，他说他很想家，很怀念家乡的味道。 另外还有一些光碟是给我母亲的。你把我拦下来，是因为怀疑这些光碟是盗版的吗？
I see. I only brought some local specialties for my father, because he said he was homesick and missing the local flavours of our hometown. Apart from that, there are some DVDs for my mother. Are you suspecting the DVDs are pirated? Is that why you stopped me here?




E4
It is a normal practice for customs to run random luggage checks. Those DVDs have to be forfeited as they appear to be counterfeit. So does the local special produce that you have, since they are meat products. What is in those paper bags? Can you please open them?





M4
那好吧，反正也不值什么钱。纸袋里没什么违禁的东西，就是一点草药。我妈 有慢性支气管炎，西药不管用，只治标不治本。所以我就在国内的中药店抓了一点草药给她。
Alright. Those DVDs are not worth much. There is nothing illicit in those bags, just some herbs. My mom has chronic bronchitis, but western medicine doesn’t seem to be working for her. It only relieves the symptoms but does not cure the disease completely. So I bought some herbs for her at a pharmacy when I was in China.




E5
You should have declared that traditional medicine on your Incoming Passenger Card, because they may contain endangered plant or animal products. I’m sorry, but that medicine also needs to be forfeited.





M5
请不要这样。她需要这些药来治病的。您这不是要她的命吗？如果我交罚款, 你是不是就不会没收这些药了？
Please don’t do that. They are very important for her treatment. Only if you want to kill her. Can I keep the medicine if I choose to pay a fine?




E6
I don’t think so. It is advisable that you take her prescriptions to an Australian medical practitioner. I’m sure Chinese medicine practitioners here can help her with this matter.





M6
随便你吧，看来也只能自认倒霉了。
Whatever… it seems I have to accept this as bad luck.
