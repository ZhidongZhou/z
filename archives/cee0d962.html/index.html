题号 101004


题目:

Some employers involve employees in the decision-making process of products and services. What are the advantages and disadvantages?

作答:

The controversial topic with respect to employee involved decision-making never fails to attract public attention. There has been a heated debate on whether empowering employees to participates in the company decision-making brings advantages or disadvantages. As far as I am concerned, I tend to be an advocate regarding this issue. My explanation will be illustrated in the following part of this essay.

It is reasonable for me to believe that empowering employees to participate in the company decision-making process is of great importance due to the fact that It can boost employee’s motivation.
Its can be seen that employees can feel their voice been heard and has actual impacts on the final products and services. Therefore, getting employees involved in decision-making can have positive impacts on employee’s motivation.

In contrast, there are quite a few to hold the opinion that it is of less value to involving employees in the decision-making process. This argument does not hold water at all since the employee’s motivation is directly tied to an employee’s performance. There is a finding conducted by the researchers at the University of Cambridge that has figured out more than 85% of companies doubled their employee’s performance after launched the employee empowerment program.

In a nutshell, I may safely reach the conclusion that employee involvement can boost employee’s motivation and better performance based on the examples and results of the research.
