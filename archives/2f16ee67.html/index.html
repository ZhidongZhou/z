三人租房

相·遇
10-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



Mr. Wang is talking with an agent about renting a property.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good Morning Mr Wang, how are you today?
早上好王先⽣， 你今天怎么样？




M1
早上好， 我很好谢谢你， 我今天是想问⼀下租房的问题
Morning, I’m very well thank you, today I’m here today to make an inquiry about renting a property.




E2
Well you have come to the right place, so what type of property do you want to rent? Rental prices vary greatly depending on the type of property.
好吧，您来对地方了，那么您想租什么类型的房屋？租赁价格视房产类型而异。




M2
是这样的， 我和我的两个朋友想要整租， 你们这⾥都有⼀些什么样的房产呢？
Me and two of my friends, would like to take a whole house. What kind of properties do you have?




E3
Yes, that wise decisions, if you all work and you have 3 people, it will be affordable for you 3 to rent a whole flat, we have all types of property here, such as: houses, apartment, studio（⼯作室） and granny flat (孝亲房·祖⺟房). 
是， 这是⼀个明智的决定， 如果你们3个⼈都在⼯作， 你们应该承担的起整租⼀整个公寓, 我么 有3种房产，




M3
我们需要有三个卧室的房⼦， 我们不知道是house好还是appartment， 你能分别介绍⼀下么， 我们想 要住在离东区不远的地⽅。
We are after a place with three bedrooms. But we don’t know which is the best: a house or an apartment. Can you explain a little about each? I want to live in a place not far away from the estern suburbs.




E4
No Problem, house (独⽴屋) best option for freedom, if you want live close to the CBD, you might want to choose apartment AS there are few houses in that area.
没问题，独立屋是自由性的最佳选择，如果您想居住在离中央商务区较近的地方，则可能要选择公寓，因为该地区的独立屋很少。




M4
住在中⼼商务区会⽐较贵吧， 我们打算找那些房租在每周$500 到 %600 的会⽐较好， 近⼀点也好， 但是不能超过%600， 我听说有些公寓有游泳池是这样么？
Isn’t CBD relatively expensive, we decide to looking for those rent with 5 or 6 pre moths, near is great, but not exceeding 6. And we also heard about some apartment have swimming pool, is that correct?




E5
Yes, understand, why don’t look at our list of appartment here, some are apartment do having swimming pool, but bit faraway from CBD.
是的了解， 不如你先看⼀下我的不同公寓的清单， 有些公寓的确有泳池， 不过距离中央商务区 稍微远⼀些。




M5
嗯， 靠近CBD实在太贵， ⽽且楼太⾼不喜欢， 离得远的价格便宜， 但是交通购物确实不够⽅便。
Hmm, appartments closer to the CBD are indeed too expensive and too tall, which I don’t like. I can afford the places which are farther away, but the public transport is bad and It’s not convenient for shopping.




E6
Or, you can rent a house with four rooms and swimming pool. The rent is slightly heigher than your stated amount, but you can always sublease the extra room to another person to balance your rent.
或者，您可以租一间有四个房间和游泳池的房子。租金略高于您指定的金额，但是您始终可以将多余的空间转租给其他人以平衡租金。




M6
你说的很对， 我们也可以考虑独立房, 把多余的房⼦再转租出去， 那我看⼀下你们的房屋列表吧， 谢谢 你的帮助。
You are right, we can also consider house, and then sublet the extra houses, then I will look at your house list, thank you for your help.
