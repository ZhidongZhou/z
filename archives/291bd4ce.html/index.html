老无所养

相·遇
17-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between a special benefit applicant, Mr. Li and a Centrelink officer Susan.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Mr. Li. My name is Susan. You put in a claim for a special benefit. You know you’ve come here for an interview regarding your claim, don’t you?
李先生，早上好。我叫苏珊。您提出一项特殊福利金的请求。您是知道您来这里是为这个请求进行面试的对吗？




M1
我知道， 你有什么问题尽管问好了， 反正我的材料都是真实的， 希望你能尽快做决定， 我已经等了好久了， 你们怎么这么慢呢？
I know why I’m here. You can ask any questions you want. All of my documents are genuine. I hope you can make a decision as soon as possible. I have been waiting for a long time. Why is it taking so long?




E2
We will try our best to make a decision as soon as we can. After this interview, you should hear from us in two weeks. According to your documents, you rent a place in Miranda. Is there any reason why you can’t live with your daughter?
我们将尽力做出决定。采访之后，您应该在两周内收到我们的来信。根据您的文件，您在米兰达（Miranda）租了一个地方。有什么理由不能和女儿住在一起？




M2
我前妻住在那里， 如果我再搬进去会特别不方便， 并且跟我前妻一见面就吵架， 还是不见的好。
My ex-wife lives with her. It would be very embarrassing to live with them, basides, my ex-wife and I will argue ifwe see each other, so it is better not to see each other.




E3
Fair enough. According to our records, you stopped working in 2012. How have you supported yourself since then?
有道理, 根据我们的记录，您在2012年停止工作。自那时以来，您如何养活自己？




M3
以前打工的时候存了点钱， 积蓄花完了之后， 都是我女儿在帮我。
I saved some money when I worked. After I spent my savings my daughter supported me.




E4
I see. FRom the bank statement you submitted before, you had $20,000 in your commonwealth Bank account, where did that money go
我知道了。从您之前提交的银行对帐单中，您的联邦银行帐户中有2万块钱，这笔钱去了哪里？




M4
我都花了， 我身体不好， 看医生要花钱， 我还开到了， 并且离婚也花了不少钱。
I spent all of the money. It costs money to see doctors as I am not physically fit 身体不健康  and I also had an operation. I spent a lot of money on the divorce as well.




E5
Es far as I know, it is free to have an operation. How much did you spend on your divorce?
据我了解，手术是免费的。你离婚花了多少钱？




M5
我不记得了， 反正很多钱
I can’t remember. Anyway it was a lot of money.




E6
Mr.Li, for us to make a decision, you need to submit some evidence to show where you money went. All the expenses have to be reasonable and only to meet your basic daily needs. I’ll give you 14 days to submit the supporting documents.
李先生为了我们能做出决定， 你需要提交证明， 以证明你的钱用在什么地方， 所有的开销必须都是合理的， 并且只是用于日常的基本开销。 我会给你14天的时间准备并提交这些支持文件 (辅助材料)




M6
我知道了， 我会尽快去搜集这些材料的。 我没有任何收入， 这是我唯一的希望， 你们要尽快做决定， 我的日子不好过啊。
I understand. I will gather (prepare) those documents as soon as I can. I don’t have any income. This payment is my only hope. I hope you can make a decision soon. My life is really hard.
