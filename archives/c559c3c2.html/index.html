儿童接种

相遇
18-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This dialogue is between a parent and an officer from the Australian Childhood Immunisation Register.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good afternoon, I’m Lion Hutton. You said you have some questions regarding the immunisations that your child received. What can I do for you today?
25




M1
我想我儿子已经接受了所有该做的免疫。但是上个星期五我收到这封信说还有其他种类的免疫。我感到有点诧异。
I think my son has received all the necessary vaccination shots. But I was surprised when I received this letter last Friday, saying there are other kinds of vaccinations needed.




E2
How old is your son? Do you have his immunisation record book with you now? Also, can I have a look at the letter you mentioned?
26




M2
我儿子上个星期刚满 17 个月。是的，我今天把免疫记录本带来了，还有这封信，给您。
last week. Yes, I have brought his immunisation record with me today and this is the letter. Here you are.




E3
Let me see. Yes, actually this letter is from the Australian Childhood Immunisation Register. It automatically sends out reminders to parents when their children are due for immunisation.
28




M3
但是我儿子已经做过好几种疫苗接种了。我记得第一次是在他出生的时候，以后又接种过两次。所以我认为他不需要再接受免疫了。
But my son has already received several vaccines. I remember the first shot was given right after he was born and there were two times after that. So I don’t think he needs any more vaccinations.




E4
You were perfectly right in saying that he has received several immunisations. The fact though is that certain types have to be given more than once to provide the child with sufficient immunity.
33




M4
好吧，就算某些疫苗需要接种一次以上，但需要三次吗？小孩子能受得了吗？你不认为这封信寄给我是寄错了吗？
Alright, even if some vaccinations have to be given more than once, does it have to be three times? Don’t you think it’s too much for a young kid to cope with? Don’t you think the letter was sent to me by mistake?




E5
We sometimes send 5 reminders. In most cases, it’s recommended that immunisations should be repeated several times. In the case of diphtheria, tetanus and whooping cough, the injection is given 5 times over 5 years.
35




M5
但是我小的时候都没有接受过这么多次免疫，可你看，我身体这么好，连一般的感冒都不得。
But I didn’t receive so many vaccinations when I was a kid. But you can see I am so physically fit and healthy that I rarely catch a cold.




E6
In the past, the vaccine wasn’t as available as it is now. Besides there were many deaths and illnesses in the past that could have actually been due to a lack of immunisation.
33




M6
我想你是对的。过去的医生没有合适的办法和仪器来正确诊断疾病。
I think you are right as doctors in the past didn’t have proper methods and equipment to give accurate diagnoses.
