裁员社保

相遇
12-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between James (J), a Centrelink Officer, and Mr Liang (L). Mr Liang is enquiring about his eligibility for a Low Income Health Care Card.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
不好意思啊，先生！我迟到了几分钟。是巴士晚点了，真的很抱歉。
I am very sorry sir. I’m a few minutes late. The bus was late, I am really sorry.




M1
No worries Mr. Liang. My name is James Morris. How may I help you today?
15




E2
最近我们公司需要缩减开支，必须要裁员，我也是其中之一。所以我来这看看有什么福利我可以申请。
Recently, my employer needed to reduce his expenses and had to make some staff redundant, and I was made redundant. That’s why I’m here to see what sorts of benefits I can apply for.




M2
You may be eligible for the “New Start Allowance” and a “Low Income Health Care Card”. Are you still engaged in any sort of employment, for example, working part-time or working as a casual employee?
35




E3
有呀。我仍然在我朋友的餐馆做服务员。每周工作三天。虽然薪水不算好，不过，好就好在它可以包午餐和晚餐。
Yes, I’m working in my friend’s restaurant as a waiter. I work 3 days a week. The pay is not that good but the good thing is that lunch and dinner are provided.




M3
In this case, I’m afraid that you can only apply for the latter one, which is the “Low Income Health Care Card”. It gives low-income earners access to cheaper prescription medicines, and various concessions from government and private organisations.
40




E4
听起来不错。那我该怎么申请这张卡呢？我身体不大好，所以总是要去看医生、买药什么的。如果有了这张卡，一定会帮到我很多的。
It sounds great. How can I apply for this card? I have some health issues so I always need to see a doctor and buy medication. It will help me a lot if I have this card.




M4
You may be eligible for the card if you meet the income test. For a single person, your weekly income must not exceed $497 or $3,976 in 8 weeks.
39




E5
我的收入肯定没有这个数！我还有个问题，这个卡是跟政府的保健医疗卡一样，有五年的有效期吗？
My income is definitely less than that. I have one more question, is this card valid for 5 years, just like the Medicare card?




M5
Mr Liang, you need to renew this card every 6 months and every time you are required to show evidence in order to qualify for it.
26




E6
什么，这么麻烦？那我都需要带什么材料呢？我能用我的工资单和银行对账单作为证明材料吗？你这儿有申请表吗？
What? That’s a real hassle! So what should I bring? Can I use my payslips and bank statements as evidence? Do you have the application form here?




M6
Yes, that’s fine. I can give you a copy of the application form, just a second.
16
