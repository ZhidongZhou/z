补课咨询

相遇
25-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mr. King (K), an officer of ABC College and Ms Wu (W), a Chinese student.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, my name is Thomas King. How can I help you?
早上好，我是 Thomas King，有什么可以帮你的吗？




M1
早上好，我是吴云，有人介绍我来你这里，因为我想咨询一下你们开设的辅导课程的情况。看是不是适合我。
Morning, my name is Yun Wu. I would like to enquire about what courses you have and whether they are suitable for me. Someone recommended I come here.




E2
Ok. Well, we run quite a few different short courses for students who are either returning to study or studying part-time. Um, tell me about your situation.
好的，是这样的，对于那些返校继续学习或者业余时间上课的学生，我 们是有很多不同种类的短期课程。嗯，能否告诉我你的具体情况？




M2
嗯，我认为我非常需要帮助，以便为新学期做准备。特别是树立我的信心以及帮助我有效的学习。因为，你看，我已经有将近 12 年都一直在工作，不再上课了，所以，距离我的学生时代，已经是很久以前的事了。
Well, I think I really need help in preparing for the coming semester, especially to build up my confidence and help me study effectively. Because, you see, I’ve been out in the work-force for nearly 12 years now, so it has been a long time since I was last a student.




E3
Let me have a look at what courses we have that might suit you. Are you an undergraduate or a postgraduate? Arts or sciences?
24




M3
本科生，我是商学院的。
Undergraduate, and I’m in the faculty of business.




E4
Right then. First of all, there’s our intensive ‘Study for Success’ seminar on the first and second of February. It’s aimed at students like you who are uncertain about what to expect at college.
好的。首先，我们在 2 月 1 号和 2 号有一期“为成功而学习”的强化讲 习班。它就是针对和你一样的那些不确定在大学该怎么做的学生。




M4
听起来不错。那这个讲习班会介绍哪些学习方法呢？
That sounds good. What are some of the strategies that are presented?




E5
Well, we try to cover all aspects of study. Some of the strategies in writing and reading, for example. By the way, it is very important to begin reading confidently right from the beginning.
嗯，我们努力想涵盖学习的各个方面。比如，一些关于写作和阅读的方 法和技巧。顺便说一下，从一开始就能够自信的阅读，这是非常重要的。




M5
我其实读写都不太好。我现在就非常没有自信。你能再说得具体一些吗？
Actually, I am not good at reading or writing, and I am extremely unconfident right now. Can you tell me more about this?




E6
With writing, you will learn how to organise your thinking. With reading, there’ll be sessions about how to analyse materials and tips on how to remember what you have read.
写作方面，你会学到一些关于如何完善写作计划 。阅读方面，那些课程 旨在培养你怎么分析材料，并告诉你一些小技巧，如何来记忆读过的内容。




M6
这真是太好了！这就是我最需要的。能告诉我怎样预约参加这个讲习班吗？非常感谢！
That’s excellent! That’s what I need the most. Could you tell me where I can book this seminar? Thank you very much!
