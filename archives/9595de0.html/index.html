招待国宾

相遇
25-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mr. Chen (C), a Chinese speaking staff of an international company and his Australian colleague Mr Kos (K).








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Hello Mr. Chen. Is there anything worrying you? You are not looking very well.
嗨，陈先生。你今天可以下班了吗？是不是有什么担心的事情？你看起来不太好。




M1
你好。明天有一个中国客户要来公司，老板让我帮忙定一家合适的餐馆，请中国客户吃晚饭。
Hi. a Chinese client will come to visit us tomorrow and our boss asked me to book a good restaurant to invite the Chinese client for dinner.




E2
Right. It is not that easy to choose a good restaurant which will suit both parties. Do you need some recommendations?
是这样的，要挑选一家双方都喜欢的餐馆不是一件很容易的事。需要什 么建议吗？




M2
是的。首先，餐馆的环境要适合商务会谈；其次，饭菜也得可口，服务还得非常好。
Yes, I do. Firstly, the atmosphere of the restaurant must be suitable for business conversation. Secondly, the meals they offer should be tasty and the service there must be excellent.




E3
Yes, exactly. It’s better to choose a place that you have been to before, so you don’t have to check it out by yourself before the dinner. What do you think?
是的，的确如此。最好选择一家你以前去过的餐馆，这样你就不用在晚 宴之前亲自去试吃了。你觉得呢？




M3
你说得太对了！我想我想到了一家不错的餐馆，就在 Darling Harbour 附近。不过是家中餐馆，你说行吗？
you are absolutely right! I think I have come up with a good restaurant, very close to Darling Harbour. But it’s a Chinese restaurant. Do you think it will be fine?




E4
I think I know which restaurant you are talking about now. I have been there many times. Their service is fantastic! However, the problem is, it’s too popular. So it may not be that quiet, especially at dinner time.
中餐馆? 我想我知道你说的是哪一家了。那家餐馆我去过很多次了。我喜 欢那儿的饭菜！他们的服务业很棒！只不过，问题是，这家餐馆太受欢迎了。所以，可能不是那么安静，特别是晚餐的时候。




M4
对，这是个问题。而且，请中国客人大老远得来澳大利亚吃中餐，总觉得有点 奇怪。
Yeah, it is a problem. Besides, inviting a Chinese client who has come all the way to Australia for Chinese food seems a bit weird.




E5
Well, it could be. However, if you think about it in a different way, maybe choosing a Chinese restaurant also shows our interest and respect for Chinese culture.
嗯，有这可能。但是，如果你换个角度想，这也许能表明我们对中国文 化的尊重和喜爱。




M5
有道理。这样的话，我想，如果我们能够预订到靠近窗户的位置，应该是不会太吵的。而且明天是周三，我想应该不会有太多人吧。
Makes sense. So, I think if we can book a spot near the window, it won’t be too noisy. Also, tomorrow is Wednesday, so I guess they won’t have many guests.




E6
Yes, I think that’s probably right. Ring them up and find out first. If there are seats available, I think it will be an enjoyable evening for all. Next time you’ll know how to choose an appropriate restaurant, and to take care of everything as early as possible.
嗯，我想是的。先给他们打电话问问看吧。如果他们有位置的话，我想 大家明天会度过一个非常美好的夜晚的。下次你就会知道还怎样选择合适的餐馆并尽可能尽早的照顾到方方面面了。




M6
好的，我这就打电话。太谢谢你了！你真是我今天的救星。
Sure, I will call them right away! Thank you so much! You really saved my day!
