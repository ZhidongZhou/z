父亲施暴

相遇
13-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between Mr. James, a school principal, and Mr. Luo, a father, who has been called in for an interview regarding hitting his son.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Thanks for coming in, Mr. Luo. I am the school principal, Steven James. Nice to meet you.
17




M1
您好，校长先生。您今天找我来，是为了什么事吗？是我儿子最近在学校调皮捣蛋了？
Hello, Principal. What is the problem that you need to talk to me about? Has my son been acting up at school lately?




E2
Well, Tom has been very well behaved recently. But his teacher noticed some bruises on his back while he was doing sports yesterday. Do you know anything about that?
29




M2
噢， 您是说那些淤青啊 … 那是因为他上一次考试又不及格， 我实在太生气了，就教训了他一下。
Oh, you mean the bruise … that is because I disciplined him a bit. You know, he failed the exam again, and I was so angry.




E3
By saying “discipline”, do you mean you, as a matter of fact, have physically punished your son and it was you who caused the bruise?
25




M3
你这样说，也可以。男孩子总是很顽皮的，我觉得体罚作为一种教育手段，是必要的。而且，这种瘀伤，很快就能好的。
Well, you can put it that way. Boys are often quite naughty and I think physical punishment is necessary as a disciplinary measure. Besides, he will recover soon from this kind of bruises.




E4
But physical violence against children is harmful, and it can cause serious injuries. I think that it is wrong to consider it a way to resolve issues with your child.
30




M4
怎么会呢？我小的时候做错了事、或者不听话的时候，也总是挨父亲的揍。但是您看，我现在不是好好的，也没受到任何的伤害呀？
How come? When I was little, my father often hit me if I did something wrong or behaved disrespectfully. Look, I turned out to be alright now. It didn’t do me any harm.




E5
I can’t agree to that, Mr. Luo. Violence only creates problems and conflicts, not the other way around. Children never deserve to be physically hit or abused. They will learn nothing positive from such action.
35




M5
这就是我们中国家长的教育方式。我们才不像澳大利亚的父母，让孩子们为所欲为，都把孩子给惯坏了！
This is the way we Chinese parents educate our kids. We are different from Australian parents who let their kids do whatever they want. Their kids are all getting spoiled!




E6
But I must ask you to stop such action since it is against Australian law. Otherwise you may be charged with child abuse and facing possible penalties and even jail sentences.
30




M6
真有这么严重？对不起，之前我还真不懂这些。好吧，那我以后不打他了。
Is it really that serious? Sorry, I didn’t know that. Alright, I won’t hit him anymore.
