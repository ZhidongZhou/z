体育项目

相遇
27-06-2020
Hits

CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between Mr Wang (W), a parent and Mr Hargraves (H), the sports coordinator of Burwood High School. Mr Wang recently received a letter from the school and would like to ask some questions about the sports programs at the school.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Hello, I am Mr. Hargraves, one of the teachers here. How can I help you?
15




M1
你好。我想问一下负责体育活动的Tyler 老師在不在啊？我跟他约了今天十点钟的见面。
Hi, I would like to know whether Mr Tyler, who is responsible for sports, is here or not. I have made an appointment to see him at 10 today.




E2
Oh, Mr. Tyler … He’s currently on long service leave so I have replaced him as the sports coordinator. Don’t worry! I’ll be able to help you. What’s the matter?
30




M2
我是 8 年纪B 班 Jenny 的爸爸，我姓王。我上星期三收到學校寄来的一封信，通知我说我女儿对于星期三的運動項目选择有問題。到底是什么問題呢？
I’m the father of Jenny from Year 8B. My family name is Wong. Last Wednesday, I received a letter from the school which informed me that there’s something wrong with my daughter’s Wednesday sports choices. What’s the problem?




E3
Just wait a second … I need to get her record from the computer system. Our records show that she hasn’t picked a sport to do and that seems to be the problem.
33




M3
是呀。我觉得有点贵，所以我没有让她參加。比如说游泳吧，半年就要 200块。我怕我负担不起。
Yes. I think it’s a bit expensive, that’s why I didn’t allow her to join. Take swimming as an example, it costs $200 for half a year. I’m afraid that we can’t afford it.




E4
I see. But look Mr. Wong, Jenny has to enrol in one of the sports programs. It’s the policy of the Department of Education that schools have to offer sports, including free sports, for students to participate in.
38




M4
我真不知道學校原來有免費的運動項目呀，可能我填申請表的时候没有看到吧，那么哪些项目是免費的呢？
I really didn’t know the school offers free sports. Maybe I overlooked them when I was filling out the application form. So, what sports are free?




E5
There are lots of them. For example, volleyball, badminton, table tennis and multi-sports in which students will do a different sport each week for 20 weeks.





M5
这個「多種運動」听起来不错，我想让她学习不同的运动和各自相关的规则。不知道还有没有位置呢？如果没有了，她可以去打羽毛球吗？
“Multi-sports” sounds great! I want her to learn different sports and their rules. Are there any positions left? If not, can she join badminton?




E6
Yes. Jenny can definitely join multi-sports and I’ll put her name down in a minute. Please remind her to bring a bottle of water and a towel with her every Wednesday!





M6
好。我明白。真是非常感谢。
OK. I understand. Thank you very much.
