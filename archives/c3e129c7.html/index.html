The following is a dialogue between a doctor and a patient with hyperthyroidism.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good afternoon Mrs. Wang. What seems to be the problem?
10




M1
医生，最近我容易发火并且心慌。以前没有过这样的感觉，只是近两个月才有的事。不知道是什么引起的。
Doctor, recently I’ve been losing my temper easily and have palpitations. I’ve never had that kind of feeling before. It just started happening in the last two months. I don’t know why.




E2
Have you lost any weight recently? Have you been sweating as much as you usually do? Have you been sleeping okay?
21




M2
尽管我饭量增加 ，两个月来，已经瘦了四公斤了。我比以前出汗出得多了，简直无法忍受热的天气。有的时候晚上会失眠，但不是每天都这样。
I eat more than usual but I’ve still lost 4 kilograms in the last two months. I sweat more than before as well, and I can’t stand hot weather. Sometimes I have difficulties falling asleep at night but not every day.




E3
Let me examine you. Swallow please. There are some tests you will have to get done and we will see you in one week. At that time we will start on some more definite therapy for your condition.
36




M3
谢谢您，医生。请您告诉我我得了什么病？但愿不是很严重。
Thank you, doctor. Please tell me what kind of disease I have. I hope it is nothing serious.




E4
I suspect that you might have hyperthyroidism. We need to arrange a time for you to have some thyroid function tests done.
22




M4
医生，最坏的结果会是什么？甲状腺出问题可以采用药物治疗吗？我不想做手术。
So doctor, what could be the worst scenario? Can thyroid conditions be cured by medicine? I don’t want to have an operation




E5
I can’t make a diagnosis before getting the test results. But of course, medication such as anti-thyroid drugs is an option. The major disadvantages of medicinal treatment are the long treatment time and the high chance that the disease will reoccur.
42




M5
我明白了。那药物治疗是不是还有什么副作用？ 那如果必须做手术，会是什么样的手术呢？肯定会在颈部留下伤疤的吧？
I see. Does the drug therapy also cause some side effects? If there has to be surgery, what kind of surgery will I need to have? It will definitely leave a scar on my neck, right?




E6
Yes, that’s why we don’t suggest drug therapy. As for surgery, I’m afraid it will leave a scar on your neck. The worst scenario for your condition is a subtotal thyroidectomy. But Mrs Wang, don’t worry at this stage. We will talk about all the options that are open to you after we get the results.
57




M6
谢谢您，医生。我会先去安排检查的时间，希望没什么大碍。等我拿到结果再来找您。
Thanks, doctor. I will go schedule a test first. I hope it’s nothing serious. I’ll come back to you as soon as the results come out.
