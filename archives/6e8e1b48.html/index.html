组织野营

A parent has received a notice from the school about a school camp. He was worried and made an appointment to see the headmaster.

ID Original Translated
E1 Nice to meet you, Mr. Hu. I understand that you’re worried about the camp for the Year 7 students. 19
M1 我当然担心了！我就这么一个女儿，我得充分确保她的安全！这野营是必须的吗？ Of course I’m worried. She is my only daughter. I have to make sure she is completely safe. Is this camp compulsory?
E2 No, not at all. However, we would like all the students to go. This is an educational activity and it’s also an important part of the school curriculum. 28
M2 多花点时间在学校学习不是更有教育意义吗？对我来说，这似乎是浪费时间与金钱。组织野营活动到底有什么意义？ Isn’t spending more time on studying at school more educational? To me, it seems a waste of time and money. What’s the point of arranging this camp?
E3 It is not going to be a holiday. The children will be learning a great deal on the camp. It offers a new learning experience, which is different from traditional classroom learning. They will live on a farm for three days, observing and experiencing what farm life is really like. 50
M3 在一个农场住三天？学校难道就没有比去农场更好的事情做了吗？这个农场有什么特别的？ Living on a farm for three days? Doesn’t the school have better things to do than visiting a farm? What’s special about this farm?
E4 It’s a mixed farm, specially set up to show children the different kinds of farms there are and all the daily activities that happen on a farm. Children can see for themselves how different animals are cared for and they even have a chance to help milk the cows. 49
M4 听起来如果我女儿参加的话，会玩得很开心。我带她去了今年的复活节农展会，她很喜欢。不过，这样的经历真的是学校教育的必要组成吗？ It sounds like my daughter will have fun if she goes. I took her to this year’s Easter Show, she enjoyed it very much. But is this experience really a necessary part of school education?
E5 Most of the children in urban schools drink milk and eat bread and eggs everyday but they have no idea where these things come from. They can find out all the information first hand through personal observation and experience. 39
M5 你说得对。这可比单纯从书本上来获得这些知识要好得多。可是我女儿对乳制品和鸡蛋过敏，我们必须非常小心她的饮食。 You are right. It’s so much better than merely learning about this kind of information from books. But my daughter is allergic to dairy and eggs. We have to be very careful about her food and drink intake.
E6 If that really worries you, you can choose not to let your daughter go. As I said before, we are not forcing any of the students to go. 28
M6 我确实不愿意让我女儿冒这个风险。但我会回家先问问她的意见，再来回复您。谢谢。 I really don’t want my daughter to take the risk. But I will talk to her first at home before getting back to you.
