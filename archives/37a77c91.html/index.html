CCL家庭暴力

相·遇
24-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NAATI › CCL › Domestic
CCL 

        

        



A Chinese woman, Mrs. Qian, has been helped by a women’s refuge to obtain a temporary intervention order against her husband because she claims he has been beating her up. She is now living with friends at a secret address, and has gone back to see the lawyer, Dane Gray, to discuss what needs to happen next.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, The court granted the intervention order last Wednesday. Has your husband tried to contact you since then?
19




M1
他尝试过。上个周五他送花到我朋友的餐馆，我朋友把花交给我了。
He has. Last Friday he sent flowers to my friend’s restaurant and my friend gave me the flowers.




E2
He’s not supposed to do that. Once the Apprehended Domestic Violence Order has been served on him, he’s not allowed to make any attempt to contact you. Does he know where you live now?
34




M2
不知道，要是他知道了，我就永远躲不开他了。可能上个周五他还没收到 法院的信吧。也许警察联系不上他。
No, he doesn’t. If he knew, I would never get rid of him. Maybe he hadn’t received the letter from the court last Friday. Maybe the police couldn’t get in touch with him.




E3
I’ll find out when he actually received it. If he sent you the flowers after the order had been served on him, he’s in breach of the order and we need to go straight back to court.
37




M3
你看，我也不想他受到什么惩罚，我只想他离我远点就可以了。你说我们 还要去一次，是为什么？
Look, it’s not that I want him to be punished. I just want to keep him away from me. You’re saying we have to go to the court again. What’s that for?




E4
The order was just an interim order to keep your husband away until the court could consider the case fully. When we go back next week, we’ll have to present more evidence.
33




M4
可是上次出庭的时候我把我的地址 – 旧的那个 – 交给法官。要是这次要给 他新的地址，我丈夫就知道我住在哪里了。他会来抓我的。
But last time I gave my previous address to the magistrate. If I provide my new one, my husband will know where I live. He will come and get me.




E5
Don’t worry. The court will let you write your address on a piece of paper and hand it to the magistrate.
21




M5
对不起，我总是很紧张，很害怕。可我再也不想被他打了。下次出庭前我 们需要什么证据呢？
Sorry I’m always nervous and terrified. I don’t want to be beaten up. What evidence do we need to prepare for the next hearing?




E6
Evidence that proves he did actually attack you physically –. Did you ever go to see a doctor after one of these incidents?
23




M6
我是有这样的证据。昨天我在看我们蜜月的照片，我穿了件无袖上装，两 个胳膊上都能看到瘀青。连我们度蜜月的时候他都打我，你能相信么？
I  have this sort of proof. While I was looking at our honeymoon pictures yesterday, I noticed I was wearing a sleeveless top and you can see bruises on my arms. Can you believe he even beat me on our honeymoon?
