土澳之旅

A mandarin speaking travel agent is now visiting an overseas travel agent, enquiring about information regarding an eco-tourism resort in the world heritage rainforest area in Far North Queensland.

ID Original Translated
E1 谢谢你今天来见我。我知道在每年的这个时间你有多么的忙。 Thank you for meeting me today. I know how busy you are around this time every year.
M1 It’s a pleasure. You mentioned that you’d like to get some information about eco-tourism possibilities. What would you like to know exactly? 22
E2 我有几个问题想咨询你。首先，有些人想参加美丽自然风光的旅行团。这个团的人对自然和当地传统文化非常敏感。你这方面有什么好介绍吗？ I have several questions. Firstly, some of our guests want to join a tour to appreciate the natural beauty there. They are very interested in the natural scenery and local traditions and culture. What would you recommend?
M2 This kind of tourism has become extremely popular in the last few years. There’s a fantastic new resort up in the Daintree rainforest. 23
E3 他们提供什么样的住宿呢？我的客人喜爱美丽的自然风光，但他们确实也不想睡在外或者条件很差的地方。 What kind of accommodations do they provide? Even though my guests love beautiful natural scenery, they don’t want to camp in the open-air or stay in a run-down place.
M3 This resort is world class. It has a four and a half star rating and the restaurant offers fresh fish from the Great Barrier Reef. The guests stay in air- conditioned villas with an en-suite bathroom. It also has two swimming pools and a spa that’s open 24 hours. 48
E4 听起来好极了。可以给游客提供什么样的活动呢？在热带雨林穿行安全吗？那里有没有毒蛇或鳄鱼？ 虽然是想亲眼看看野生动植物，但还是安全第一。 Fantastic! What activities are provided for tourists? Is it safe to walk through the tropical rainforest? Are there any poisonous snakes or crocodiles? We’d like to see some wildlife, but we have to put safety first.
M4 The cost of basic accommodation covers a lot of extras, including walking in the rainforest with a local Aboriginal guide and a full-day four-wheel drive tour. It’s very safe and they can go on a river cruise. So if they’d like to see crocodiles, that’s also easy to arrange. 49
E5 你提过大堡礁。游客在那里可以进行哪些活动呢？我知道那是一个潜水胜地，但这些旅客并不很有兴趣。 You mentioned the Great Barrier Reef. What can tourists do around there? I know it is a great place for diving, but they are not very interested in that.
M5 In addition to diving, guests can go swimming and fishing there or if they’d prefer, they can go horse riding and then come back and enjoy a free massage and spa treatment. 32
E6 最后一个问题，有机会了解土著人的艺术和文化吗？我之前体验过回旋镖，那确实令人印象深刻啊。 Last question: Do they have the opportunity to learn about Aboriginal art and culture? I once tried throwing a boomerang, which I have fond memories of.
M6 Yes, of course. The local Aboriginal people run various workshops and they are very welcoming of visitors. 14
