彩票理财

相·遇
28-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



A Mandarin speaking client is asking a financial advisor about how to invest his money.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good afternoon, Mr. Su. Please come in. I am the bank manager here. Now, what can I do for you?
20




M1
我从来都没有想过我会向别人请教如何花钱，但我最近看了很多文章，发现专业的理财还是很有必要的。所以我来找你。
I never thought that I would ask people about how to spend money. But I’ve read a lot of articles recently and found professional money management is quite necessary. That’s why I’m here.




E2
Sure I will try my best to assist you. By the way, I can tell that you are a business-minded person, am I right? First, can you tell me how much money you have to invest?
36




M2
其实我没做过生意，但是我上周很幸运的中了彩票，得到了 6 万多块钱。我想那其中的 5 万来投资。
Actually, I’ve never had my own business. But luckily, I won the lottery last week and got a little over $60,000. So I want to take $50,000 of it to invest.




E3
There are a number of investment options you could consider, including investing in shares, although this is not a good idea at present. Other options could be a savings investment account or buying various bonds.
35




M3
确实，最近的全球金融危机和欧洲的债务危机基本已经打消了我投资股票的念头。你能跟我说说其他的一些选项吗？
Indeed, I have given up on the idea of investing in shares due to the recent global financial crisis and European debt crisis. Can you tell me other options?




E4
Certainly. Government bonds are an outstanding investment for cautious investors, at least in this country. They are a good way to save for your retirement, children’s education, an overseas holiday and many other things.
34




M4
那储蓄投资帐户是什么呢？这里的柜员常常向我推荐，但我总是谢绝了。
So what is the savings investment account? The tellers here have recommended it to me many times, but I always respectfully declined their offer.




E5
A savings investment account is ideal for those people who still need to withdraw on the amount they’ve invested. All you need is to deposit $200. The interest is calculated on a daily basis.
34




M5
如果我需要取钱，我需要等待多久呢？你们这里的定期存款项目是怎样的呢？
If I need to make a withdrawal, how long do I need to wait before I can get the money? How about the term deposit scheme here?




E6
You can make a withdrawal at any time. A term deposit is ideal if you have money to invest and are prepared to commit it for a certain period in order to earn higher interest.
33




M6
非常感谢你的详细解释。不过我觉得我还需要更多时间来考虑，然后才能决定。我过两天再来见你，好吗？
Thank you for your detailed explanation. However I still need some time to think it over before making a decision. I will come to see you again within a couple of days. Is that ok?
