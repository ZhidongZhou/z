移民专业

相·遇
30-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between Jason, a migration agent (MA) and Mr. Wang, a recently graduated international student.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, Mr. Wang. My name is Jason, and I’m a migration agent. I understand that you came here because your student visa is going to expire shortly. Is that right?
31




M1
是的，你好。我非常担心，我已经毕业了 2 个月了。下个月我的学生签证就 过期了。我想一直留在这里工作生活，但是我学的专业不能移民，现在也找 不到工作。我可不想这么快就回去。
Yes, I’m very worried. I graduated two months ago and my student visa is expiring next month. I’ve always wanted to stay here. But I don’t qualify for immigration because of my major and I can’t find a job now. I don’t want to go back home so soon.




E2
Don’t worry, Mr. Wang. I’m here to help you with this. But firstly, please tell me more about your education background and the exact date of expiry of your student visa.
31




M2
希望你们真的能帮到我。我是 2010 年 2 月 24 号持 573 学生签证来澳洲读书 的，我在 UNSW 学建筑学的本科。
I hope you can help me out. I came to Australia for study on a 573 visa on 24th February 2010. I did a Bachelor of Architecture at the University of NSW.




E3
Umm … Let me have a look at your file. Yes, your student visa is going to expire next month, and you are not entitled to apply for a Skilled GraduatesTemporary Visa, since your occupation is not on the Skilled Occupation List.
43




M3
我来澳洲的时候我的专业还是在这个列表里的，但是去年 7 月份这个职业就 不再是移民专业了。所以我现在处境很艰难，而且我现在也很难找到工作申 请工作签证。我还能怎么办呢？
My occupation was on the list when I arrived but it hasn’t been since July last year. So I’m in a very difficult situation. Besides, it’s very hard for me to get a job to apply for a working visa. What else can I do?




E4
My suggestion is that you apply for another course which matches the SOL. For example, you can apply for a TAFE course closely related to your occupation such as welding, bricklaying, tiling and so on. Then you may have a better chance to secure permanent employment as well as to apply for your permanent residency after you graduate.
58




M4
啊？这些在中国都是低等且没前途的工作，干这个会给人笑的。而且这些工 作干一辈子也赚不了多少钱啊，我父母是不会同意的。
What? In China, these are all considered inferior jobs with no real future prospects. I will be looked down upon if I pick on of those professions. Also, even if I do this job my life, I won’t earn much. My parents will never agree.




E5
Mr Wang. You shouldn’t be biased towards these occupations. In Australia, every occupation is treated equally. Occupations that I have mentioned above are all on the SOL, and you can earn a considerable income if you have one of those jobs. Most importantly, you can easily get a permanent resident visa under any GSM Scheme or Employer Nomination Scheme.
59




M5
是这样啊，那我知道了。那未来几年移民政策还会变么？这些职业该不会很 快又不在列表上了吧。
All right, I understand. Will there be other changes to the immigration policy in the near future? Is it possible that those occupations will be removed from the list soon?




E6
At this stage, I can tell you all these occupations are still in high demand. However, I cannot guarantee no change will occur, because the SOL is subject to a series of variations. We are not responsible for any future changes to the list.
44




M6
这就是我担心的。我第一次来的时候我也以为我的专业在这个国家，尤其是 偏远地区还是很急缺的。谁知后来说变就变了。算了，我还是再去别家中介 问问看吧。
That’s my concern. When I first came here I thought my occupation was in high demand, especially in remote areas. Who could predict the sudden change that followed? Never mind, I’d better go to some other agents and make some enquiries.
