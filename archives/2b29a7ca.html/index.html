男女朋友

相遇
16-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between a social worker and a girl with some interpersonal communication problems.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning. I’m Mathew and I’ll be assisting you today. So what have you made this appointment for?
早上好。我是Mathew，今天将为您提供帮助。那么，您此次预约的目的是什么呢？




M1
我对自己的状态很不满意。 我觉得很难想别人敞开心扉。 我想我应该要提升一下自信。
I’m very unsatisfied with myself. It is difficult for me to open myself up to other people. I think I need to boost my self-confidence.




E2
Can you tell me a little bit about yourself? What’s your current situation? Do you live at home?
你能简单介绍一下你自己吗? 你现在住在家里么?




M2
是的， 我现在和我的男朋友Sam一起住。 我和他在一起很长时间了， 关系很稳定。
Yes, I live with my boyfriend Sam. We’ve been together for a long time and it’s a pretty stable relationship.




E3
Ok, You were telling me about your thoughts and feelings and about how you can’t open up. Do you feel that’s affecting your relationship with Sam, considering he is the primary person in your life?
好的，您是在告诉我您的想法和感受，以及您如何无法敞开心扉。考虑到他是您生活中的主要人物， 您是否认为这会影响您与Sam的关系？




M3
嗯 ··· 我想是的。 现在你这么说了我想是的。 Sam 又时会怀疑是否应该相信我。 比如我对他说我爱你， 他不知道是不是真的， 因为我对于表达自己的感受有困难。
Hmm… I think so. Now that you mention it, I do feel it might be like that. Sam would sometimes doubt whether he should trust me. For example when I tell him I love you, he would wonder whether it is true, because I have difficulty expressing my feelings.




E4
Ok, do you think the issue is with yourself or Sam? Do you think he is struggling to open up to you or?
好的，您认为问题出在您自己还是Sam？ 您是否认为他正在努力向你敞开心扉？




M4
我觉得他不觉得对我敞开心扉很困难。 我只是不确定敞开心扉是不是一件好事。 他总是说我不跟他说自己生活中发生的事， 这说明我不信任他。 但我并不是这么想的。 我只是觉得这是个人的事， 我不想和别人说。
I don’t think he is struggling to open up to me. I’m just not sure whether opening up is a good thing. He always says that I don’t tell him about what’s happening in my life, hwich means I don’t trust him. But that’s not true. I just think this is personal stuff, which I don’t want to share with others.




E5
I think we need to sort this issue out with your boyfriend if he’s stuggling to deal with the fact that you can’t open up to him. We may need to have a consultation with both of you because it is clear to me that he doesn’t understand your feelings. Would you agree?
我认为我们需要解决你男友是否因为你不能敞开心扉而感到挣扎这个事情。 我们可能需要给你们两位进行一次会诊， 以为在我看来他不理解你的感受。 你同意吗？




M5
是的， 我觉得他并不理解我所经受的痛苦。 他并没有意识到这是我的选择。
Yes, I don’t think he understands what I’ve been through. He doesn’t realise this is my choice.




E6
Yse, absolutely. We should all have freedom to make our own decisions. Would you like to have another appointment with just me next session or you want to bring Sam here as well?
是的， 完全正确。 我们有自由做出自己的决定。 我希望下次的见面带上SAM, 还是只有你和我？




M6
就咱俩吧。 我还没有准备好跟Sam谈这个事， 我还需要一点时间
Just you and me. I am not ready to talk about this with Sam. Ineed more time.
