楼梯摔伤

相遇
12-07-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




The following dialogue takes place between a specialist and a patient, Mrs. Li, who has just broken her hip and arm.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Mrs. Li. I’m Doctor Clark, an orthopaedic surgeon. How can I help you today?
16




M1
我从楼梯上摔下来，当时顿时觉得左胯部疼得厉害，根本没法自己站起来。现在还是觉得很疼。
I felt a sharp pain in my left hip after I fell down the stairs. I couldn’t stand up by myself. Now it still hurts.




E2
When did it happen? Have you taken any painkillers? If not, how did you manage the pain?
17




M2
昨天下午发生的。我什么药都没吃，我的家庭医生把我转介到这里的。主要是伤到了我的左胯部还有左臂。
It happened yesterday afternoon. I didn’t take any medicine. My family doctor referred me to you. My left hip and left arm were seriously injured.




E3
The x-ray and MRI results show a fracture in your left arm. To make sure the bone heals properly, I suggest you be admitted to the hospital. You need an operation to fix it. Is your left elbow still in pain?
41




M3
是的。受伤后左胳膊僵硬，发紫发胀。有时候还觉得很麻。什么都做不了,现在只能先在家。而且就算什么都不做，伤处都会疼。
Yes. I felt stiffness in my left arm and it was swollen and turned blue. Sometimes I feel numbness. I’m not able to do anything now and have to stay at home for the time being. The injured parts are still in pain even when I stay still.




E4
We will give you an “L” type plaster cast on your elbow and shoulder. You will have to have it for about six weeks.
24




M4
除了带石膏托外，我还需要注意什么吗？这是我第一次受这么严重的伤。
Apart from wearing the plaster cast, what else do I need to pay attention to? It’s the first time that I’ve gotten injured so badly.




E5
When you rest in bed, please keep your elbow higher than your heart. Move your finger joints from time to time. Keep an eye on the colour of your fingers. If you have pain and numbness or if the colour of the fingers turns paler or darker, please come back to see me straight away.
55




M5
好的，我知道一旦伤到骨头就很麻烦，需要很长时间才能恢复。我还想麻烦您一件事，我以前从来没住过院，您能不能帮我给医院打个电话，联系住院的事，或者让他们帮我安排一个翻译？
Alright. I know that if you have broken bones it is a big hassle and will take a long time to heal. I would like to ask you to do me another favour. I’ve never been admitted to hospital before so could you please call the hospital to make the necessary arrangements for me or ask them to hire an interpreter for me?




E6
No worries Mrs. Li. I’ll send the results back to your family doctor and either your GP or I will ring the hospital for you. I believe the hospital has an interpreting service available.
35




M6
多谢您了，大夫。
Thank you, doctor.
