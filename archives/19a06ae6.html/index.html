Mrs.Guo, who previously suffered from lumbago, is talking to her GP again.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning Mrs. Guo. How are you feeling? Has the pain in your lower back improved?
22




M1
早上好医生。我的腰还是老样子。每次咳嗽、打喷嚏都会疼，只能天天吃 止痛药。您让我去拍 X 光，又叫我去做骨密度测验，结果出来了吗？
Morning. It is still the same. It kills me every time I cough or sneeze, so I have to take painkillers every day. You asked me to do an X-ray and a bone density test. Have you gotten the results?




E2
Yes, I received the results three days ago. Based on the X-ray and bone density test result, you’re suffering from osteoporosis-induced fractures in the spine.
25




M2
骨质疏松？啊，对了，上次您提到过这个可能性的。那天回家后我儿子帮 我上网查了一下，说那是缺钙引起的。
Osteoporosis? Ah yes. Last time you mentioned the possibility of this. I asked my son to do some online research for me and he said it is caused by a lack of calcium.




E3
Precisely. The lack of calcium in your bones makes them weak and more susceptible to fractures. Your persistent back pain was a result of fractures in your spine.
28




M3
这个病该怎么治呢？我儿子让我吃钙片， 说这样能让我以后不会轻易骨 折。我现在一天吃一片，您说这有用吗？
How do you treat this condition? My son asked me to take calcium supplements, saying they would prevent me from breaking my bones easily. I take one pill per day. Do you think it helps?




E4
Calcium supplements can indeed help to reduce further fractures, but its effectiveness is relatively low. I will also prescribe some other medication to strengthen your bones.
26




M4
唉，我最近吃的药片越来越多了。那这个药怎么吃？有什么副作用？我怕 吃这么多药，虽然老毛病治好了，又引起新毛病。
I’m having more and more pills. How should I take the medicine? Are there any side effects? I’m worried that the pills will solve the old problems while causing new ones.




E5
You take this medication once a week on an empty stomach. You will need to avoid food or lying down for the half an hour following. It may cause reflux, so I would recommend you cease taking your anti-inflammatory medication.
40




M5
可是我的腰还是疼啊。扑热息痛我还能不能继续吃？或者您再给开个什么 药？
But my back is still hurting. Can I continue to take paracetamol? Or can you prescribe something else?




E6
You can continue to take paracetamol as long as you do not exceed 4g every 24 hours. As for your back pain, I’ll prescribe an analgesic patch for you, which does not interact with your new medication.
37




M6
好的， 谢谢您， 希望能把这腰疼治好， 不然这平常什么都做不了。
All right, thank you. Hopefully, the back pain will go away soon, otherwise I won’t even be able to perform daily activities.





背景:
Lumbag: 腰痛 /lʌm’beɪgəʊ/ A common, painful condition affecting the lower portion of the spine (脊柱).
Low back pain is caused by injury to a muscle (strain) or ligament (sprain). Common
causes include improper lifting, poor posture, lack of regular exercise, a fracture, a (ruptured disc 腰椎间盘突出) or arthritis (关节炎).
Often, the only symptom is pain in the lower back.
People may experience:
Pain areas: in the back, muscles and bones, hip, or leg
Sensory: leg numbness or pins and needles
Also common: back joint dysfunction or muscle spasms
Most low back pain goes away on its own in two to four weeks. Physiotherapy and pain
relievers can hel . A few cases may require surgery
