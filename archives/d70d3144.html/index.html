临时入住

相遇
20-06-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a dialogue between Mr. Li, a Chinese-speaking guest, and Herbert, a hotel concierge. Mr Li has just arrived at the hotel and wants to check in.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good Afternoon! My name is Herbert, how can I help you, sir?
12




M1
不好意思， 我们没有提前做预订， 但是， 我们想要一个带浴室的双 人房，有漂亮的风景的。不知道你们有没有这样的空房？
Sorry, we didn’t make a booking in advance. But we’d like to have a double room with a private bathroom and a nice view. Do you have such a room available?




E2
Yes, we still have one double room with a harbour view available. It’s on the top floor with a bath and a shower. How many nights will you be staying with us?
32




M2
听起来很不错。 我们今晚肯定是要住这里， 或许还有明天晚上， 这 要看你们提供的价位怎样。还有，你们接受运通卡吗？
Sounds good. We’ll stay here definitely tonight and perhaps tomorrow night as well. It depends on the price you offer. One more thing, do you accept American Express?




E3
The room costs $200.00 per night for two people. We can provide an extra bed if it’s for three guests, but you need to pay an additional $50.00 per night for that. And yes, we do accept American Express.





M3
就我跟我朋友两个人。 这个价位可以接受。 那我们就定两晚吧。 给 你。 对了，今天下午我们想在这个城市周围逛逛， 而且我们晚上想 吃海鲜。你推荐我们去哪里呢？
It’s just the two of us, myself and my friend. The price sounds reasonable so we would like to stay two nights. By the way, we’d love to stroll around the city and have some seafood for dinner. Do you have any recommendations?




E4
You can catch a free tram called the city circle near the main entrance of the hotel. It will take you to several sightseeing spots around the city. Our hotel’s restaurant offers a fantastic seafood buffet dinner. It costs $60 per person, but you will receive a 20% discount as you are staying as guests at the hotel.





M4
我在旅行指南上看到过你们酒店的海鲜自助， 据说还挺受欢迎的， 或许我们今天应该试试。 能否告诉我，从你们酒店这里到机场要多 长时间？
I know your hotel’s seafood buffet is quite popular. I have read about it in a tourist guidebook. Maybe we should give it a try today. How long does it take to get to the airport from here?




E5
It usually takes only half an hour but I suggest you allow an hour just to be on the safe side. May I have your card please, and would you please print your full name and address on this form?





M5
给你。当然， 都写好了。 所以后天我们会退房。 您可以帮我们在那 天叫一辆九点钟的出租车吗？我们能不能有一个早上六点的叫醒服 务?
Here you are. Sure. All done. So we will check out the day after tomorrow. Can you help us book a taxi for 9am on that day? And can we have a morning call service at 6am as well?




E6
Certainly. Oh, one more thing. We’ve had some issues in the past with the bath. It only takes 3 minutes to fill so please watch it so it doesn’t overflow. You will have to pay for any water damage if the carpet gets wet.





M6
谢谢你的提醒，我会小心的。
Thanks for warning us. We’ll be careful.
