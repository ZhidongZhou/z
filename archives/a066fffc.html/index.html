托儿咨询

相遇
01-08-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mrs. Meng (M) a new migrant and a counsellor (C) from VIC Department of Education and Early Childhood Development.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Good morning, welcome to the Department of Education and Early Childhood Development. My name is Lisa Green. I am a counsellor here, nice to meet you.
早上好，欢迎来到教育与幼儿教育部。我是 Lisa Green，我是这里的咨询顾问。很高兴见到您




M1
噢，你好，很高兴见到您。我和我丈夫刚刚拿到了永居签证。所以我想了解一下澳大利亚的教育和儿童照管服务。
Oh, hello, nice to meet you, too. My husband and I have just received our Australian permanent residency, so I want to learn a little about Australia’s education and child care.




E2
Sure, I can definitely help you with that. First, could you tell me how many children you have and how old they are?
当然，这方面我肯定能帮到您。首先，请告诉我您有几个孩子？ 他们都多大了?




M2
我有一个男孩儿和一个女孩儿。我儿子三岁了，但我女儿今年七月份就满 6 周岁了。我周一到周五都得上班，所以我白天的时候不怎么有空照顾他们。
I have a boy and a girl. My son is three, but my daughter is turning six in July this year. And I have to work from Monday to Friday, so I don’t have time to look after them during the day.




E3
For your son, there are many types of full-time and part-time child care services available for children too young to go to school, as well as for school age kids outside of school hours.
针对你儿子的情况，我们有多种类型的全日制和非全日制的托儿服 务。这些服务是为学前儿童以及课余时间的学龄儿童提供的。




M3
据我所知，好像是有很多不同种类的托儿服务机构，对了，还有幼儿游戏小组呢，它们都各自提供什么样的服务呢？我可以在黄页电话簿中找到他们的联系方式吗？
As far as I know, there are many different types of child care agents. Right, there are also playgroups. What kind of services do they each provide? Can I find their contacts in the Yellow Pages telephone directory?




E4
Yes, you can. In Australia, there are preschools or kindergartens, which prepare children for school; child care or day care centres, which look after younger children; and family day care. And playgroups are where parents bring their children along so the children can learn to interact with other children and the parents can chat and share information.
是的，可以。在澳大利亚，有为学龄前儿童上学做准备的幼稚园和学前班，照顾婴幼儿的托儿所或日托中心，和家庭日托中心。幼儿游戏小组是指，让各位家长和其孩子们聚在一起，让孩子学习如何相处，并 且让家长们聊聊天、分享信息。




M4
我现在还在上 AMEP 的英文课，我班上也有一些同学和我情况差不多。他们告诉我说，我有可能有资格获得免费的学龄前儿童托儿服务。这是真的吗？
Currently, I am still a student in the Adult Migrant English Program (AMEP). Some of my classmates are in a similar situation to me. They told me that I may be eligible for free child care for my kids under-school-age. Is that true?




E5
Yes. However, if you progress to a different study program after completing your AMEP entitlement, it is important to review child care payment arrangements as other programs do not cover these costs.
是的。但是，当您完成了 AMEP 课程，进升到不同的学习计划后，您便要为您的托儿服务另作安排，因为其他的学习计划是不会支付 这些费用的。




M5
那您是否刚好知道，我是不是也有资格获得托儿福利金或家庭补助金，来帮助支付托儿服务及其他开支呢？
So do you happen to know whether or not I am also eligible for the Child Care Benefit and Family Assistance payments to help with child care and other expenses?




E6
Families with low incomes may receive some assistance from the Government to send their child to preschool. However, this is to do with the social welfare policy. I am afraid you will have to go to speak with Centrelink about this issue.
低收入家庭可能由于送孩子到幼儿园学前班，而获得一些政府津贴。但这涉及到社会保障的政策。我恐怕，您必须得去 Centrelink 咨询这方面的事宜。




M6
我还听说，小孩注册儿童保育的时候，都需要出示孩子的疫苗接种记录。我该去哪查询这方面的信息呢？
Also, I have been told I will need to present my children’s immunisation record when enrolling my children in child care. Where can I get more information about this?
