儿子打架

相·遇
30-05-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › CCL
CCL 

        

        



This is a conversation between Mr. Craig, a school teacher and a parent Mr Wan, whose son was involved in a fight.








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Hello Mrs. Wan. Thanks for coming on time today. I am going to talk with you about your son James.
20




M1
你好老师，没事儿，儿子的事儿才是最重要的事儿。他出什么事了？
Hello, Mr. Craig. Not a problem at all. Nothing is more important to me than my son. What happened?




E2
He has always been a good student at school. But this morning, he fought with another student and injured that student’s face. Do you know anything about that?
28




M2
啊？不会吧？这不可能。他一向都非常乖，无论是在家还是在学校。怎么会 跟人打架呢。一定是你搞错了。
What? Really? No Way. He has been so well-behaved all the time, both at school and at home. My son would never get involved in a fight. It must be a mistake.




E3
Unfortunately, I am not joking with you. He did severely hurt the other boy. At first, I couldn’t believe it either, but when I went to the school clinic I found out it was true.
35




M3
啊！那真是对不起。现在 James 在哪里？有没有受伤？那另一个小孩现在怎 样了，对方家长说什么了么？
Really? I’m sorry. Where is James now? Is he injured? How is the other boy? Have his parents said anything?




E4
James is fine and is now talking to our school psychologist. We think he might have some unstable psychological condition. By the way, the other boy’s parents are very angry and they are at the hospital now.
37




M4
这一切真的是太意外了。James 性格内向，一向很听话， 甚至都很少和人争 执。您知道他们到底是因为什么才打架的吗？
This is so shocking! James is an introverted and obedient child. He rarely argues with others. Do you know what on earth caused this fight?




E5
So far, we are not so clear about the reason they fought, but I was told by some students in his class that it’s because of a girl.
28




M5
因为一个女孩？现在的小孩怎么那么早熟啊。而且他才是一个小学生，懂什么叫做爱情啊。我看他是应该去看看心理医生了。我现在都不知道怎么去面 对另外一个孩子的家长了。
Because of a girl? How come today’s children get mature so early? Besides, he is just a primary school student, what does he know about love? I think it is time for him to see a psychologist. I don’t know how to face the other kid’s parents.




E6
I’d suggest you talk to your son and find out what is going on with him. And go to the hospital to apologise to the other boy’s parents. I don’t think they will take you to the court if you sincerely apologise to them.
44




M6
好的。我知道了，我现在就去校医室看看我的儿子。然后我会带他一起去医 院给对方赔礼道歉去。谢谢你了老师，给你们添大麻烦了。
All right, I see. I’ll go to the clinic to see my son right now. Then I will take him to the hospital and make a sincere apology to the other boy’s family. Sorry for the trouble we caused you.
