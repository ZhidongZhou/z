客房失窃

相遇
02-08-2020
Hits
学习笔记 › NATTI › CCL
CCL 

        

        




This is a dialogue between Mr. Dong (D) whose room has just been burgled and a police officer (P).








ID
Original
Translated





E1
Are you Mr. Dong? Did you call the police at 3:15 this afternoon, saying a burglar seemed to have broken into your room?
你是董先生吗？是你今天下午 3 点 15 分打电话报警说似乎有贼进入你的房间行窃吗?




M1
是的，是我。是我打的电话。我们是外国来的游客，我们已经在这个酒店住了一个星期了。
Yes, I am. And it was me who made the phone call. We are tourists from overseas, and we have been staying in this hotel for a week.




E2
Can you tell us about the situation first? I mean, can you tell us in detail what happened.
能先跟我们介绍一下情况吗？我是说，请告诉我们更多的关于此 事的详情。




M2
好，当然。我们从市区观光回来的时候也就才 5 点钟。之后我们就去了朋友的房间，在那儿聊天大概呆了有一个小时的样子。我确定我们锁好了门，但当我们回来的时候，门却是开着的。这非常奇怪。
Yes. Of course. It was just 5 o’clock when we came back from a city tour. Then we went to our friend’s room, and stayed there talking for an hour or so. I am certain we locked the door properly, but when we came back, the door was open, which was very strange.




E3
That is very strange. Did you run into anyone suspicious in the hallway outside your room at that time? And what happened after you came into the room?
这的确很奇怪。你在那时有没有在你房间外面的走廊里遇见什么可 疑的人？那你进屋之后又发生了什么?




M3
我谁也没看见。我发现我的行李箱是开着的，我的相机以及钱包里的 500 元钱都不见了。但我女朋友的情况更糟。我想她的行李箱都被偷走了，而且她放在桌上的旅行支票也都不见了。
I saw no one. I found my suitcase was open, and my camera and $500 in cash inside the wallet were gone. But for my girlfriend, her situation is much worse. I think her suitcase was stolen. And the travellers cheques that she left on the table are gone.




E4
Sorry to hear that but I am afraid that the cash is gone for good. As for the rest of the things, I have to ask: are you both insured?
很遗憾，但是我恐怕你们必须放弃这笔现金了。至于其他的物品，我必须得问，你们俩都上保险了吗？




M4
是的。这是我的海外旅游意外保险卡，这是她的。我想我们必须给中国大使馆打个电话，因为丢了的行李箱里有我女朋友的护照。
Yes. This is my overseas travel insurance card. And that’s hers. I think we have to call the Chinese embassy, because my girlfriend’s passport was in that stolen suitcase.




E5
Yes. I think you should. Did you ask the hotel to send up a security guard to your room after the accident? It could be an inside job considering there is no damage or any scratches on the door itself.
是的，我想你应该打这个电话。事故发生后，你有没有让酒店派一个保安到你屋里来？考虑到门的本身并没有损坏或划痕，有可能是内 部作案。




M5
我们叫了。可是客房部的人说还得通知经理，可是经理那会儿不在。
We did. But a staff from the housekeeping department told us they had to report this accident to their manager, but their manager was not there.




E6
I will make a report for you … Ok, here is the copy. Please attach this report to your claim to the insurance company.
我会给你写份报告的。好了，这是复本。请带着这份报告的复本 到你的保险公司去索赔。你可以用这个复本来申请保险赔偿。




M6
好的。虽然我们是很倒霉，但万幸的是，我们上了保险。
Alright. Even though we’ve had this bad luck, we’re lucky that we are insured.
