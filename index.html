邻里纠纷

CCL Original Translated
A1 Good afternoon Mrs Lai. My name is Time wilson and I’ll be looking after your case. Please take a seat and we can get started. 下午好， 赖太太， 我叫 Tim Wilson, 我将会接手你的案子。 请坐， 我们可以开始了。
Z1 谢谢你来见我， 我从来没有和邻居发生过任何问题， 这真是一个很头疼的问题。 Thank you for seeing me, Not once have I had a problem with my neighbours. This whole situation has been such a headache.
A2 Yes, I read through your file, you said your neighbour, Bob Matt, used a chainsaw (电锯) to cut down the tree on the boundary between your house and his house, but he didn’t get your premission first. Is that correct? 是的， 我已经读过你的档案， 你说你的邻居Bob Matt在没有征得你同意之前用链锯将你和他房子边界上的树砍了， 对吗？
Z2 上周四， 他连一个电话都不(Without even a phone call)打就把树砍倒了。 他也没有清理所有的树枝和树干， 弄的满地都是。 然后他还把下水管道 (Drainage Pipe / Sewerage Pipes ) 给弄坏了 He just cut it down last Thursday without so much as a phone call. And he didn’t clean up all the twigs and branches either - he just left them all over the ground. Then he pulled the roots out as well, causing damage to the sewerage pipes.
A3 I see, so do you have any idea why he will do such thing? 我懂了， 你知道他为什么这样做么？
Z3 他跟我说他想在我们的房子之间修个栅栏。 他是个按摩师， 在家工作。 他说我们的孩子在院子里玩的时候太吵了， 会影响他的生意。 我可以理解这一点， 可是他太过分了。 He told me he wants to build a fence between our properties. He’s masseur (男按摩师) and works from home. He says our children are too noisy when they play in the yard and that it disrupts his business. I understand that, but he went too far.
A4 And tell me what kind of solution 那么告诉我， 你想要怎么处理这件事呢？
Z4 我希望他能付钱请人来修理下水管道， 而且如果他真的很想修围栏的话， 我希望他在修之前跟我商量。 I’d like him to pay for someone to fix the sewerage pipes and, if he really wants to go ahead with the fence, I want him to talk to me first before he puts it up.
A5 Well, let me tell you a bit about mediation process, all right? We can set up a meeting between the two of you, along with a mediator who will help you two explore a variety of opitons to resolve your dispute. 好的， 我先跟你讲一下调解的程序好么？ 我们会安排你们两个会面， 在一个调解员的陪同下帮你们从多种选项中探求解决办法。
Z5 我担心Bob可能不会参加我们的调解会议。 有什么办法可以确保他参加呢？ I’m worried that Bob’s may not attend our mediation session. Is there any way to make sure he comes?
A6 Mediation is voluntary process and no one will be forced to attend I’m afraid. But we will try our best to convince his of why it would he in his best interests to attend. 调解过程是自愿的， 我有人会被强制参加。 但是我将会尽力劝说并告诉他参加调解对他是最好的。
Z6 那就麻烦你一定要说服他去， 我要把帐彻底算清楚。 Please do so, I want to settle this with him once and for all.
  • Intermediator 中间人
  • Chainsaw 电锯
  • Branches 分支/树枝
  • Trunk 树干
  • Sewer Pipe 下水道
  • Massage Therapist 按摩师
  • Masseur 男按摩师
  • Freelance 自由职业者

房东串门

CCL Original Translated
A1 Hello, Mr Zhang. I understand that you are having some problems with your landloard and want some advice from us. Can you tell me more about is? 张先生, 你好！ 我知道你和你的房东有些矛盾， 所以希望征求我们的建议。 你能详细说说么？
Z1 是的。 我不知道到底我自己是对还是错的。 我是新来到这个国家的， 我对这里的法律不大熟悉。 但我的房东经常跑到我的房子来 Sure. I don’t know whether I am right or wrong. I am new to this country and don’t know much about the laws here. But my landlord is visiting me too often.
A2 Has he ever asked for your premission or produced an order from the Consumer, Trader & Tenancy Tribunal? 他没有征得我的同意， 或者从CTTT, 消费者， 商人与租务仲裁庭哪里申请到一份法令呢？
A2 没有， 他从不事先通知我， 就将他的东西及垃圾放在后院。 当我抱怨时， 他就说这是他的房子， 他喜欢怎么做， 就可以怎么做。 No. He always puts his things and garbage in the backyard without letting me know beforehand. When I complained about this to him, he just said he could do whatever he likes because it’s his house.
A3 Well, that’s simply not right. The Residential Tenancy Agreements outline the landlord’s and the tenant’s basic rights. The landlord has the right to enter the premises but only with the tenant’s consent or with an order from the CTTT. 嗯， 这就不对了。 住房租赁合同写明了房东和租客的权利。 房东只有在征得租客同意、 或者申请到了CTTT法令的前提下， 才能进入租客的房间。
Z3 我告诉他， 我认为这样做是错的， 说我们也有权利。 他呢， 当我跟他说时， 却嘲笑我。 你能否告诉我， 作为租户我有什么权利呢？ I told him, I thought he has making that wrong, and we have the right. But he don’t listen and laugh at me. Can you tell me, what legal right I have as a tanent.
A4 As a tenant you are entitled to your privacy as long as you comply with your obligaations under the lease. The landlord can’t just come whenever he pleases. 作为租客只要你遵守租约规定的义务， 你有隐私权。 房东不能想来就来。
Z4 可能我不知道自己应该负些什么责任。 我在签字时并不明白自己签的是什么， 因为我不懂英语。 Perhaps I don’t know what obligations I have. I didn’t understand what it said on the contract when I signed it, because I don’t understand English.
A5 Well, you should have had an interpreter with you. In any case, your obligations are to pay the rent on time, to keep the place clean and in good order, and to tell the landlord of any repairs that are needed. 嗯， 你当时应该找个口译员陪着你。 无论如何， 你的责任是安置交房租； 保持房屋整洁； 如果房屋需要修补， 通知房东。
Z5 是的， 我们做的事一直是正确的。 而且我们还同他房子里需要修理的东西， 但他都一一拒绝， 他还说这些修理要用我们什么的押金去支付 Yes, we always do the right thing and we told him what repairs needed to be done to the house but he refused our requests. He said that the cost of any repairs would come out of our bond or something.
A6 That’s terrible! Your landlord obviously doesn’t know what he’s talking about. You need to make an application to the CTTT if you want to stay there, unless of course, you’d prefer to move out of the place. 这太糟糕了！您的房东显然不知道他在说什么。除非您希望搬出那个地方，不然如果您想继续住在那里，你就需要向CTTT提出申请。
Z6 你这么一说我有底气多了。 我这就去维护我的合法权益！ I am more confident now with all the information you’ve given me. I’m going to fight (Defend; Advocate) for my legal rights.
  • migration review tribunal (移民审仲裁庭)
  • refugee review tribunal (难民上诉仲裁庭)
  • social security tribunal (社会保障仲裁庭)
  • Consumer, Trader & Tenancy Tribunal - CTTT (消费者商人与租务仲裁庭)
