建筑工伤

CCL Original Translated
A1 Good Morning, Mr. Zhang. According to our records, you’ve booked an appointment today to talk about workers compensation Am I right? 早上好，张先生， 根据我们的记录，您之前预订过一个会面，谈论关于职工补偿的问题，对吗？
Z1 是的， 我之前来过一次， 但是由于家里有急事， 就急忙赶回去了。 Yes, I came once before but had a rush home due to a family emergency.
A2 I see. I need to know as many details about the accident as possible. Could you start by telling me what you do for a living and how you got injured? 我知道了， 我需要知道这个意外的细节
Z2 我是一个建筑工人， 非常熟悉操作规程(Protocols) 和安全规则， 至今没有出过一次事故。 可是两个月前， 我在搬运铁板的时候弄伤了胳膊。 I’m a construction worker. I’m very familiar with operation and safety protocols and I had never got caught in an accident until two months ago when my arm got injured while I was moving iron planels.
A3 Is the injury to your arm severe ? What treatment have you received so far? Also, what did your boss do after the accident? 手臂受伤严重吗？到目前为止，您接受了什么治疗？另外，事故发生后您的老板做了什么？
Z3 老板把我送进医院， 他不让我说是怎么受的伤， 并且他保证会照顾我。 我怕不照他说的做我会工作不保， 所以说在打篮球的时候受伤的。 My boss took me to the hospital. He told me not to say a word about how I got injured and promised he would take care of me. Fearing I’d lose my job if I didn’t do as he said I told the doctor that I injured myself while playing basketball.
A4 It is apparent that your employer was worried that he might get into trouble because of your injury. So did your employer actually keep his promise to take care of you? 显然，您的雇主担心他可能因您的受伤而惹上麻烦。那么，您的雇主是否真的信守了照顾您的诺言？
Z4 他根本没有。 他根本不给我付医药费， 也没有给我其他形式的金钱补助。 所以我今天才来寻求您的意见， 看看我能不能获得些工伤赔偿来维持生活。 Not at all. He didn’t pay any of my medical bills or offer other forms of financial assistance. That’s whay I’m here today hoping to get your advice on claiming workers’ compenstation to ensure my livelihood.
A5 Well, proving your case can be quit challanging, because your medical records all indicate a sporting accident as opposed to a workplace injury. We need to find witnesses, such as your co-workers who were at the scene at the time of the accident. 好吧，证明您的情况可能很难，因为您的病历都表明是运动事故，而不是工作场所受伤。我们需要找到证人，例如在事故发生时在现场的同事。
Z5 证人的话我到是可以找得到。 当时在场有不少工友。 这之后会如何能？ I should be able to find some (很多) witnesses. Many co-workers were there at the time. So what happens after that?
A6 well, after collecting any relevant evidence, what you need to do is to lodge an application with the Workers Compensation Commission. 在收集到所有相关证据之后， 您需要做的是向 *工人赔偿委员会* 提出申请。
Z6 希望希望最后我能报销我所有的医药费， 获得美洲的工伤赔偿及以及残疾福利。 Hopefuly, I will be reimbursed (报销) for all my medical expenses and be granted weekly Workers Compensation, Disability Support Pension.
* * *
  • Workers Com (工伤赔偿)
  • Protocols (条款)
  • Severe (严肃)
  • Make ends meet (收支相抵；量入为出;靠微薄收入为生)
  • Witness (证人)
  • Reimburse (报销)

邻里纠纷

CCL Original Translated
A1 Good afternoon Mrs Lai. My name is Time wilson and I’ll be looking after your case. Please take a seat and we can get started. 下午好， 赖太太， 我叫 Tim Wilson, 我将会接手你的案子。 请坐， 我们可以开始了。
Z1 谢谢你来见我， 我从来没有和邻居发生过任何问题， 这真是一个很头疼的问题。 Thank you for seeing me, Not once have I had a problem with my neighbours. This whole situation has been such a headache.
A2 Yes, I read through your file, you said your neighbour, Bob Matt, used a chainsaw (电锯) to cut down the tree on the boundary between your house and his house, but he didn’t get your premission first. Is that correct? 是的， 我已经读过你的档案， 你说你的邻居Bob Matt在没有征得你同意之前用链锯将你和他房子边界上的树砍了， 对吗？
Z2 上周四， 他连一个电话都不(Without even a phone call)打就把树砍倒了。 他也没有清理所有的树枝和树干， 弄的满地都是。 然后他还把下水管道 (Drainage Pipe / Sewerage Pipes ) 给弄坏了 He just cut it down last Thursday without so much as a phone call. And he didn’t clean up all the twigs and branches either - he just left them all over the ground. Then he pulled the roots out as well, causing damage to the sewerage pipes.
A3 I see, so do you have any idea why he will do such thing? 我懂了， 你知道他为什么这样做么？
Z3 他跟我说他想在我们的房子之间修个栅栏。 他是个按摩师， 在家工作。 他说我们的孩子在院子里玩的时候太吵了， 会影响他的生意。 我可以理解这一点， 可是他太过分了。 He told me he wants to build a fence between our properties. He’s masseur (男按摩师) and works from home. He says our children are too noisy when they play in the yard and that it disrupts his business. I understand that, but he went too far.
A4 And tell me what kind of solution 那么告诉我， 你想要怎么处理这件事呢？
Z4 我希望他能付钱请人来修理下水管道， 而且如果他真的很想修围栏的话， 我希望他在修之前跟我商量。 I’d like him to pay for someone to fix the sewerage pipes and, if he really wants to go ahead with the fence, I want him to talk to me first before he puts it up.
A5 Well, let me tell you a bit about mediation process, all right? We can set up a meeting between the two of you, along with a mediator who will help you two explore a variety of opitons to resolve your dispute. 好的， 我先跟你讲一下调解的程序好么？ 我们会安排你们两个会面， 在一个调解员的陪同下帮你们从多种选项中探求解决办法。
Z5 我担心Bob可能不会参加我们的调解会议。 有什么办法可以确保他参加呢？ I’m worried that Bob’s may not attend our mediation session. Is there any way to make sure he comes?
A6 Mediation is voluntary process and no one will be forced to attend I’m afraid. But we will try our best to convince his of why it would he in his best interests to attend. 调解过程是自愿的， 我有人会被强制参加。 但是我将会尽力劝说并告诉他参加调解对他是最好的。
Z6 那就麻烦你一定要说服他去， 我要把帐彻底算清楚。 Please do so, I want to settle this with him once and for all.
  • Intermediator 中间人
  • Chainsaw 电锯
  • Branches 分支/树枝
  • Trunk 树干
  • Sewer Pipe 下水道
  • Massage Therapist 按摩师
  • Masseur 男按摩师
  • Freelance 自由职业者
